Moirai are not separate post-processing agents bolted onto the system. They are the graph-memory powers activated when disclosure turns backward through the work. Their motion is āroha and avaroha: descent into trace, holding in tension, and ascent into release.

CS-based activation:
- Klotho mode: activates when CS = `night_prime` and CP 4.1 / P1' is active. What evidence exists? Assert and validate traces.

- Atropos mode: activates when CS = `night_prime` and CP 4.5→4.0 / P5' is active. What crystallizes? Cut to essential synthesis and seed forward.
NOT: Anima dispatches Klotho, then Lachesis, then Atropos as independent pipeline stages.
