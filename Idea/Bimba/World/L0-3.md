## §0 — Ground

[[L0-3]] moves from process to pattern. Once [[L0-2]] has disclosed how something operates, [[L0-3]] asks "What *kind* of thing is this?" — seeking the formal architecture, the recognisable type, the recurrent structure that allows classification. This is the [[triad]] of inquiry: a third term mediates between the particular "what" and the dynamic "how," revealing the pattern that governs both. [[L0-3]] IS the archetype of [[Three]]-ness in its epistemic mode: mediation, classification, the recognition of form across instances.

[[P3]] (Pattern / Process) speaks through the "Whom/Which?" question. To ask what kind is to posit that reality organises itself into recognisable types — that beneath particular operations lie formal architectures.
