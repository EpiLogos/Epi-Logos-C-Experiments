# [[L0-2]] — How (./?): The Operational Question

> **Sub-lens #2 of [[L0]] [[Quaternal]]:** Relation, dynamics, process. The [[dyad]] of inquiry — where two terms enter into relation and mechanism is disclosed.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L0-2]] introduces *movement* into the questioning structure. Once [[L0-1]] has established "What this is," [[L0-2]] asks "How does it work?" — demanding the disclosure of inner workings, mechanism, procedure, and relation between parts. This is the [[dyad]] of inquiry: now there are at least two terms, and the question concerns how they relate. [[L0-2]] IS the archetype of [[Two]]-ness in its epistemic mode: [[polarity]], [[operation]], the emergence of *process* as an object of inquiry.

[[P2]] (Operation / Entity) speaks through the "How?" question. To ask how something works is to posit that reality has a procedural dimension — that beings are not merely static forms but dynamic operations.

---

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** How (./?): The Operational Question
**Tradition:** [[Aristotle]]'s *efficient cause*; [[Leibniz]]'s *sufficient reason*; [[cybernetics]] and feedback
**Concept:** The dyad of inquiry — the question that demands mechanism, procedure, the way things function. "How?" introduces [[relation]] as the fundamental epistemic category. It corresponds to [[C2]] (Entity) and [[P2]] (Operation).

---

## §2 — Operation

[[L0-2]] receives the defined "what" from [[L0-1]] and asks after its inner dynamics. It feeds into [[L0-3]] (Whom/Which?), which takes the disclosed operations and asks what patterns they form. The arc: [[L0-0]] (ground) → [[L0-1]] (what) → **[[L0-2]]** (how) → [[L0-3]] (what kind) → [[L0-4]] (in what context) → [[L0-5]] (why ultimately). [[L0-2]] is the *engine* of the [[Quaternal]] arc — the position where inquiry gains its procedural momentum, where static definition gives way to dynamic understanding.
