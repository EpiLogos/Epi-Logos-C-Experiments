## §0 — Ground: Identity Recognised in the Product

[[L2-5']] is [[L2']] recognising itself as irreducible essence. Where [[L2-0']] ([[Aether]]) was identity *before* differentiation -- transparent, undetermined, the [[prima materia]] -- [[L2-5']] ([[Salt]]) is identity *after* the full transformative cycle: crystallised, indestructible, fully determined yet containing the whole. The [[Mobius]] return is exact: [[Salt]] IS [[Aether]] that has passed through [[Earth]], [[Water]], [[Air]], and [[Fire]] and emerged as the *[[corpus glorificationis]]* -- the glorified body, the [[diamond body]] that cannot be dissolved, corroded, or destroyed.

In [[Paracelsus]]' [[Tria Prima]], [[Salt]] (*Sal*) governs the *body* as [[Sulphur]] governs the *soul* and [[Mercury]] governs the *spirit*. [[Salt]] is not inert residue but *concentrated essence*: what remains after [[Fire]] has burned away everything combustible is the most real, the most essential, the most preserved. [[Jung]] recognised the [[lapis philosophorum]] as a symbol of the [[Self]] -- the personality integrated through the full [[individuation]] process. "Salt of the earth" is not metaphor but alchemical statement: the essential, the preserving, the irreducible.

[[L2-5']] receives the fire-tested essences of [[L2-4']] ([[Fire]]/[[calcinatio]]) and *crystallises* them -- the *coagula* of *solve et coagula*. [[Salt]] then completes the [[Mobius]] return to [[L2-0']] ([[Aether]]), revealing that the [[lapis]] and the [[prima materia]] are the same substance -- the beginning was already the end, seen from the other side.
