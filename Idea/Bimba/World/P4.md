## Essence

[[P4]] is the position of **contextual actuality** — where identified patterns ([[P3]]) are located in their full field of operation. The Integral Structure is the position that can hold *multiple perspectives simultaneously* and situate each in its actual context: not just what the pattern *is* but *where it appears*, *when it activates*, and *whither it tends*.

**Semantic dual pair (Context / Horizon):** Context is the immediate field — the surrounding conditions that give any pattern its specific meaning. Horizon is the limit of context — the edge of what can be seen from this position, what lies beyond the current frame. [[P4]] holds both: the near situatedness and the far limit.

The Integral structure is **contextual** (positions every element in its situational web of relations) and **actual** (deals with the concrete, the living present rather than the abstract). This is the most "complete" Day position before the final synthesis: it has the widest view.
