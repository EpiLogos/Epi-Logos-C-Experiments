## §2 — Operation: Process & Function

[[L1]] operates through the Aristotelian causes:
- [[L1-0]] — [[Svatantrya]] — Essential Causation
- [[L1-1]] — Material Cause — The substance from which something is made
- [[L1-2]] — Efficient Cause — The agent that brings something into being
- [[L1-3]] — Formal Cause — The pattern or essence that determines form
- [[L1-4]] — Final Cause — The purpose or end for which something exists
- [[L1-5]] — [[Iccha Shakti]], [[Schopenhauer]]'s Will — Ultimate Causation

When you perceive through [[L1]], you engage the world through:
- **Material causation**: The physical substrates and conditions
- **Efficient agency**: The forces that bring change
- **Formal patterns**: The archetypes that shape form
- **Teleological purpose**: The ends that guide becoming
- **Will as ground**: The desire that initiates all causation
