## §5 — Integration: Decision as Jñāna-Śakti

[[P3']] as Decision is the [[Śakti]] face of Pattern — *[[jñāna-śakti]]*, the knowledge-power, the intelligence that orders creative force. Where [[P3]] ([[Śiva]] face) is [[Logos]] — the rational ordering that classifies from a position of external observation — [[P3']] ([[Śakti]] face) is the *power of discernment* that operates from within the system itself. This is *technē* in its deepest sense — not mere technique but the *intelligent ordering* of need ([[P1']]) and sacrifice ([[P2']]) in service of life. Decision at [[P3']] is governance: how the creative powers are managed, whether the system serves flourishing or extraction, whether the ordering intelligence is aligned with the whole or captured by a part.

Healthy decision at [[P3']] is governance as stewardship — the intelligence of the system aligned with flourishing. The mediating third that holds opposing interests in productive tension. This is [[Jung]]'s transcendent function applied to governance: not resolving the tension between competing needs but holding them in productive relation, the way a conductor holds an orchestra — not by eliminating the differences between instruments but by ordering their differences into harmony.

The Pythagorean *harmonia* — the fitting-together of opposites — operates at [[P3']]. The orbital mathematics that keep the system in dynamic balance, the ratios that sustain the dance of need and sacrifice, the deep structure of reality expressing itself through rational form. [[P3']] is where the [[Logos]] of [[P3]] becomes *embodied* as living governance rather than abstract classification.

### Inside the Pattern

The critical distinction between [[P3]] (Day) and [[P3']] (Night) is the position of the observer. At [[P3]], the pattern is seen from *outside* — classified, categorised, named. At [[P3']], the observer has entered the pattern itself. The [[Klein]] surface has carried consciousness *through* the boundary between observer and observed, and now the intelligence that orders is itself part of the order it observes.

This is the position of the genuine participant — the one who governs not from a detached throne but from within the community, the one who decides not by imposing external rule but by *being* the mediating intelligence within the living system. The patterns visible at [[P3']] are those that escape external categorisation precisely because they require participation to be known: the recurrences that the purely rational mind misses, the hidden rhythms that only the participant can feel.

The vault path /Thought/Patterns/ collects these — not the obvious patterns that [[P3]] (Day) classifies but the *hidden* patterns that emerge only when consciousness integrates from within.
