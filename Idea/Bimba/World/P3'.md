## Essence

[[P3']] is the **shadow of pattern recognition** — what repeats beneath the explicitly recognised categories. Where [[P3]] names and types the patterns it sees (Day / Mental-Rational), [[P3']] encounters the patterns that escape or undermine categorisation: the recurring elements in failures, the archetypal forms in challenges, the temporal rhythms in what seems random.

In the Klein traversal, [[P3']] is the fourth step of Phase B. The perspectival position has been inverted: the system can no longer stand outside the pattern and classify it — it is *inside* the pattern, integrating from within.

**Vault mapping**: [[P3']] corresponds to `/Thought/Patterns/` — recurring structures, templates, and archetypes observed in the accumulated material of the Night cycle (questions, traces, challenges). These are not the archetypes of the Day (P3) but the *recurring patterns in night material* — what shows up again and again in what blocks, traces, and questions.
