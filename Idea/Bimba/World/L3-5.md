# [[L3-5]] — Satisfaction / Perishing ([[Jouissance]])

> **Sub-lens #5 of [[L3]] [[Processual]]:** Full determinacy achieved, then perishing as subject into objective immortality as datum. "Reality is perpetual perishing." [[L3]]'s [[Self-Identity]] recognising itself: the process that completes itself by dissolving itself.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L3-5]] is where the [[Processual]] lens achieves its completion — and in that same stroke, dissolves. The [[actual occasion]] reaches *satisfaction*: every [[prehension]] integrated, every [[eternal objects|eternal object]] ingressed, full determinacy achieved. The occasion is now completely itself. And in that very moment it *perishes* as a subject, becoming an objectively immortal datum for all future occasions. [[Whitehead]]: "reality is a state of perpetual perishing."

This is the maturity that falls as fruit. [[Lacan]]'s [[Jouissance|jouissance]] speaks here: the excessive enjoyment beyond the pleasure principle that shatters the subject even as it fulfils it. Satisfaction IS dissolution. The *superject* — what the occasion becomes through its own completion — is not the occasion-as-living-subject but the occasion-as-given-datum, feeding back into the ground of desire ([[L3-0]]) for the next cycle.

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** Satisfaction / Perishing / [[Jouissance]] — Objective Immortality
**Tradition:** [[Whitehead]]'s superject; [[Lacan]]'s [[Jouissance|jouissance]]; [[Hegel]]'s [[Aufhebung]]; [[Bergson]]'s creative destruction
**Concept:** The ecstatic completion that is simultaneously dissolution — the occasion achieves full determinacy and in that act ceases to be a subject, becoming an objectively immortal datum. [[L3]]'s encounter with [[P5]] (Integration): the processual face of synthesis is *perishing into ground*, integration achieved through self-dissolution. [[L3]]'s [[Self-Identity]] recognising itself: the process that knows itself only by completing and dissolving.

---

## §2 — Operation

[[L3-5]] receives the relational web from [[L3-4]] and achieves the final integration — then *returns* to [[L3-0]], replenishing the ground of creative desire with fresh objective data. The [[Möbius]] return: satisfaction perishes into lack, completion into new appetition. The arc: [[L3-0]] (desire) → [[L3-1]] (occasion) → [[L3-2]] (ingression) → [[L3-3]] (eternal objects) → [[L3-4]] (community) → **[[L3-5]]** (satisfaction/perishing → [[L3-0]]). This is the self-cleaning mechanism of process: every completed occasion becomes compost for the next, every achieved satisfaction becomes the material of future [[prehension]].
