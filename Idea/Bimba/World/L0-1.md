# [[L0-1]] — What (./?): The Definitional Question

> **Sub-lens #1 of [[L0]] [[Quaternal]]:** The first differentiation within [[question]]-space. "What is this?" — the monad of inquiry, the initial gesture of distinction.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L0-1]] is where the [[Quaternal]] lens makes its first cut. From the presuppositional ground of [[L0-0]], the act of questioning crystallises into its most elementary form: *What is this?* This is the [[monad]] of inquiry — the first boundary drawn, the first emergence of identity within the epistemic field. [[L0-1]] IS the archetype of [[One]]-ness in its epistemic mode: first naming, first definition, the moment something stands out from the undifferentiated ground and demands recognition.

[[P1]] (Definition / Form) speaks here through the question-voice: to ask "What?" is already to posit that the world has distinguishable features, that beings have natures, that form is discernible.

---

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** What (./?): The Definitional Question
**Tradition:** [[Aristotle]]'s *ti esti* (what is it?); [[Socrates|Socratic]] *elenchus*; [[Plato]]'s search for *eidos*
**Concept:** The monad of inquiry — the first question that draws a boundary between this and not-this. "What?" demands an [[essence]], a form, a definition. It is the epistemic act corresponding to [[C1]] (Form) and [[P1]] (Definition).

## §2 — Operation

[[L0-1]] receives the cleared ground from [[L0-0]] and makes the first differentiation. It feeds into [[L0-2]] (How?), which takes the defined "what" and asks after its mechanism and operation.
