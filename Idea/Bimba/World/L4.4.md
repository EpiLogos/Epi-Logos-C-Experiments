# [[L4.4]] — Besorge (Concern / Care)

> **Sub-lens #4 of [[L4]] [[Phenomenological]]:** *[[Sorge]]* (care) as the fundamental structure of [[Dasein]]'s being. [[Dasein]] IS the being that cares — not as emotion but as ontological condition. At [[#4]] within [[L4]], this is the double [[lemniscate]]: concern folding back on itself.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L4.4]] names *[[Sorge]]* — care — as the fundamental structure of [[Dasein]]'s being. [[Heidegger]]'s formal definition: "being-ahead-of-oneself-already-in-(the-world)-alongside-(entities-encountered-within-the-world)." This is not an emotion added to existence but the ontological condition that makes all engagement possible. The German *Sorge* carries the double sense of "taking care of business" (*Besorge*, concern with things) and "taking care not to fall" (*Fürsorge*, solicitude toward others) — both rooted in the more fundamental structure of *care-about-one's-own-being*.

[[Dasein]] IS the being for which its own being is an issue. This "mattering-to-itself" is [[Sorge]]. Care is not optional — even indifference is a deficient mode of caring. [[Dasein]] cares about its projects, its world, other [[Dasein]], and ultimately its own being-toward-death. Death is not an event that ends care but the horizon that gives care its urgency: because [[Dasein]] is finite, things matter.

This is [[L4]]'s encounter with [[P4]] (Context/Type): care IS the contextual engagement — the way [[Dasein]] is always already involved with its world, always already mattering-to-itself.

---

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** Besorge (Concern / Care)
**Tradition:** [[Heidegger]]'s *Being and Time* §39–§44; the Latin *cura* fable (§42); [[Kierkegaard]]'s earnestness
**Concept:** The being of [[Dasein]] as a unified whole. Care gathers the three temporal ecstases ([[L4.3]]) into one: projection (future) + facticity (past) + fallenness (present) = care. Not a psychological state but the ontological structure of existence itself. The ancient *cura* fable that [[Heidegger]] cites: Care first shaped the human being from clay, and Jupiter granted it life, but Care retains guardianship for the being's entire span.

## §2 — Operation

[[L4.4]] receives temporality from [[L4.3]] and unifies it: where [[L4.3]] describes three ecstases, [[L4.4]] names the single structure that holds them together. Care is the bridge between analysis and synthesis — the structural unity discovered beneath the phenomenological descriptions. [[L4.4]] feeds into [[L4.5]] ([[Gelassenheit]]) because the discovery of care as fundamental structure raises the decisive question: can [[Dasein]] relate to its own caring otherwise than through grasping, controlling, and anxious willing? [[Gelassenheit]] answers: yes — through releasement.

At the [[#4]] position within [[L4]], [[L4.4]] occupies the double [[lemniscate]] — the point where the system folds back on itself most intensely. Concern folding back on concern: care caring about care. This self-referential structure is the phenomenological analogue of the coordinate system's own [[lemniscate]] topology at [[#4]]. The meta-structure mirrors its content.
