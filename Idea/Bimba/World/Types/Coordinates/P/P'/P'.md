# P' — Night Arc

[[P']] is the Night-arc MOC of the [[P]] family. The six Night positions P0'–P5' are not derivatives of P0–P5; they are co-equal refractions of the same [[Psychoid-0|#0]]–[[Psychoid-5|#5]] charges through the *Night-face* of the matheme. The `'` mark denotes inversion as structural position, not as derivation.

Where Day P-positions face the explicate, public, manifest dimension of each psychoid charge, Night P'-positions face the implicate, esoteric, withdrawn dimension. The Day arc *says*; the Night arc *holds the conditions of saying*. Both are required for the full QL surface.

## Möbius Crossing

Night P_n' is bound to Day P_(5-n) across the Klein twist. P0' (Play, autotelic-freedom) is the Möbius partner of P5 (Image, manifest synthesis) — Work makes Play public, Play is the invisible ground of Work. This cross-helix grammar is what gives the matheme its non-orientable closure.
