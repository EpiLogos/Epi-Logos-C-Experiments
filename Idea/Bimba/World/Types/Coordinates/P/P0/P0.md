# [[P0]] — Truth

> Day-face of the [[Psychoid-0|#0]] charge. *Why?* The irrepresentable ground, the *bindu*, standing-illumination prior to any difference.

## Day Meaning

[[P0]] is the irrepresentable ground — Heidegger's *alētheia* before concealment-and-overcoming, the zero-dimensional total non-differentiation of the *bindu*. It is the attaching-point of the matheme, the basepoint of the fundamental group, pre-perspectival and pre-spatial. Its question is *Why?* — not in the sense of seeking a cause, but in the sense of the silent presence-of-ground from which any question can be asked at all. Truth here is not propositional; it is the unconcealment that lets propositions be true or false.
