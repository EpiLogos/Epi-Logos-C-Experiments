# P — Position Family

[[P]] is the QL positional articulation of the [[Psychoid-0|#0]]–[[Psychoid-5|#5]] wells. Where the psychoid charges are raw number-archetypes, [[P]] gives their *functional-semantic* refraction — the six places where a stance, a question, a definition, a process, a context, or a synthesis can stand. Each P-position is the **Day-face**; each P'-position is the **Night-face** of the same psychoid charge. They are not derivative of one another. The mark `'` does not mean *derived from* — it marks the inversion as an independent structural position.

The Day arc runs P0 (Truth) → P1 (Mind) → P2 (Word) → P3 (Logos) → P4 (Son) → P5 (Image). The Night arc runs P0' (Play) → P1' (Need) → P2' (Sacrifice) → P3' (Decision) → P4' (Love) → P5' (Work). Both arcs are co-equal refractions of the same six psychoid charges; one is not the shadow of the other.

## Möbius-Pair Invariant

Day P_n ↔ Night P_(5-n)' across the Klein twist. The complementary QL pairs sum to 5: (P0, P5), (P1, P4), (P2, P3) on the Day side, and the matching cross-helix pairings (P0, P5'), (P1, P4'), (P2, P3') under the Möbius return. The Night arc reverses semantic direction; the Klein twist binds the two arcs into a single non-orientable surface.
