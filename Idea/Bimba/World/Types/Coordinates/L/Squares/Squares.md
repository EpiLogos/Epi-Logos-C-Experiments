# Squares — Three Klein V₄ Lens-Squares

The L-hexad organises into **three Klein V₄ Squares**, each holding two Day lenses and their two Night Möbius partners, around a complementary P-pair whose indices sum to 5. The Squares are the lens-level structural backbone of the Psychoid Web.

## The Three Squares

- [[Square A]] — **P0+P5 Speech-Number axis.** Lenses L0 · L5 · L5' · L0'. The outer ring binding the question (L0) to its manifest articulation (L5).
- [[Square B]] — **P1+P4 Cause-Experience axis.** Lenses L1 · L4 · L4' · L1'. The **primary runtime entry** to the hexad — L4/L4' form the encounter axis (lived↔observed) and L1/L1' the causal/typological axis.
- [[Square C]] — **P2+P3 Logic-Process axis.** Lenses L2 · L3 · L3' · L2'. The inner ring; L2 carries the tetralemma; L2' is the element-bearing lens.

## Complementary QL Pair Rule

Each Square is built around a P-pair whose positions sum to 5: P0+P5, P1+P4, P2+P3. This sums to the **C-family converse-mirror rule** (see [[Family C — Converse-Mirror]]) and instantiates the Klein V₄ structure at the lens level.
