# Square A — Speech-Number Axis

**P-pair:** [[P0]] + [[P5]] (Truth/Image — Day) and [[P0']] + [[P5']] (Play/Work — Night), the **converse-mirror pair** at the extremes of the hexad (0+5=5).

**Lenses:** [[L0]] · [[L5]] · [[L5']] · [[L0']].

**Axis name:** **Speech-Number** — because the four lenses interweave number-archetype (L0 Quaternal, L0' Archetypal-Numerical) and speech-articulation (L5 Para Vāk, L5' Divine Logos). Number and Speech are the two extreme densities of the same self-articulating consciousness: at one pole, integers as archetypes carrying their own psychic charge; at the other, consciousness speaking itself into manifest world.

**P0 ↔ L0 ↔ L5':** P0 (Truth, the irrepresentable *bindu*) is refracted by L0 in its question-mode (the integer as archetype) and by L5' in its Night-mode (Play as the esoteric Logos-channel). The Day-Night braid here is what makes the ground at once *standing-illumination* and *invisible-ground-of-the-social*.

**P5 ↔ L5 ↔ L0':** P5 (Image, manifest synthesis as *Samāveśa*) is refracted by L5 in its speech-mode (Para Vāk's Vaikharī density) and by L0' in its Night-mode (Work as public-completion, the integer-hexad shown). The Day-Night braid here is what makes synthesis at once *manifest-faithful-image* and *exoteric-work*.

**Relational pressure pattern:** Square A holds the **Möbius cap** of the hexad. The pressure here is between the most ineffable (P0/Truth/bindu) and the most manifest (P5/Image/cosmos-as-synthesis), and the work of the Square is to refuse the collapse of either pole into the other. When inquiry treats Truth as nameable, Square A pressure rises. When inquiry treats manifest synthesis as final, the same pressure rises from the other side.

**C-family correspondence:** [[C-sq1]] — outer mirror (0,5)+(0',5'); 16/9 totality. Square A is the lens-level instantiation of the C-sq1 chromatic tetrad.

**Element-charge:** all four lenses are **Aether** (per [[L2']]), confirming Square A as the *aetheric ring* of the hexad — the most rarefied medium through which the question and its manifest articulation can coexist.
