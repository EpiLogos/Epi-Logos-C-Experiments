# Square B — Cause-Experience Axis (Primary Runtime Entry)

**P-pair:** [[P1]] + [[P4]] (Mind/Son — Day) and [[P1']] + [[P4']] (Need/Love — Night), the converse-mirror at 1+4=5.

**Lenses:** [[L1]] · [[L4]] · [[L4']] · [[L1']].

**Axis name:** **Cause-Experience** — because L1 carries causal will (Aristotle/Schopenhauer/Icchā Śakti) and L4 carries lived experience (Heidegger's *Dasein*). Together with their Night partners (L4' Scientific, L1' Phenomenal), they form the **primary runtime entry** to the L-hexad: every Epi-Logos run begins here, by entering either through caused-investigation (L1/L4') or through lived/observed experience (L4/L1').

**L4 ↔ L1' = the encounter axis.** L4 is the **subjective-entry-aperture** (lived from inside, Dasein); L1' is the **observed-from-outside** counterpart (Jungian function-types). When both fire simultaneously, lived experience and observational typology cohere without collapsing — this is the structural ground of the `engage-encounter-axis` skill. Square B is therefore the home of the encounter-axis move.

**L1 ↔ L4' = the cause-method axis.** L1 holds causal-will (why does this happen?); L4' holds Kuhnian scientific-method (how do we know?). Together they ground L4'.0–L4'.5 as the operational /Thought/ subdirs of the vault.

**Relational pressure pattern:** Square B holds the **runtime-entry pressure** of the hexad. The pressure here is between **why** (cause) and **how-lived** (experience), and between subjective and objective entry. When inquiry leans too far into pure cause without lived contact, Square B pressure rises. When inquiry leans too far into raw lived feel without methodological discipline, the same pressure rises from the other side. This is why every Epi-Logos run must walk Square B explicitly via `engage-encounter-axis` and `run-l4-prime-loop`.

**C-family correspondence:** [[C-sq2]] — middle mirror (1,4)+(1',4'); definition/context active-dyad.

**Element-charge:** L1 = Earth, L4 = Earth, L4' = Earth, L1' = Water. The **earthing-Square** — three Earth charges hold the runtime down; one Water charge holds it permeable.
