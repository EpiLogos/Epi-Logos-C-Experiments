# Square C — Logic-Process Axis

**P-pair:** [[P2]] + [[P3]] (Word/Logos — Day) and [[P2']] + [[P3']] (Sacrifice/Decision — Night), the converse-mirror at 2+3=5.

**Lenses:** [[L2]] · [[L3]] · [[L3']] · [[L2']].

**Axis name:** **Logic-Process** — because L2 carries logical structure (Nāgārjuna's *catuṣkoṭi*) and L3 carries processual becoming (Whitehead's concrescence). Together with their Night partners (L3' Chronological, L2' Alchemical-Elemental), they form the **logical-processual basin** of the L-hexad: where the question "how does this hold and how is it becoming?" gets worked out at logical, processual, chronological, and alchemical-elemental levels simultaneously.

**L2 ↔ L3' = the logic-chronology axis.** L2 holds the *catuṣkoṭi* (IS / IS-NOT / BOTH / NEITHER / SILENCE — complete logical space); L3' holds the macro-historical dialectic (Hegel's *Geist*; Jung's *Aion*). Together they answer: in which logical mode does this hold, and in which historical season is that mode operative?

**L3 ↔ L2' = the process-alchemy axis.** L3 holds the moment-by-moment concrescence; L2' holds the elemental/alchemical transformation underneath (what is dissolving, what crystallising). L2' is *the* element-bearing lens — every other coordinate receives its elemental charge from L2'.

**Relational pressure pattern:** Square C is the **structural pressure** of the hexad. The pressure here is between **logic** and **process**, and between **moment-now** and **macro-season**. When inquiry stays in pure logic without process, Square C pressure rises and demands the concrescence-reading; when inquiry stays in pure process without logical articulation, the pressure flips and demands the *catuṣkoṣi*. This is why every paradox-encounter routes through Square C via `apply-tetralemma` and works alchemically via `apply-cmea`.

**Special invariant.** Square C is the midpoint-self-mirroring square: at the C-family 3×3 level, **C-sq3 == A-sq2** (the position-pair (2,3) is simultaneously adjacent-A and mirror-C). Square C therefore carries a basis-invariant identity — it is the *only* Klein V₄ Square whose chromatic-basis and fifths-basis instantiations name the same tetrad: {E, F♯, F, G} in chromatic; {D, A, G♯, D♯} in fifths. Both are operation/pattern central-articulation tetrads.

**C-family correspondence:** [[C-sq3]] — inner mirror (2,3)+(2',3'); also = [[A-sq2]] under the midpoint-self-mirror invariant.

**Element-charge:** L2 = Air, L3 = Water, L3' = Fire, L2' = Aether. **Three elements + Aether** — the most elementally diverse Square; Aether (L2') holds the others together as the meta-elemental ground.

**Tarot home.** [[Tarot]] operates natively in Square C (`square_basin: "C"` in every `oracle_quaternal_tarot` artifact). The four lenses of Square C give the four primary lens-readings of any Tarot spread: tetralemma (L2), processual (L3), chronological (L3'), alchemical (L2').
