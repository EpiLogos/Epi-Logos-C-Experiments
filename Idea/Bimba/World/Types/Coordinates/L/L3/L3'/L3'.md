# L3' — Chronological Lens

**Anchor:** Hegel / Jung's *Aion*.

**Essence:** Macro-temporal dialectic of *Geist* through history — thesis / antithesis / synthesis; seasonal and aeonic mapping.

**Square membership:** [[Square C]] (P2+P3 Logic-Process axis).

**Refracts:** [[P2']] (Sacrifice / cost-of-action / operational-shadow).

**Element (per [[L2']]):** Fire.

L3' is the Night counterpart of [[L3]]. Where L3 reads process as the *micro* flow of actual occasions (Whiteheadian instants), L3' reads process as the *macro* unfolding of historical/aeonic time. Hegel's dialectic and Jung's *Aion* (the great-year of psychic constellations) both treat history as patterned movement of consciousness, with each age sacrificing its dominant form to the next.

This is why L3' refracts P2' (sacrifice): every chronological transition costs something at the operational level.
