# L4 — Phenomenological Lens

**Anchor:** Heidegger *Dasein*.

**Essence:** Structures of lived experience as the pathway to Being itself; the **primary *subjective-entry-aperture*** to the L-hexad.

**Square membership:** [[Square B]] (P1+P4 Cause-Experience axis — primary runtime entry).

**Refracts:** [[P4]] (Son / incarnation / aperspectival / lemniscate-anchor).

**Element (per [[L2']]):** Earth.

L4 is the **Where/When?** lens become **phenomenological**. Heidegger's analytic of Dasein refuses to begin with an abstract subject: it begins with *being-in-the-world*, with care (*Sorge*), with thrownness, with the always-already-temporal structure of lived experience. L4 is where the system's user/agent enters as a *lived position*, not as a category.

L4 forms the **encounter axis** with its Möbius partner [[L1']]: lived from inside (L4) versus observed from outside (L1'). Together they hold the subjective↔objective tension that any Epi-Logos run must walk.
