# L2 — Logical Lens

**Anchor:** Nāgārjuna's *catuṣkoṭi* (tetralemma).

**Essence:** Complete logical space — IS / IS-NOT / BOTH / NEITHER / SILENCE — four positions plus the fifth of meaningful silence.

**Square membership:** [[Square C]] (P2+P3 Logic-Process axis).

**Refracts:** [[P2]] (Word / active process / energic operation).

**Element (per [[L2']]):** Air.

L2 is the **How?** lens become **how-logical**. Where classical bivalent logic permits two values, the *catuṣkoṭi* opens four (with the fifth of silence as meta-position), and exactly matches the polar-narrative-verbal field of [[Psychoid-2|#2]]: a world of forces in tension can be IS, IS-NOT, BOTH, and NEITHER without contradiction collapsing the space.

L2 is the home of the tetralemma move in Epi-Logos thinking. When a situation generates the same impasse from binary framing, L2 is the lens that opens it.
