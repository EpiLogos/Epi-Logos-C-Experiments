# L1' — Phenomenal Lens

**Anchor:** Jung's psychic functions (sensation / feeling / thinking / intuition × introversion / extroversion).

**Essence:** Subjective-immediacy of experience — the typology of *how* a psyche meets the world; the **observed-from-outside** counterpart to [[L4]]'s lived-from-inside.

**Square membership:** [[Square B]] (P1+P4 Cause-Experience axis).

**Refracts:** [[P4']] (Love / *Sorge* / holding-relation / frame-examining-its-own-framing).

**Element (per [[L2']]):** Water.

L1' is the Night counterpart of [[L1]]. Where L1 carries vital causal will (totem, definition), L1' carries the **typology of experiential function** — the eight Jungian function-attitude positions through which any psyche actually meets a phenomenon. Diagnostic, observational, classificatory — the outside view of inside life.

L1' is the other side of the **encounter axis** with [[L4]]: when both fire together, lived and observed cohere without collapsing into either pole.
