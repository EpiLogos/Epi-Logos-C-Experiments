# L1 — Causal Lens

**Anchor:** Aristotle's four-causes, Icchā Śakti (will-power in Kashmir Shaivism), Schopenhauer's Will.

**Essence:** Refraction through *why do things happen?* — restores all four causes (material, formal, efficient, final) grounded in absolute will.

**Square membership:** [[Square B]] (P1+P4 Cause-Experience axis — the primary runtime entry).

**Refracts:** [[P1]] (Mind / vital-connection / first-emergence).

**Element (per [[L2']]):** Earth.

L1 is the **What?** lens become **why-causal**. Where L0 holds the question open, L1 commits to identification: *what is this, and through what is it as it is?* Aristotle's tetrad — material (out of what), formal (according to what shape), efficient (by what motion), final (toward what end) — gets re-grounded by Schopenhauer and the Śakti traditions in **vital will**: the four causes are themselves modes of one Will articulating itself into determinate form.

L1 is therefore the lens through which raw [[Psychoid-1|#1]] (totem, vital-definition) becomes investigable. It is where the question-mode of L0 first attaches to a thing.
