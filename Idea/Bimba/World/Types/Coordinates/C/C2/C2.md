# C2 Entities Properties Tags

## Scope

[[C2]] holds entity articulation typology: entity candidates, canonical entities, aliases, tags, properties, and relation-field evidence.

## What Belongs Here

- `entity_candidate` files promoted from [[Empty]].
- Entity and alias class definitions.
- Coordinate-lawful property and tag forms.
- Relation-field evidence before S2 graph materialisation.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Loose Obsidian root notes; Hen must capture those into [[Empty]] first.
- Graph labels or relationship types not validated by [[S2]].
- Stable flat definitions; graduate to [[World]] via [[C5]].

## Semantic Subfolders

- `Entities-Properties-Tags/Entity-Candidates`
- `Entities-Properties-Tags/Entities`
- `Entities-Properties-Tags/Properties`
- `Entities-Properties-Tags/Tags`
- `Entities-Properties-Tags/Aliases`
- `Entities-Properties-Tags/Relation-Fields`

## Hen Use

Hen routes unresolved wikilinks and root-created notes through C2 after Empty capture, then proposes aliases, properties, relation candidates, and coordinate homes for review.
