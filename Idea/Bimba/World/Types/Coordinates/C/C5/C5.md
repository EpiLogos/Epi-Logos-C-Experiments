# C5 Crystallisations Pratibimba

## Scope

[[C5]] holds crystallisation typology: flat World graduation, integration forms, graduation receipts, and reflection/archive handoff.

## What Belongs Here

- Graduation receipts from World/Types incubation to flat [[World]].
- Integration forms and crystallisation-state definitions.
- Reflection/archive handoff forms.
- Stable World-form source pointers.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Live Seed evidence that has not stabilised.
- Duplicate copies of flat World definitions.
- Raw thought artifacts before they have passed through [[T]] / [[T′]] and Hen review.

## Semantic Subfolders

- `Crystallisations-Pratibimba/World-Forms`
- `Crystallisations-Pratibimba/Integration-Forms`
- `Crystallisations-Pratibimba/Graduation-Receipts`
- `Crystallisations-Pratibimba/Reflection-Archives`

## Hen Use

Hen uses C5 to record and verify graduation: the flat World form becomes canonical meaning, while type-local files remain MOC/source pointers.
