# Codon

## Definition

[[Codon]] is **[[M3]]'s symbolic-transcription unit** — the atomic carrier through which the symbolic-language substrate of Mahamaya emits readable structure. It is **not** a metaphor on biological codons; it *is* the same Cl(4,2) algebra read at the symbolic level, with biological codons being one downstream specialisation.

## Construction

**64 codons from 3 [[Nucleotide]]s × 2 bits each = 6 bits = 2⁶ = 64.** Each nucleotide contributes a bit-0 polarity (Yin/Yang) and a bit-1 mobility (Moving/Resting); three nucleotides stacked produce the 64-codon space.

## Rotational States

Codons split by internal symmetry into two classes:

- **40 non-dual codons** — at least one repeated adjacent pair, generating a symmetry axis → **7 rotational states** each.
- **24 dual codons** — all three nucleotide-positions distinct, no symmetry axis → **8 rotational states** each.

**Total rotational state count: 40 × 7 + 24 × 8 = 280 + 192 = 472 rotational states.**

## Quaternion Evaluation

Every codon evaluates to a quaternion `(pp, mm, mp, pm)` in **Cl(4,2)** — the same Clifford algebra carrying [[M1]] Paramasiva's SU(2) ring-quaternion and the M4-4-4-4 personal-quaternion. The **invariant** holds across the entire space:

```
pp + mm + mp + pm  =  4X
```

— where X is the codon's elemental-quaternion charge.

## Elemental Quaternion

Codons carry a four-element elemental-quaternion `(W, X, Y, Z) = (Earth, Fire, Water, Air)`. This is the L2' alchemical-elemental projection per [[L2']]; it is *not* an external decoration but a property of the codon-quaternion itself.

## Shared Algebra

The same Cl(4,2) algebra appears in three guises:

- [[M1]] Paramasiva — SU(2) **ring-quaternion** (the ontological ring).
- [[M3]] Mahamaya — codon **symbolic-quaternion** (this definition).
- [[M4]] Nara — M4-4-4-4 **personal-quaternion** (the inhabited shape).

This shared-algebra fact is what licenses [[Tarot]] and [[I-Ching]] to be reciprocal projections of the *same* substrate.
