# C3 Processes Canvases Diagrams

## Scope

[[C3]] holds process-form typology: MOC canvas forms, ideal canvas templates, architecture diagram forms, process canvases, and workflow-pattern canvases.

## What Belongs Here

- Ideal canvas forms and canvas templates.
- Architecture diagram forms after their Seed evidence stabilises.
- MOC canvas patterns and process-workspace patterns.
- Workflow diagrams that define reusable process structure.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Live architecture diagrams still under active spec development; keep those in [[Seeds]] diagram packs.
- Type authority folders; use [[C4]].
- Flat stable definitions; graduate to [[World]] through [[C5]].

## Semantic Subfolders

- `Processes-Canvases-Diagrams/MOC-Canvas-Forms`
- `Processes-Canvases-Diagrams/Architecture-Diagram-Forms`
- `Processes-Canvases-Diagrams/Workflow-Patterns`
- `Processes-Canvases-Diagrams/Process-Canvases`
- `Processes-Canvases-Diagrams/Ideal-Canvas-Templates`
- `Processes-Canvases-Diagrams/Ideal-Diagram-Templates`

## Hen Use

Hen uses C3 when an artifact's durable meaning is a process or visual workspace. Stable canvas and diagram forms become type evidence; live diagrams remain Seed evidence until crystallised.
