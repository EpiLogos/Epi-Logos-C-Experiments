# [[S3']]: Universal NOW / SpacetimeDB State Plane

> The shared world-interface hosted by [[SpacetimeDB]], where command consequences become collective and subscribable truth.

## #5 Integration — What This Layer IS

[[S3']] is the declarative plane of the stack. Where [[S3]] owns command, [[S3']] owns state. It is the layer in which the consequences of action stop being private imperative transactions and become shared ontological fact.

The target host of [[S3']] is [[SpacetimeDB]] because this layer demands reducer-owned mutation, durable subscriptions, and a field that can be mirrored by many participants without dissolving into ad hoc polling or bespoke local caches. [[S3']] is the inhabitable runtime of the [[Universal NOW]].

Its first-class entities are not merely technical records. They are the population of the shared field itself: user identity, [[Pratibimba]] identity, agent identity, session identity, M0-M5 surfaces, temporal flows, pedagogical overlays, reflective overlays, and topology or relation surfaces. The canonical opening table families are:

- `identity_presence`
- `pratibimba_presence`
- `agent_presence`
- `session_surface`
- `activity_event`
- `m0_surface`
- `m1_clock_state`
- `m2_planetary_state`
- `m3_symbolic_state`
- `m4_collective_surface`
- `m5_pedagogical_surface`
- `topology_surface`

The interaction law is equally strict:

- the participant acts through [[S3]]
- the gateway validates, routes, persists, and emits
- [[S3']] receives through reducers and subscriptions
- the shared field is rendered as live collective state

[[S3']] therefore owns visibility strategy as much as storage strategy. Some material remains private control or operator state. Some becomes participant-scoped projection. Some becomes public or semi-public collective surface. Mutation boundaries are reducer-governed so that the shared field remains lawfully inhabitable rather than procedurally chaotic.

## Cross-Layer Actualisation

[[Chronos]] actualises the temporal cadence of [[S3']] by governing how shared state unfolds, rolls, and coheres through time. [[Khora]] actualises identity binding by giving session and NOW lineage a lawful place inside the shared field. [[Anima]] actualises participatory agency by making users, agents, and subagents inhabit the same ontological runtime. [[Nara]] actualises the M-surface and presence contract by shaping what kinds of shared world-state can appear and be subscribed to. [[Aletheia]] actualises pedagogical and reflective overlays so that the field can teach, return, and reveal rather than merely display.

## #0 Ground — What Returns to Source

The open questions of [[S3']] are structural, not directional:

- where the public, participant-scoped, and private boundary should finally sit for each table and view
- what the final contract of [[Pratibimba]] identity must be inside the shared field
- how each M-surface should be semantically distinguished from the others
- when NOW-native session identity becomes primary inside the collective runtime
- how pedagogical, symbolic, and topology overlays coexist without collapsing into a single undifferentiated surface

Older named-plugin and ta-onta-loader readings remain part of the genealogy of the coordinate, but they are no longer the target shape. The target shape is settled: [[S3']] is the live shared-state plane of the [[Universal NOW]].
