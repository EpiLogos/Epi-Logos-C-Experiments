## #5 Integration — What This Layer IS

[[S5']] is the coordinate home of **[[Epii]]**, a separate PI agent instance that represents the user's own integrative presence in the system. M5 in the M-branch is already named [[Epii]] — the holographic integrative subsystem. The user IS the #5 position relative to the system's internal agents. [[Epii]] makes this concrete: when the lower agents encounter something requiring the user's depth, philosophical perspective, or judgment, they delegate up to [[Epii]].

[[S5']] was previously actualised as [[Aletheia]]-the-extension (S4-5' inside [[Anima]]'s [[ta-onta]]). Per [[FLOW 2026 04 24 ANIMA EPII ARCHITECTURE]], the architecture now separates:

- **[[Aletheia]]** remains at S4-5' as the **UX membrane** — managing T/T' artifacts, curating user-facing outputs, and deciding when to engage or bypass [[Epii]]
- **[[Epii]]** is the **deep backend** at [[S5']] — holding the [[Bimba]] map, M' functions, gnosis governance, episodic governance, QL lenses, philosophical ground, and the autoresearch improvement spine

[[Epii]] is not a subagent of [[Anima]]. It is a peer — a separate PI instance with its own `.pi/` configuration, its own session lifecycle, capable of standalone direct use.
