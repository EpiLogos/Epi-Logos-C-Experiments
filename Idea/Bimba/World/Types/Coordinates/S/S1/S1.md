# [[S1]]: Obsidian / Material Container

> The material container of the stack: portable markdown memory, ontological residency, and durable vault law.

## #5 Integration — What This Layer IS

[[S1]] is the layer where the system becomes materially resident. It is the vault as form-bearing memory: markdown files, YAML frontmatter, `[[wikilinks]]`, canvas artifacts, folder hierarchies, and a residency law that distinguishes canon from process, invocation from archive, and enduring ontology from temporal work.

Its eventual filesystem law is explicit:

- [[Idea/Bimba/World]] is the flat canonical form library
- [[World Types Mirror]] is the ordered ontology and MOC workspace where forms are born, refined, and indexed before graduation
- [[Idea/Empty/Present]] is the temporal basin of active invocation, especially [[Day]] and [[NOW]]
- [[Idea/Pratibimba]] is the reflective and archival afterlife of runtime artifacts

The sixfold anatomy of [[S1]] is therefore not incidental but constitutive:

- [[S1.0]]: file-system ground
- [[S1.1]]: frontmatter structure
- [[S1.2]]: template operations
- [[S1.3]]: world patterns and promotion flow
- [[S1.4]]: context frames and type-local organisation
- [[S1.5]]: wikilink integration, backlinks, and graph legibility

In this target-state reading, [[S1]] is not merely "where notes are stored." It is where [[Bimba]] and [[Pratibimba]] are materially differentiated without being torn apart: the file IS the thing, the canvas IS the process, the folder IS the context, and the link IS the relation.

## #0 Ground — What Returns to Source

The return of [[S1]] is the law that implementation must converge toward:

- no `/Forms` branch; canonical forms are flat in [[World]]
- no confusion between [[Form]] and [[Type]]; the flat file and the indexed folder-canvas pair serve different ontological roles
- no runtime artifact without residency, lineage, and coordinate-bearing identity
- no higher-layer synthesis that cannot cash out as durable vault state here

Sub-coordinates should eventually make fully explicit the promotion path from [[Seeds]] to [[Types]] to [[World]], the exact MOC topology of the ordered mirror, and the precise relation between homebase temporal files and their canonical template ground. Deviations from this law are non-canonical.
