# Diagrams Type MOC

## Scope

[[Diagrams]] indexes diagram and MOC surfaces without relocating live architecture diagrams out of [[Seeds]].

## What Belongs Here

- References to [[ARCHITECTURE-DIAGRAM-PACK]] heading anchors.
- Coordinate-local canvases under [[World/Types]].
- Visual MOC governance and diagram-to-crystallisation flows.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Whole-system architecture diagram packs that are still active Seed evidence.
- Renderer-local diagrams with no Seed/spec provenance.

## Flat World Forms

Stable diagram meaning may crystallise into flat [[World]] definitions such as [[World-Ontology]], while visual canvases remain in [[World/Types]] or Seed packs.

## Seed Evidence

Start with [[ARCHITECTURE-DIAGRAM-PACK#Diagram And MOC Residency Protocol]], then follow the owning coordinate specs and shard specs.

## MOC/Canvas Rule

This file is the textual MOC. [[Diagrams.canvas]] is the visual MOC. Every diagram consumed by a spec must be linked by heading anchor, not only by Mermaid label.

## Graph Label Intent

Diagram folder ancestry should become diagram/MOC evidence for Hen/S2 promotion. It does not move the canonical Seed diagram source.
