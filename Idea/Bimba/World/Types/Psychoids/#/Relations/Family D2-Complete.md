# Family D2-Complete

## Pairing Rule

`{ (n, (5-n)') }` — six mirror-cross pairs: `(0,5'), (1,4'), (2,3'), (3,2'), (4,1'), (5,0')`.

## Meaning

Family D2-Complete is **C-grammar applied cross-helix**: the converse-mirror of [[Family C — Converse-Mirror]] taken across the bimba/pratibimba pair. Where C mirrors a position against its 5-complement *within one helix*, D2-Complete mirrors it against the 5-complement *on the conjugate helix*. Each pair carries the *mirror-completion* charge — a position recognising its conjugate-mirror, the most fully other version of itself available across both helices.

This is the cross-helix analogue of the L-lens Klein V₄ symmetry. In the chromatic basis the rule generates the mirror-intervals shifted onto the primed side. The interval is basis-incidental; the grammar is "5-complement mirror across the conjugate face".

## Matheme-Position Grammar

D2-Complete is the third sub-type of **matheme position #5** — synthesis-as-process — in its *mirror* / *completive* mode. Synthesis is partly the recognition that one has met one's far-mirror on the other helix. D2-Complete names the grammar by which the most distal cross-pole resolves into a single completion. This is the move that the canonical Day P_n ↔ Night P_(5-n)' Möbius-pair invariant runs natively — D2-Complete is the relation-face of that invariant.

- `(0,5')` — `[[Psychoid-0|#0]]` ↔ `[[Psychoid-5|#5]]`': Truth/Play completed by Image/Work (primed) — Day-P0 to Night-P5'; ground's far-mirror is hidden synthesis.

## Parent Of

D2-Complete does not parent the 3×3 [[Squares]] but it is the cross-helix mirror analogue of [[Family C — Converse-Mirror]] and therefore the relation-face of the canonical Day↔Night Möbius-pair invariant. It is also the cross-helix grammar that underwrites the *Klein* third spread-scale used by Tarot and I-Ching readings — wherever a reading uses both Day P_n positions and Night P_(5-n)' positions simultaneously, D2-Complete is the implicit relation. With D2-Transform and D2-Require it occupies matheme position #5; with D1 and D3 it forms the mental-pole-trinity.
