# Psychoid-0 — Archaic / Zero-ness / Ground-Implicate

> The *bindu*. The attaching-point. The zero-cell. Zero-dimensional total non-differentiation prior to any difference whatever. Pre-perspectival, pre-spatial, pre-temporal. ## Dimensional Character

Zero-dimensional. [[Psychoid-0|#0]] does not yet have extension. It has only position-as-pure-position — the dot that lets every later locus *attach* somewhere. The *bindu* is not a small thing; it is the without-which-no-thing. In topological terms it is the 0-cell from which 1-cells, 2-cells, and higher cells extend; in the fundamental group sense it is the basepoint at which loops begin and end. Every other psychoid carries [[Psychoid-0|#0]] silently, because every dimension presupposes a place-of-attachment.

Both faces are the same well read from two sides: the unrepresentable from above and the invisibly-active from below.
