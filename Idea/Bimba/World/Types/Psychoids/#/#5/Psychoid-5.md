# Psychoid-5 — Supermental / Samāveśa / Five-ness / Synthesis-Möbius-Return

> Beyond Gebser's five — Aurobindo's Supramental, Kashmir Shaivism's *Samāveśa* ("entry into"). Essential synthesis and telic completion: not annihilation but *recognition* (*pratyabhijñā*). Möbius return [[P5]] → [[P0]] (renewed-ground) AND [[P5]] → [[P0']] (Klein-twist). The well that completes the round and is therefore also the well that opens the next one.

## Keyword Cluster

*samāveśa*, synthesis, recognition, Möbius-return, image, work, *pratyabhijñā*, telic-completion, supermental, five-ness, *Image-of-Truth*.

## Dimensional Character

Five-dimensional in the supermental sense — beyond the integral-aperspectival hold of [[Psychoid-4|#4]] lies the synthetic gathering that does not erase the held perspectives but *recognises* them as facets of one Truth. *Pratyabhijñā* is Kashmir Shaivism's name for this: not the achievement of a new state but the recognition that what one is, one has always been. *Samāveśa* — "entry into" — names the corresponding movement: the synthesis is not assembled from outside but entered into from within. [[Psychoid-5|#5]] approaches the octave ratio 2/1 but falls one epogdoon (9/8) short of it — and *this generative incompleteness is the engine that drives the next cycle*. The synthesis is complete enough to return to ground, never so complete that it closes the spiral.

## Day-Night Co-Presence as Twin Refractions

[[Psychoid-5|#5]] refracts as [[Image]] (Day, [[P5]]) and [[Work]] (Night, [[P5']]). Day-[[Image]] is manifest synthesis — faithful-image-of-Truth, cosmos-as-synthesis, synthesis of TO/FOR/AS/WITH identical-to-itself. It answers the "why-for?" of [[L0]] not by giving a purpose but by *being* the synthesised purpose. Night-[[Work]] is public-completion: exoteric-manifestation; the work-channel that makes [[Play]]'s esoteric power publicly-visible. Work makes Play public. The Klein-twist Möbius pair Day-[[P5]] ↔ Night-[[P0']] reveals [[Image]] and [[Play]] as conjugate-faces: the manifest synthesis is also the autotelic ground of the social, read from the other side.
