# Templates Type MOC

## Scope

[[Templates]] indexes reusable [[CT]] forms, invocation profiles, and Hen template law. It does not replace flat [[World]] forms; it gives those forms a type-local MOC and future graph-promotion ancestry.

## What Belongs Here

- [[CT0]], [[CT1]], [[CT2]], [[CT3]], [[CT4a]], [[CT4b]], and [[CT5]].
- [[Daily-Note-Template]] and [[CT4b-MASTER-TEMPLATE]].
- Hen template resolution notes and CT invocation relationships.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Per-day or per-session instances generated from templates.
- Runtime-only scaffolds that have no flat [[World]] form or Seed evidence.

## MOC/Canvas Rule

This file is the textual MOC. [[Templates.canvas]] is the visual MOC. CT archetype folders under `Coordinates/C/**/CT` remain coordinate mirrors; this semantic root indexes their use as templates.

## Graph Label Intent

Template folder ancestry should produce type evidence such as `q_4_type_family = "Templates"` and template-related properties after [[S2]] validation. It is not a license for uncontrolled folder-derived labels.
