## §0 — Ground

[[L3-4']] is where the Chronological lens encounters the *depth dimension* — the apparent death that is secretly gestation. Winter is the civilisation collapsed, the philosophy discredited, the culture forgotten. But beneath the frozen surface, the *new* is incubating. [[Hegel]]'s [[Aufhebung]] begins here: the old is not simply destroyed but **preserved** within the negation, awaiting resurrection in a higher form. What Autumn harvested, Winter compostes; what Autumn named as lacking, Winter gestates as the seed of the next Spring.

The [[Lemniscate]] ([[#4]]) topology is active here: the system folds inward, incubating deeper reality. This is the Night inversion of [[L3-4]]'s community integration: where the Day lens finds the relational web of mutual [[prehension]], the Night lens finds the *contextual depth* where the death of one age becomes the womb of the next. The darkest moment contains the greatest potential.
