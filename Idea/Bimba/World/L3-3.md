# [[L3-3]] — Eternal Objects

> **Sub-lens #3 of [[L3]] [[Processual]]:** Pure potentials, "forms of definiteness" — [[Whitehead]]'s version of Platonic Forms without static eternity. The repertoire of possible patterning.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L3-3]] is where the [[Processual]] lens encounters *pattern itself*. [[Eternal objects]] are [[Whitehead]]'s name for the pure potentials that constitute the character of experience — shades of colour, mathematical structures, aesthetic forms, logical relations. They are not themselves temporal; they do not come into being or pass away. Yet they are not static Platonic Forms either: they exist only *in their ingression* into becoming occasions, as the repertoire of possible ways things can be.

This is the blooming moment where pattern declares itself. [[L3-3]] resonates with [[P3]] (Pattern / Process): [[eternal objects]] ARE the patterns — the formal architecture of possibility. They are not "things" but the *way things can be*. The processual lens at #3 encounters a paradox: process itself requires something non-processual — the stable forms that give each occasion its definite character.

---

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** Eternal Objects
**Tradition:** [[Whitehead]]'s *Process and Reality*; [[Plato]]'s Forms (corrected by process); [[Deleuze]]'s virtual multiplicities; [[Lacan]]'s symbolic order
**Concept:** The formal patterns available for actualisation — "forms of definiteness" constituting the character of each experience. Not themselves temporal but the *repertoire of possible patterning* that temporal occasions can realise. [[L3]]'s encounter with [[P3]] (Pattern): the processual face of pattern is the timeless repertoire that makes each temporal occasion qualitatively unique.

---

## §2 — Operation

[[L3-3]] receives the dynamic of [[ingression]] from [[L3-2]] and provides the full catalogue of what CAN ingress. It feeds into [[L3-4]] (Community Integration), where the patterns that individual occasions realise become the basis for larger organisational structures. The arc: [[L3-0]] (desire) → [[L3-1]] (occasion) → [[L3-2]] (ingression) → **[[L3-3]]** (repertoire of forms) → [[L3-4]] (community) → [[L3-5]] (satisfaction). [[L3-3]] is the *library* of becoming: the stock of formal possibilities from which each occasion selects its unique combination, guided by its subjective aim.
