# [[L2-4]] — NEITHER: IS nor IS-NOT — Void, Ineffability

> **Sub-lens #4 of [[L2]] [[Logical]]:** Transcendence of the binary framework entirely. [[Nāgārjuna]]'s [[śūnyatā]], [[Wittgenstein]]'s silence at the boundary of language. The [[lemniscate]] fold where logic turns inward.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L2-4]] is the fourth corner of the [[catuṣkoṭi]], and it is the most radical: neither IS nor IS-NOT. Here, the entire framework of affirmation and negation is *transcended* — not by combining them (that was BOTH) but by stepping outside the space in which they operate. [[Nāgārjuna]]'s [[śūnyatā]] ("emptiness") is not mere nothingness but the emptiness of *inherent existence* in every conceptual determination. Nothing — not IS, not IS-NOT, not BOTH — has intrinsic, independent being.

[[Wittgenstein]]'s *Tractatus* arrives at the same boundary from the analytic tradition: "Whereof one cannot speak, thereof one must be silent." The NEITHER position is this silence — not ignorance but the recognition that some realities lie beyond the reach of any propositional logic, however many values it admits.

As the [[#4]] position, [[L2-4]] occupies the [[lemniscate]] fold — the point where logic turns inward and encounters its own limit, the context that reveals the boundary conditions of all logical positions.

---

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** NEITHER (IS nor IS-NOT) — Void, Ineffability
**Tradition:** [[Nāgārjuna]]'s [[śūnyatā]] (emptiness of inherent existence); [[Wittgenstein]]'s *Tractatus* 7; [[intuitionistic logic]] (rejection of [[excluded middle]]); [[Pyrrhonian]] *epochē* (suspension of judgement); [[Meister Eckhart]]'s *Abgeschiedenheit*
**Concept:** The transcendence of the binary framework — not a fifth option within the system but the recognition that IS/IS-NOT/BOTH does not exhaust reality. NEITHER is the logical face of [[śūnyatā]]: the emptiness that is not nothing but the groundless ground of all determinations.

---

## §2 — Operation

[[L2-4]] receives the paradoxical energy of [[L2-3]] (BOTH) and *releases it* — if BOTH shows that contradictions can coexist, NEITHER shows that the entire oppositional framework can be set down. This is not indifference but liberation: what [[Pyrrhonism]] called *ataraxia* (tranquillity) through suspension. [[L2-4]] feeds into [[L2-5]] (SILENCE), because the recognition that neither affirmation nor negation (nor their conjunction) captures reality naturally opens into the silence that holds all of them.

The [[lemniscate]] topology at #4: logic folds back upon itself. [[L2-4]] is the point where the Torus of the [[catuṣkoṭi]] crosses its own track — outward logical movement turns inward to encounter the conditions of logic itself.
