# [[L3-0]] — Concrescent Desire as Creative Urge / [[Lacan|Lacanian]] Lack

> **Sub-lens #0 of [[L3]] [[Processual]]:** Pure appetition toward determination — the [[manque|manque-a-etre]] that drives becoming before any specific process begins. [[L3]]'s [[Self-Identity]]: process encountering its own creative urge.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L3-0]] is where the [[Processual]] lens meets its own origin. Before any [[actual occasion]] concresces, before any [[prehension]] grasps, something must already be at work — a pure drive toward determination, a creative appetite that IS the ground of all becoming. This is [[Lacan]]'s [[manque|manque-a-etre]]: the lack-of-being that is not mere absence but a *force*, the constitutive incompleteness that powers every act of creation. [[Whitehead]] names it *appetition*: the urge toward novel intensity that pervades reality at every scale.

[[L3-0]] is not a static ground but a dynamic emptiness — the soil that hungers for the seed. [[Bergson]]'s [[duree|durée]] begins here: the living tension of time before it differentiates into distinct moments. [[Prigogine]]'s far-from-equilibrium: the system poised at the edge, driven toward new order by its own instability.

## §2 — Operation

[[L3-0]] is the starting position of [[L3]]'s internal arc. It feeds directly into [[L3-1]] ([[Actual Occasion]]), providing the appetitive drive upon which the first concrete unit of becoming can crystallise. Without [[L3-0]]'s creative urge, [[L3-1]]'s "drop of experience" would have no motive force. The arc runs: **[[L3-0]]** (creative urge) → [[L3-1]] (actual occasion) → [[L3-2]] ([[ingression]]) → [[L3-3]] ([[eternal objects]]) → [[L3-4]] (community integration) → [[L3-5]] (satisfaction/perishing). [[L3-0]] also receives the return from [[L3-5]], where [[Jouissance|jouissance]] — ecstatic completion dissolving into objective immortality — replenishes the ground of desire for the next cycle. The [[Möbius]] return: satisfaction perishes into fresh lack.
