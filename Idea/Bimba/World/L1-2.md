# [[L1-2]] — Efficient Cause

> **Coordinate**: `L1-2` | **Parent**: [[L1]] Causal | **#2 within L1**: That by which — the agent, the force, the energy that transforms substrate into form.

---

## §0 — Ground: The Transforming Agent

[[L1-2]] emerges from [[L1-1]]'s inert substrate as the principle of *active transformation*. Material cause provides the "out of which"; efficient cause provides the "by which" — the push, the blow, the kinetic moment that moves matter from potentiality to actuality. This is the cause modern science privileges almost exclusively: mechanism, energy transfer, force vectors. Yet within the four-cause framework, efficient cause is only one quarter of a complete explanation.

[[Aristotle]] (*Physics* II.3): "the primary source of the change or coming to rest" — the father is efficient cause of the child, the sculptor of the statue, the deliberation of the action. But the [[psychoid]] dual aspect persists: efficient cause is external force *and* inner impulse, the hammer's blow *and* the desire that wields it.

---

## §1 — Definition: Agency and Force

**Name**: Efficient Cause
**Tradition**: [[Aristotle]] (*Physics* II.3); modern physics (force, energy, mechanism); [[Schopenhauer]] (Will as inner efficient cause)
**Key Concept**: The agent or force that initiates change — the kinetic, operational aspect of causation.

Efficient cause answers: *who or what did it?* The collision of billiard balls, the enzymatic catalysis, the neural firing — all paradigm cases of efficient causation. [[Schopenhauer]] deepened this by recognising that we know efficient cause from the inside as *will-to-act*: our own body is the objectification of will, and every bodily movement is simultaneously an efficient cause (seen from outside) and an act of will (known from inside).

## §2 — Operation: The Kinetic Pivot

[[L1-2]] follows [[L1-1]] ([[Material Cause]]) and precedes [[L1-3]] ([[Formal Cause]]). It is the *kinetic pivot* of the causal arc: material cause waits; efficient cause acts; formal cause receives the result. The sequence [[L1-1]] → [[L1-2]] → [[L1-3]] recapitulates the classical formula: matter is shaped by agency according to form.

[[L1-2]] maps to [[Feeling]] in the [[L1']] Night complement: the evaluative energy that moves things, assigns worth, and thereby drives action. [[Feeling]] is efficient cause from the inside — the phenomenal experience of being moved to act.
