## §2 — Operation: Process & Function

[[L4]] operates through [[Heidegger]]ian existential analysis:
- [[L4.0]] — [[Sein]] ([[Being]]) — The question of the meaning of Being
- [[L4.1]] — Thrownness ([[Geworfenheit]]) — The givenness of existence
- [[L4.2]] — [[Dasein]] — Being-there, human existence
- [[L4.3]] — [[Zeit]] ([[Time]]) — Temporality as horizon of Being
- [[L4.4]] — [[Besorge]] ([[Concern]]) — Caring engagement with world
- [[L4.5]] — [[Gelassenheit]] ([[Releasement]]) — Letting-be, openness

When you perceive through [[L4]], you engage the world through:
- **Being-in-the-world**: The unity of subject and object
- **Thrownness**: Finding oneself in a world not of one's choosing
- **Care** ([[Sorge]]): The fundamental structure of Dasein
- **Temporality**: Time as the meaning of Being
- **Science of experience**: Phenomenological investigation

- **Context is primary**: Meaning arises from situation
