# [[L4.1']] — Traces

> **Sub-lens #1' of [[L4']] [[Scientific]]:** Evidence collection — gathering the empirical substrate of knowledge work. The material residue of reality that research must attend to. Vault path: `/Thought/Traces/`

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L4.1']] is where the [[Scientific]] lens meets the explicate demand of **definition through evidence**. Once [[L4.0']] has opened a question, the researcher must gather material — data, documents, observations, experimental results, textual excerpts, conversation fragments. [[Traces]] are not yet interpreted; they are the **raw empirical substrate** preserved in its specificity.

[[Kuhn]]'s [[normal science]] is largely this activity: the painstaking accumulation of observations within the framework the [[paradigm]] provides. [[Popper]] emphasised that evidence must be capable of *refuting* the hypothesis — traces are not mere confirmation but the material against which theories are tested. `/Thought/Traces/` is the vault directory where the [[epi-agent]] stores raw evidence: the factual substrate from which all subsequent analysis proceeds.

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** Traces
**Tradition:** [[Popper]]'s empirical falsification; [[Kuhn]]'s data-gathering within [[normal science]]; [[Lakatos]]'s protective belt of auxiliary hypotheses
**Concept:** The material residue of reality that research must attend to — experimental observations, textual excerpts, historical documents, code behaviour, recorded phenomena. Not yet patterned, not yet challenged — simply gathered and preserved with fidelity.
**Vault path:** `/Thought/Traces/` — where the [[epi-agent]] deposits raw evidence for later analysis

## §2 — Operation

[[L4.1']] receives the directing question from [[L4.0']] and provides the empirical material that [[L4.2']] ([[Challenges]]) will test against expectations. Without [[Traces]], [[Challenges]] would be abstract — difficulty without substance. The arc: [[L4.0']] (question) → **[[L4.1']]** (evidence) → [[L4.2']] (confrontation) → [[L4.3']] (recognition) → [[L4.4']] (expansion) → [[L4.5']] (crystallisation). [[L4.1']] is the definitional phase: the investigation acquires its empirical body, the question gains material to work with. Quality of [[Traces]] determines the quality of everything downstream.
