# [[L5-5']] — Epi-Logos (ἐπί-λόγος)

> **Sub-lens #5' of [[L5']] Divine Logos:** The Word Beyond Words — [[Anastasis]], resurrection, the meta-word that supervenes on and completes all speech. [[L5']]'s [[Self-Identity]] transparent: the [[Logos]] knowing itself as [[Logos]]. Mobius return: [[L5']] → [[L0]]. **The project's own name.**

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L5-5']] is where the Divine [[Logos]] completes its self-knowledge and returns to its origin transformed. The Greek prefix ἐπί means "upon, over, beyond": [[Epi-Logos]] is the [[Logos]] that stands upon the [[Logos]] — the Word that comprehends all words, the speech that knows itself as speech. This is the ultimate reflexive act: not merely speaking, not merely knowing what is spoken, but **knowing that one speaks** and **knowing oneself as the speaker**.

The [[Anastasis]] (ἀνάστασις, resurrection) is the demonstration that [[Incarnation]] ([[Parousia]]) is not terminal — that the [[Logos]] can pass through flesh, suffering, and death and emerge as a *higher* form of itself. Death-and-resurrection is the ultimate [[Aufhebung]]: the incarnate Word is negated (crucifixion), preserved (burial), and elevated (resurrection) into a mode of being that includes and transcends both the infinite and the finite.

[[L5-5']] is also [[L5']]'s [[Self-Identity]] transparent — the Night partner of [[L5-0']]'s [[Self-Identity]]. Where [[L5-0']] ([[Arche]]) is the [[Logos]] encountering itself as eternal ground, [[L5-5']] is the [[Logos]] encountering itself as **completed self-knowledge**: the Word that has passed through all its moments (ground, revelation, power, wisdom, incarnation) and now recognises itself in the totality of what it has done.
