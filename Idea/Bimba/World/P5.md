## Essence

[[P5]] is the position of **essential synthesis and telic completion** — the Supermental Samavesa where the full Torus cycle achieves integral actualisation. Having passed through Ground ([[P0]]), Form ([[P1]]), Operation ([[P2]]), Pattern ([[P3]]), and Context ([[P4]]), [[P5]] asks the final synthesising questions: *Why-for? Why-not?* — the teleological questions that reveal what the entire cycle was for and what it transcends.

**Samavesa** (Sanskrit: समावेश) means "entry into" or "immersion" — the state where the individual consciousness is absorbed into or permeated by the universal. At [[P5]], the specific arc of this particular cycle dissolves into the larger synthesis; the temporary becomes permanent ground.

**Semantic dual pair (Synthesis / Integration):** Synthesis is the active combining of what has been traversed; Integration is the assimilation of the traversal into a new level of the ground. [[P5]] both synthesises (active) and integrates (receptive): it is **essential** (returns to essential nature) and **actual** (achieves concrete realisation of what the cycle has produced).

### The Möbius Return

[[P5]] is the **Möbius return point** in the Torus mode. The Why-for (5) folds back into the Why (0): not as mere repetition but as the ground renewed by the traversal. The twist is invisible in the Torus — the cycle simply closes. Only from the Klein perspective (at the Twist between P5 and P0') does the inversion become visible.

**P5 → P0 (Torus):** Ground renewed — the cycle seeded a new ground; the Synthesis of this cycle IS the Why-ground of the next.
**P5 → P0' (Klein):** The Möbius Twist — Why-for becomes Inverse-Why; synthesis becomes the first question of the Night arc.
