## Essence

[[P2]] is the position of **active process and energic operation** — where the defined form ([[P1]]) begins to move, transform, and produce effects. The Mythic Structure is the domain of narrative becoming: the world is not a set of objects but a field of forces and processes engaged in dynamic interaction.

**Semantic dual pair (Dynamis / Operation):** Dynamis is the inner potency or force that drives; Operation is the actualised execution — the function running, the process transforming, the energy moving. Together they name the double face of P2: potency-in-reserve and potency-in-act.

The Mythic structure is **operational** (things are understood through what they do) and **energic** (meaning arises from forces, not substances). This is why myth is full of *verbs* — gods do things, heroes undergo transformation, processes unfold.
