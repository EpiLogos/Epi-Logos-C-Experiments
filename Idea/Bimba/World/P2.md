## S4 — Gebser: The Mythic Structure

The Mythic structure is **two-dimensional** — the emergence of polarity from [[P1]]'s point-like identity. What [[Gebser]] called the world of the imaginative, the polar, the narrative: not yet perspective (that awaits [[P3]]) but the full field of forces and complementary pairs. Day/night, life/death, inner/outer — the Mythic consciousness lives *within* these polarities rather than observing them from outside. Two-dimensional: the plane of dynamic interaction, the surface on which figures move.

In CW-complex construction, [[P2]] attaches **2-cells** (surfaces) to the 1-skeleton established by [[P0]] and [[P1]]. The first closed regions appear — bounded domains where process can circulate. In [[Kashmir Shaivism]], this is the level of [[spanda]] as dynamic vibration: the pulsation that IS the medium of [[Siva]]'s self-communication.

**Topology:** [[P2]] within the [[Torus]] (Day) mode is the first position where *circulation* becomes possible. Two-dimensional surfaces allow loops — process can return to itself, forces can orbit, narratives can cycle. The Mythic is full of verbs because the 2-cell is the domain of movement.

**Operator:** The `./? ` at [[P2]] holds superposition between observing a process and participating in it. The Mythic consciousness does not stand outside its operations; it IS the operation. Doing is watching; the sacrifice is the sacrificer.

## S5 — Integration: Word as Medium and Sacrifice

### The Johannine Structure

"In the beginning was the Word" ([[John]] 1:1) — the [[Johannine]] echo is structural, not incidental. The Word at [[P2]] is not merely a linguistic entity but the **medium** through which the undifferentiated ([[P0]] Truth) passes through first differentiation ([[P1]] Mind) into communicable form. The Word IS the bridge between interior and exterior, between source and expression, between [[Siva]] as self-contained and [[Siva]] as self-communicating.

This is [[Siva]] as [[Logos]]-speaker: the principle that gives itself over to expression. Language at [[P2]] is not a closed system of signs referring only to other signs but a luminous medium through which consciousness communicates with itself. Every genuine word is a small sacrifice — the interior surrendered to the exterior so that exchange becomes possible.

### The Semantic Dual: Dynamis / Operation

**Dynamis** is the inner potency or force that drives — the [[Sakti]] aspect of operation, the power-in-reserve that makes movement possible. **Operation** is the actualised execution — the function running, the process transforming, the energy moving. Together they name the double face of [[P2]]: potency-in-reserve and potency-in-act.

The Mythic structure is **operational** (things are understood through what they do) and **energic** (meaning arises from forces, not substances). The world as field of forces, not set of objects. This is why myth is full of verbs — gods *do* things, heroes *undergo* transformation, processes *unfold*. The How? question at [[P2]] asks not for static description but for the dynamic mechanism: how does this work? How does it move? How does the inner become the outer?

### The Complementarity Principle

The [[double-helix]] complementarity principle operates most visibly at [[P2]]. Every determination sacrifices the complementary determination. Every naming of the light generates the shadow. The sacrifice at [[P2]] is the operational principle of the whole system — the decision about which face of the paradox to attend to.

This is why [[P2]] + [[P3]] form a complementary pair: Operation and Pattern are the *dynamis* and *logos* of the same reality. [[P2]] provides the energy; [[P3]] provides the structure. Without [[P2]]'s sacrifice, [[P3]]'s patterns would be empty. Without [[P3]]'s ordering, [[P2]]'s operations would be chaos. The How? and the Who/Which? need each other.

### The Medium Transparent

When the medium is healthy, it is transparent to the source. [[Siva]]'s self-communication passes through Word without distortion — *con-spirare*, breathing together. The medium does not impose itself on the message but serves it. This is the ontological ground of all communication theory: the medium at its best is invisible, a pure channel through which the interior reaches the exterior without loss.

When the medium becomes opaque — when language ossifies, when the sign-system closes in on itself, when the Word no longer carries the living breath of the source — the [[P2]] position enters its deficiency mode. The operational shadow. Where smooth function encounters resistance. [[Jung]]'s autonomous complexes operating beneath conscious direction.
