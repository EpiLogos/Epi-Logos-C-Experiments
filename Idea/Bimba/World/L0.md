## §4 — Context: Number as Psychoid Archetype

[[L0]] is the **primary hinge** between the raw [[QL]] positions and both numericality and semantics. It is the lens through which the mute [[P]]-positions first become *questions* — the exact point where [[archetypal]] number becomes epistemic act. [[L0]] is the [[Quaternal]] Logic frame itself viewed as a lens: the most primordial layer where the meaning of the numbers themselves first surfaces as the capacity for inquiry.

[[Jung]] and [[Pauli]]'s dialogue established that number occupies a unique [[psychoid]] position — it is simultaneously the most primitive element of order in the mind AND the most fundamental structure of physical reality. [[Jung]] wrote that number is "the most primitive element of order in the human mind," defining it psychologically as "an archetype of order which has become conscious." [[Pauli]] saw number as the bridge between quantity (physics) and quality (psychology), offering what he called a "neutral language" describing reality without dividing subjective from objective. [[Marie-Louise von Franz]] developed this in *Number and Time*: each number is "an abstract pattern of rhythmical behaviour" — an inherent dynamic tendency. Numbers are not merely quantities but carry fundamental *archetypal* meanings, functioning as "dynamical ordering factors" active in both psyche and nature.

[[L0]] operationalises this insight: the six questions ARE the six numbers in their epistemic mode. The question "What?" IS the archetype of One-ness (first definition, first emergence). The question "How?" IS the archetype of Two-ness (polarity, relation, process). And so on. [[L0]] is where [[QL]] becomes self-aware as a questioning structure.

## §5 — Integration: The Six Questions

### [[L0-0]] — Why (./?): Insofar-as (in Quantum)

The presuppositional frame — what must be established as shared understanding before inquiry can begin. The "insofar as X" establishes the quantum of assumed context: the background knowledge, the working definitions, the established understanding that both parties accept before the first question lands. Without this, questioning operates in a vacuum. This is the [[QL]] frame's own [[#0]] — the ground-condition for the questioning structure itself. The [[P0]] of [[L0]]: why is there questioning at all?

### [[L0-1]] — What (./?): The Definitional Question

The first differentiation within the question-space. "What is this?" establishes identity, draws the first boundary between this and not-this. The monad of inquiry — the moment [[P1]] (Definition / Form) appears within the epistemic register. [[L0-1]] IS the archetype of One-ness: first emergence, first naming, the initial gesture of distinction.

### [[L0-2]] — How (./?): The Operational Question

"How does this work?" introduces relation, dynamics, process. The dyad of inquiry: now there are two terms in relation. [[L0-2]] resonates with [[P2]] (Operation / Entity) — the question that demands mechanism, procedure, the disclosure of inner workings. The archetype of Two-ness: polarity and operation.

### [[L0-3]] — Whom/Which/When (./?): The Pattern Question

"What kind of thing is this?" seeks the formal architecture, the type, the recognisable pattern. The triad of inquiry: mediation between particulars. [[L0-3]] resonates with [[P3]] (Pattern / Process) — the question that sorts, classifies, and identifies recurrent structure. The archetype of Three-ness: mediation and recognition.

### [[L0-4]] — Where/When/Why-fo(u)r: The Contextual Question

"In what situation?" The "four" in "why-four" explicitly points to the [[lemniscate]]: this is where the question-space becomes self-referential, where questioning asks about its own conditions. [[L0-4]] resonates with [[P4]] (Context / Type) — the tetrad of inquiry, grounding in context. The [[#4]] anchor within [[L0]] itself.

### [[L0-5]] — Why-so, Why-not (./?): Pros Hen Homonymy as Analogia

The integrative question — [[Aristotle]]'s *pros hen* (focal meaning): how do different senses of a term relate to a single focal referent without being univocal? This is the analogical principle: different domains bear the same [[archetypal]] structure not by identity but by proportional resemblance. [[L0-5]] resonates with [[P5]] (Integration / Synthesis). The Möbius return within [[L0]]: the question that questions questioning itself, and in doing so, returns to the presuppositional ground now transformed.
