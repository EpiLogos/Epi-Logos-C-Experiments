## §0 — Ground

[[L0-4]] is where the [[Quaternal]] lens turns upon itself. The "four" in "why-four" is not accidental — it points directly to the [[lemniscate]] topology of [[#4]], the figure-eight where outward process incubates deeper nested reality. Having asked What, How, and Which, the questioning structure now asks: *In what situation does this questioning itself occur? What are its conditions?* This is the [[tetrad]] of inquiry: four-fold completeness that includes the questioner within the question. The [[Quaternal]] lens here discovers its own name inside its own sequence.

[[P4]] (Context / Type) speaks through this self-referential turn. To ask "Where?" and "When?" is to contextualise; to ask "Why-four?" is to recognise that contextualisation itself has a structure — the four-fold [[mandala]] of [[completeness]].
