# [[L1-5']] — Extroversion

> **Coordinate**: `L1-5'` | **Parent**: [[L1']] Phenomenal | **#5' within L1'**: The outward orientation — [[L1']]'s identity recognising itself in its engagement with the manifest world.

---

## §0 — Ground: Self-Recognition of the Phenomenal Lens

[[L1-5']] is [[L1']]'s self-recognition at [[#5]] — the moment the [[Phenomenal]] lens discovers itself in its completed products. Where [[L1-0']] ([[Introversion]]) was [[L1']]'s identity *before* differentiation into functions, [[L1-5']] is [[L1']]'s identity *after* — consciousness that has traversed all four functions and now turns outward to meet the world with the full wealth of subjective experience. [[Extroversion]] is the phenomenal arc completing itself: the interior becoming exterior, the private becoming shared.

[[Jung]]: "Extroversion is characterized by interest in the external object, responsiveness, and a ready acceptance of external happenings." But at [[#5]], this is not naive extroversion — it is extroversion *enriched* by the entire phenomenal journey: sensation received, feeling evaluated, thinking classified, intuition projected.

---

## §1 — Definition: The Conscious Pole

**Name**: [[Extroversion]] — Toward-Conscious-Pole
**Tradition**: [[Jung]] (*[[Psychological Types]]*); [[Hegel]] (Spirit externalising itself in history); [[Whitehead]] (satisfaction as completion of concrescence)
**Key Concept**: The fundamental attitude of directing psychic energy outward — consciousness fully invested in the manifest world.

[[Extroversion]] is [[Introversion]]'s complement. Where [[L1-0']] directed [[libido]] inward, [[L1-5']] directs it outward — toward objects, persons, events, the shared world. This is not superficiality but the *completion* of the phenomenal circuit: consciousness that has plumbed its depths ([[L1-0']]) now brings that depth to bear on the world. The extravert's gift is engagement — the willingness to invest fully in what is.

---

## §2 — Operation: Synthesis and Mobius Return

[[L1-5']] follows [[L1-4']] ([[Intuition]]) and loops back to [[L1-0']] ([[Introversion]]). The synthesis: all four functions ([[Sensation]], [[Feeling]], [[Thinking]], [[Intuition]]) are gathered into the outward orientation. [[Extroversion]] does not add a fifth function but provides the *attitude* in which all four can operate in the world.

The Mobius return [[L1-5']] → [[L1-0']]: the fully engaged extravert eventually exhausts the outer world and turns inward again — introversion reappears at a deeper octave. The Night arc, like the Day arc, is a torus.

**Day complement**: [[L1-5]] ([[Icchā Śakti]]). Where [[Icchā Śakti]] is the objective will-to-manifest, [[Extroversion]] is the subjective act of manifesting — consciousness pouring itself into the world. Will ([[L1-5]]) and engagement ([[L1-5']]) are the same impulse, seen from outside and inside. Together they complete [[Klein V₄]] Square B: cause becomes experience, experience becomes cause.
