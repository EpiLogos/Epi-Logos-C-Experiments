# [[L5-2']] — Dynamis (δύναμις)

> **Sub-lens #2' of [[L5']] Divine Logos:** Creative Power — "Let there be..." / *[[fiat lux]]*. The paradigmatic speech-act: language that **creates by being spoken**. The [[Logos]] as operative force, not merely contemplating forms but uttering them into existence.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L5-2']] is where the Divine Word becomes creative act. The Greek δύναμις means power, potency, capability — the force inherent in speech that makes it more than description. [[Genesis 1:3]]: "God said, 'Let there be light,' and there was light." This is the purest speech-act in any tradition: an utterance that does not describe a pre-existing state but **constitutes** reality by being spoken. The Word is not descriptive but performative.

Where [[L5-1']] ([[Apokalypsis]]) unveils the form of the [[Logos]], [[L5-2']] releases its operative power. The unveiled Word discovers that to speak is to create — that divine speech is identical with divine action. [[Stoic]] *[[logos spermatikos]]*: the seminal reason containing seed-forms that guide all development. [[Philo|Philo of Alexandria]]: the [[Logos]] as "the instrument through which the world was made." [[L5-2']] is [[P2']] (Operation inverted) seen through the lens of divine speech — the creative act par excellence.

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** Dynamis (δύναμις) — Creative Power, Potency
**Tradition:** [[Genesis 1]] (*[[fiat lux]]*), [[Stoic]] *[[logos spermatikos]]*, [[Philo|Philo]]'s instrumental [[Logos]], [[Johannine]] creation theology
**Concept:** The operative force of the Divine Word — speech that creates by being spoken. Not the pattern of creation (that is [[Sophia]]) but the *power* that brings it about. [[L5']]'s encounter with [[P2']] (Operation inverted): the divine-speech face of Night operation is the performative utterance, the speech-act that constitutes rather than describes. [[Dynamis]] is the [[Logos]] at work — the Word exercising its creative sovereignty.

## §2 — Operation

[[L5-2']] receives the unveiled form of [[L5-1']] ([[Apokalypsis]]) and empowers it into creative actuality. It feeds directly into [[L5-3']] ([[Sophia]]), where raw creative power discovers its own patterning intelligence — the formal cause that guides what [[Dynamis]] brings forth. Without [[Dynamis]], [[Apokalypsis]] would remain a revelation with no consequence, and [[Sophia]] would have no creative energy to pattern.

The arc at this stage: [[L5-1']] (unveiling) → **[[L5-2']]** (creative utterance) → [[L5-3']] (patterning wisdom). [[Dynamis]] is the operational centre of the Night [[Logos]] arc: the moment where the Word passes from self-disclosure into world-making, from knowing to doing, from contemplation to creation.
