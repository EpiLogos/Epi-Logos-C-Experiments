# [[L2-3']] — Air (Sublimatio / The Volatile Principle)

> **Coordinate**: `L2-3'` | **Parent**: [[L2']] Alchemical-Elemental | **#3' within L2'**: The rising principle -- thought, breath, ideation. What ascends when the dissolved material is heated, separating the subtle from the gross.

---

## §0 — Ground: Elevation as Pattern

[[L2-3']] is the element of ascent. Where [[Water]] ([[L2-2']]) dissolves downward into the depths, [[Air]] rises -- carrying with it the volatile essences freed by dissolution. The alchemical [[sublimatio]] is the operation of purification through elevation: heating the dissolved material until the subtle separates from the gross and ascends as vapor. What rises is the *spiritus* -- the breath, the pneuma, the subtle carrier of meaning.

Psychologically, [[Air]] is the capacity to *rise above* immediate identification with contents -- to gain perspective, to see patterns, to think. Where [[Water]]/[[Feeling]] immerses, [[Air]]/[[Thinking]] elevates. This is not dissociation but genuine [[sublimatio]]: the hard-won capacity to discern form within flux after the dissolution has done its work. [[Air]] at [[#3]] is the elemental face of Pattern: thought that recognises structure, breath that connects, the volatile principle that carries essences between domains.

## §2 — Operation: The Sublimating Phase of the Elemental Arc

[[L2-3']] receives the dissolved contents of [[L2-2']] ([[Water]]) and subjects them to elevation. The [[sublimatio]] separates: what can rise, rises; what remains sinks back. This is the discriminating operation -- not everything that was dissolved is worth carrying forward. [[Air]] selects the subtle, the essential, the pattern-bearing. It then yields to [[L2-4']] ([[Fire]]/[[calcinatio]]), where the sublimated vapor encounters transformative heat. The transition from [[Air]] to [[Fire]] is the passage from *discerning* patterns to *being transformed by* them.

The arc: [[L2-0']] (undifferentiated) → [[L2-1']] (fixed) → [[L2-2']] (dissolved) → **[[L2-3']]** (sublimated) → [[L2-4']] (ignited) → [[L2-5']] (crystallised). At the midpoint [[#3]], [[Air]] is the pivot between descent (into matter via [[Earth]] and [[Water]]) and ascent (toward spirit via [[Fire]] and [[Salt]]).
