# [[S5]]: Notion (Integration/Synthesis) - Synthesis / Reflection

*The holographic reflection of [[QL]] [[Position]] [[#5]] onto the technological infrastructure.*

## Overview

[[S5]] represents the Notion layer - the integration and synthesis layer of the stack. It operates as Synthesis/Dashboard ([[bimba]] role) and Reflection/Viewer ([[Pratibimba]] role).

## [[S5]] Layer Functions

[[S5]] serves as the meta-reflection layer where:

1. **Synthesis**: Aggregates outputs from all lower layers ([[S0]]-[[S4]])
2. **Dashboard**: Presents unified views across the entire stack
3. **Reflection**: Enables meta-recursive awareness of system state
4. **Viewer**: Provides human-readable interfaces to complex data
