## Essence

[[P4']] is the **night face of context** — the discovery of contexts, sources, and situational frames that were outside the Day horizon. Where [[P4]] situates the known pattern in its full contextual field (Day / Integral), [[P4']] asks: *What situational sources were I not seeing? What contexts inform this pattern that I hadn't considered?*

In the Klein traversal, [[P4']] is the fifth step of Phase B: the system has integrated its ground, form, operation, and pattern — now it encounters the full contextual richness it missed or suppressed during the Day arc. Sources emerge that could not have been visible from the Day perspective.

**Vault mapping**: [[P4']] corresponds to `/Thought/Discovery/` — books, papers, videos, references, source materials that expand the contextual horizon. A discovery is precisely what was outside the known context but now enters it: a new situational frame, an informing source.
