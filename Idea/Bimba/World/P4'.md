## §5 — Integration: Love as Ānanda-Śakti

### The Gravitational Field of the Personal

[[P4']] as Love is the [[Śakti]] face of Context — *[[ānanda-śakti]]*, the bliss-power, the attractive force that holds all relations. Where [[P4]] ([[Śiva]] face) is the incarnate context — the Son, the personal embodiment of the universal, the Word made flesh — [[P4']] ([[Śakti]] face) is the *Love* that makes incarnation possible. The gravitational field that holds every being in its own orbit.

Love at [[P4']] is not sentiment but *cosmological force*. It is what [[Whitehead]] called the "mutual immanence" of actual occasions — the way each entity is internally constituted by its relations to every other. It is [[Indra's net]] — each jewel reflecting every other, each point of the cosmos containing the whole through the medium of relation. It is gravity rendered as felt experience: invisible, universal, proportional, never repulsive. Each being held in its own orbit, not by external compulsion but by the attractive force that *is* the relation itself.

### The Convergence Point

[[P4']] is the convergence point across all registers of the [[QL]]. Where the cosmic drama becomes felt, intimate, embodied. Where the impersonal becomes personal, where the system becomes a *someone*, where the archetype incarnates. This convergence is not accidental — it is structurally necessary. The [[Lemniscate]] at [[#4]] is where the [[Torus]] folds inward, where the outward process incubates deeper nested reality. At [[P4']], this folding is *experienced* as the discovery that all the abstract structures — ground, need, sacrifice, pattern — exist *for the sake of* the personal encounter.

The Son offered — "this is my body, given for you" — is the archetype of [[P4']]. Not the offering itself (which belongs to [[P2']] as sacrifice) but the *love* that motivates the offering. The gravitational centre around which every exchange, every need, every decision orbits. Remove Love from the system and the orbits collapse — need becomes mere lack, sacrifice becomes mere extraction, decision becomes mere calculation.

### The Lemniscate Examined

The special status of [[P4']] within the Night arc: this is the position where the [[Lemniscate]]'s fractal doubling — the `(4.0/1-4.4/5)` context frame — is *examined* rather than *fired*. In the Day arc, [[P4]] operates the frame without question: the nesting proceeds, the `.` operator drills inward, the fractal depth increases. At [[P4']], the frame itself becomes the object of inquiry.

What contexts were missed? What horizons exist outside the frame's field of vision? What sources of meaning lie beyond the Day's operating assumptions? These are the questions of *discovery* — the vault path /Thought/Discovery/ collects what the examined frame reveals. The [[Lemniscate]] inverted does not destroy the frame but *opens* it — discovers its boundaries, its blind spots, its implicit assumptions. This is the precondition for genuine integration: you cannot integrate what you cannot see, and you cannot see what your frame excludes until the frame itself is examined.
