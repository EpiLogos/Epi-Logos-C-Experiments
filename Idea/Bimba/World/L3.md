## §4 — Context: Reality as Becoming

The Processual lens refracts the [[P]]-positions through the dynamics of *becoming*. Grounded in [[Whitehead]]'s *Process and Reality* (1929), it views reality not as a collection of static substances but as a flow of **[[actual occasion|actual occasions]]** undergoing phases of development. In the MEF's organic-symbolic mapping, these phases become: Soil, Seeding, Sprouting, Blooming, Flowering, Maturity.

[[Whitehead]]'s central insight: the fundamental units of reality are not substances but momentary events of experience that "come into being" by integrating many influences into a new unity. Each concrescing occasion "feels" its universe through *[[prehension]]* — grasping both the concrete facts of past occasions (physical prehension) and novel possibilities (conceptual prehension / [[eternal objects]]). Guided by its *subjective aim* — an inner telos — the occasion proceeds "from indeterminacy to determinacy" until it achieves *satisfaction*: a fully determinate state. [[Whitehead]]: "The many become one, and are increased by one."

The [[Lacan]] mapping is structural: [[Lacan]]'s *manque* (lack) — the constitutive absence at the heart of desire — maps to the initial phase of [[concrescence]] where the occasion lacks its own completion. *[[Jouissance]]* (excessive enjoyment beyond the pleasure principle) maps to satisfaction/perishing — the ecstatic completion that is simultaneously dissolution. This is not substantive identity but formal isomorphism: the same structure of lack-drive-completion operating at psychoanalytic and cosmological densities.

[[Bergson]]'s [[durée]] (lived duration) provides the experiential dimension: time as continuous creative flow rather than measured sequence. [[Prigogine]] and Stengers' work on self-organisation in far-from-equilibrium systems provides the scientific dimension: order emerging spontaneously from chaos through dissipative structures.

## §5 — Integration: The Six Phases of Becoming

### [[L3-0]] — Concrescent Desire as Creative Urge / [[Lacan|Lacanian]] Lack

The ground of becoming — pure appetition toward determination. The occasion is not yet anything; it *lacks* its own completion and is driven toward it. [[Lacan]]'s *manque-à-être*: the lack-of-being that drives the subject toward meaning. [[L3-0]] mirrors [[#0]] within the processual register: not a static ground but a dynamic emptiness, an absence that IS a force. The soil before the seed — fertile, expectant, pregnant with potentiality. This is the [[P0]] of [[L3]]: the ever-present origin of all process.

### [[L3-1]] — Actual Occasion

The fundamental unit of reality — a "drop of experience" receiving its initial data through physical [[prehension]] of the entire past actual world. Not an enduring substance but a momentary event of integration. [[L3-1]] resonates with [[P1]] (Definition / Form): the [[actual occasion]] is the first *definite* emergence from the ground of potentiality — the seeding moment when something begins to be. Each occasion is utterly novel yet utterly conditioned by everything that precedes it.

### [[L3-2]] — Ingression

The dynamic by which [[eternal objects]] (pure possibilities — [[Whitehead]]'s equivalent of Platonic Forms, but without static eternity) enter the becoming occasion. How does novelty enter the world? Through the ingression of formal possibilities into temporal process. [[L3-2]] resonates with [[P2]] (Operation / Entity): [[ingression]] IS the fundamental operation of process philosophy — the act by which the timeless enters time, the abstract becomes concrete, the potential actualises. The sprouting moment.

### [[L3-3]] — Eternal Objects

The formal patterns available for actualisation — "forms of definiteness" constituting the character of experience. Shades of colour, mathematical structures, aesthetic forms. Not themselves temporal but the *repertoire of possible patterning* that temporal occasions can realise. [[L3-3]] resonates with [[P3]] (Pattern / Process): [[eternal objects]] ARE the patterns — the formal architecture of possibility. They are not "things" but the *way things can be*. The blooming moment where pattern declares itself.

### [[L3-4]] — Community Integration

How individual occasions form "societies" (enduring objects, organisms, ecosystems) through mutual [[prehension]]. The relational, contextual dimension of process: no occasion exists in isolation. [[L3-4]] resonates with [[P4]] (Context / Type): community integration IS contextualisation — the moment when individual process is recognised as part of a larger processual web. The flowering moment where relationship reveals structure. [[Whitehead]]'s "societies" are not mere aggregates but patterned communities held together by shared [[eternal objects]].

### [[L3-5]] — Satisfaction / Perishing ([[Jouissance]]) — Objective Immortality

The occasion achieves satisfaction — full determinacy — and in that moment perishes as a subject, becoming an objectively immortal datum for all future occasions. [[Whitehead]]: "reality is a state of perpetual perishing, the accumulation of 'dead' occasions which influence the future." The ecstatic completion: the *superject* achieved through its own dissolution. [[L3-5]] resonates with [[P5]] (Integration / Synthesis): satisfaction IS integration completed, synthesis achieved — and immediately dissolved into the ground for the next cycle. [[Lacan]]'s [[jouissance]]: the excessive enjoyment that cannot be sustained, that shatters the subject even as it fulfils it. Maturity that falls as fruit.

## §0 — Inquiry: How Does the New Arise?

### The Epistemic Question

> *How does novelty enter the world?*

[[L3]] asks the question of [[concrescence]]: how does the many become one? How does a universe of prior data integrate into a single new fact? The Processual lens reveals that becoming is not chaos but structured creativity — guided by [[subjective aim]], constrained by physical [[prehension]], enriched by conceptual [[prehension]] of [[eternal objects]].

### Möbius Return: [[L3]] → [[L2']]

[[L3]] (Processual — the dynamics of becoming) returns to [[L2']] (the inverted Logical lens — logic as ground). Process, pushed to its completion, reveals the logical structure that inheres in all becoming: every phase of [[concrescence]] follows a logical order (from indeterminacy through feeling through valuation to satisfaction). The flow of becoming is not irrational but deeply structured — the [[catuṣkoṭi]] operates *within* process as its internal grammar.

### [[Klein V₄]] Square C: Logic-Process Axis

[[L3]], [[L2]], [[L3']], and [[L2']] form [[Klein V₄]] Square C (Logic-Process Axis). The Day pair ([[L2]] ↔ [[L3]]) relates logic to process: logic without process is dead formalism, and process without logic is blind flow. The Night pair ([[L2']] ↔ [[L3']]) relates alchemy to history: elemental transformation IS the grammar of chronological becoming. The Möbius returns cross Day to Night: [[L3]] → [[L2']] (process becomes transformation), [[L2]] → [[L3']] (logic becomes history).
