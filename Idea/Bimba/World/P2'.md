## Essence

[[P2']] is the **operational shadow** — where the smooth function of [[P2]] encounters its resistance, inversion, or deeper complexity. The Mythic Night faces the negative aspect of process: what blocks, what loops without resolution, what dynamic force works against the intended operation.

In depth psychology terms, this is the "shadow of the function" — the autonomous process that operates beneath or against the consciously directed operation. In the Klein traversal, [[P2']] is Phase B's operational step: the system is now integrating (not just diagnosing) the resistance it encountered during the Day arc.

**Vault mapping**: [[P2']] corresponds to `/Thought/Challenges/` — the blockers, obstacles, and difficulties that the operation produces or encounters. A challenge is the inverse face of operation: where [[P2]] is the function running, [[P2']] is the function failing, resisting, or requiring deeper work.
