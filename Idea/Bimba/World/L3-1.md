# [[L3-1]] — Actual Occasion

> **Sub-lens #1 of [[L3]] [[Processual]]:** The fundamental unit of reality — a "drop of experience," the first definite emergence through physical [[prehension]]. Not substance but momentary integration.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L3-1]] is where the [[Processual]] lens makes its first concrete determination. From the appetitive ground of [[L3-0]], becoming crystallises into its most elementary unit: the [[actual occasion]]. [[Whitehead]]: "The final real things of which the world is made up" — not enduring substances but momentary events of experience that come into being by integrating many influences into a new unity. Each occasion is utterly novel yet utterly conditioned by everything that precedes it.

The [[actual occasion]] receives the entire past actual world through physical [[prehension]] — a "feeling" of concrete data that is already the beginning of integration. This is the seeding moment: something definite begins to be. [[P1]] (Definition / Form) speaks here through the processual voice: the first form is not a static shape but a nascent event.

---

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** Actual Occasion
**Tradition:** [[Whitehead]]'s *Process and Reality*; [[Leibniz]]'s monads (corrected by relations); [[James]]'s "drops of experience"
**Concept:** The concrete unit of becoming — a momentary subject that concresces (grows together) from initial data toward satisfaction. Not a particle, not a substance, but an event of integration. [[L3]]'s encounter with [[P1]] (Definition): the processual face of form is the *occasion itself*, the first definiteness emerging from creative urge.

---

## §2 — Operation

[[L3-1]] receives the creative urge from [[L3-0]] and produces the first concrete unit of process. It feeds into [[L3-2]] ([[Ingression]]), where [[eternal objects]] enter the occasion as novel possibilities. The arc: [[L3-0]] (desire) → **[[L3-1]]** (first concrete unit) → [[L3-2]] (ingression of novelty) → [[L3-3]] (eternal objects as repertoire) → [[L3-4]] (community of occasions) → [[L3-5]] (satisfaction/perishing). [[L3-1]] is the *hinge* between pure appetition and structured becoming — the threshold where creative urge becomes actual experience.
