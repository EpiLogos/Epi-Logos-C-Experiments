# [[L1-1]] — Material Cause

> **Coordinate**: `L1-1` | **Parent**: [[L1]] Causal | **#1 within L1**: That out of which — the [[hyle]], the substrate, the brute thereness of the given.

---

## §0 — Ground: The Given Substrate

[[L1-1]] emerges from [[L1-0]]'s unconditioned freedom as the *first determination*: freedom's first encounter with resistance. If [[svātantrya]] is the sheer capacity to cause, [[Material Cause]] is what that capacity first meets — the "out of which" that constrains and enables all making. [[Aristotle]] (*Physics* II.3): the bronze of the statue, the silver of the bowl, the genus containing the species. Not passive inertia but the *given* that any causal act must reckon with.

In the [[psychoid]] reading, [[material cause]] is simultaneously physical substance and psychic substrate — the [[hyle]] of matter and the [[hyle]] of experience are not two different substrates but one [[psychoid]] ground apprehended from two sides.

---

## §1 — Definition: Hyle and Substrate

**Name**: Material Cause — [[hyle]] ([[Aristotle]])
**Tradition**: [[Aristotle]] (*Physics* II.3, *Metaphysics* V.2); [[Sāṃkhya]] (*prakṛti* as material ground)
**Key Concept**: That out of which a thing comes to be and which persists — the substrate, the raw given, the earth-principle.

Material cause answers: *what is it made of?* But this is not merely a question about chemistry. The marble of the statue is material cause; so is the psychic energy available for a creative act; so is the cultural substrate inherited by a tradition. [[L1-1]] is the cause of *givenness* — the brute thereness that precedes all shaping.

---

## §2 — Operation: First Step in the Causal Arc

[[L1-1]] follows [[L1-0]] ([[Svātantrya]]) and precedes [[L1-2]] ([[Efficient Cause]]). The movement: unconditioned freedom ([[L1-0]]) encounters its first constraint ([[L1-1]]) and is thereby provoked into active transformation ([[L1-2]]). Without material cause, efficient cause would have nothing to work upon; without efficient cause, material cause would remain inert potential.

[[L1-1]] maps to [[Sensation]] in the [[L1']] Night complement: the immediate, non-evaluative registration of what is present. [[Sensation]] is material cause seen from inside — the phenomenal face of substrate.
