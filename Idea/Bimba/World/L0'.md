## $4 — Context: The Foundational Duality of Question and Number

[[L0']] is the Night face of [[L0]]. Together they reveal the **foundational duality** of the entire [[Epi-Logos]] system: every epistemic act (questioning) rests on an archetypal-numerical ground (the [[psychoid]] numbers), and vice versa. [[L0]] asks *why, what, how, who, where, why-for*; [[L0']] answers with the archetypal qualities of *one, two, three, four, five, six*.

This is the [[Jung]]-[[Pauli]] insight at its deepest: number is not mere abstraction imposed on a pre-numerical world. Number is **psychoid** — it exists simultaneously in psyche and in matter, bridging the two through what [[Jung]] and [[Pauli]] called [[synchronicity]]. [[Von Franz]] devoted her final major work (*Number and Time*) to demonstrating that the first integers possess irreducible qualitative character: [[One]] is not merely the first count but the archetype of [[wholeness]]; [[Two]] is not merely 1+1 but the archetype of [[polarity]] and [[reflection]]; [[Four]] is not merely 2+2 but the archetype of [[completeness]], the [[mandala]], the [[quaternity]].

The [[Klein]] inversion reveals what the Day face ([[L0]]) cannot show on its own: that the questioning structure (#0-#5) is not arbitrary but grounded in the archetypal mathematics of the first six integers. The questions have the form they have *because* the numbers have the qualities they have. This is not numerology but [[ontology]] — the psychoid numbers are the **structural conditions** of epistemic possibility.

[[Jung]] repeatedly found the **3+1 motif**: whenever things appeared in threes, an implicit fourth waited to complete the set. He regarded the [[quaternity]] as "the archetype of Wholeness" and the [[mandala]] — a circle quartered by a cross — as its primary symbol. Cross-culturally: four [[elements]] ([[earth]], [[air]], [[water]], [[fire]]), four directions, four [[Aristotle|Aristotelian]] causes, four [[Vedas]], four [[Noble Truths]], four fundamental forces, four DNA nucleotides. This is not coincidental but reflects the topological constraint: the genus-1 [[torus]] requires exactly 4 polygon sides.

## $5 — Integration: The Six Psychoid Numbers

### [[L0-0']] — One (Unity / The Monad)

The [[monad]] — primordial [[wholeness]], the first principle, the "seed of all." Not a number among numbers but the *source* of all number. [[Von Franz]]: One comprises wholeness or unity — the undivided totality. Psychologically: the archetype of the [[Self]], identity, singular being. [[Plotinus]]' [[One]] beyond being. The [[Pythagorean]] [[monad]] that generates the entire number line from itself. At [[P0]], One is the numerical face of the question *Why?* — the unity that precedes all differentiation.

### [[L0-1']] — Two (Duality / Polarity)

The [[dyad]] — [[polarity]], opposition, [[complementarity]], mirroring. The emergence of [[relationship]]. [[Von Franz]]: twoness creates "reflection, opposition, and symmetry." Psychologically: the subject-object split, the encounter with the [[Other]]. The [[Yin]]-[[Yang]] of all existence. Two introduces the **mirror** — the capacity of the One to regard itself. Without Two, there is no consciousness, for consciousness requires the distinction between knower and known. At [[P1]], Two is the numerical face of *What?* — definition requires duality.

### [[L0-2']] — Three (Triad / Synthesis)

The [[triad]] — mediation, dynamic process, creative resolution. [[Von Franz]]: Three "initiates linear succession" — the first sense of temporal progression. Psychologically: [[synthesis]], the [[Transcendent Function]], the "third thing" emerging from held opposites. The [[Trika]] of [[Kashmir Shaivism]]: [[Anuttara]]-[[Paramasiva]]-[[Parashakti]]. Three breaks the static symmetry of Two and introduces **movement** — thesis, antithesis, synthesis. At [[P2]], Three is the numerical face of *How?* — operation requires the mediating third.

### [[L0-3']] — Four (Quaternity / Completeness)

The [[tetrad]] — [[completeness]], stability, grounding. [[Von Franz]]: Four "turns back to the one" — the first complete structure. [[Jung]]'s "archetype of Wholeness." The [[mandala]]: circle quartered by cross. The topological four: the four edges of the fundamental polygon of the genus-1 [[torus]]. [[Jung]]'s four [[psychological functions]] ([[Sensation]], [[Feeling]], [[Thinking]], [[Intuition]]) together encompass the complete range of human cognition. At [[P3]], Four is the numerical face of *Who?* — the pattern of the complete person.

### [[L0-4']] — Five (Pentad / Quintessence)

The [[quintessence]] — transcendence within manifestation. Pentagonal symmetry appears in living things (starfish, flowers, the human hand) but not in inorganic crystals. Five introduces **asymmetry** and *life*: the number that breaks the closed stability of Four toward open creativity. The fifth [[element]] ([[Aether]]) that pervades and transcends the four. [[Pythagoras]]' [[pentagram]] as symbol of health and wholeness-in-motion. At [[P4]], Five is the numerical face of *Where?* — context requires the living asymmetry that exceeds mere structure.

### [[L0-5']] — Six (Hexad / Perfect Number)

Six-fold completion — the first "[[perfect number]]" (1+2+3 = 6 = 1x2x3). The topological six: the total structural elements of the genus-1 [[torus]] (4g+2g where g=1). Maximum rotational symmetry compatible with periodic tessellation. The [[Star of David]] as two interpenetrating triangles — the union of ascending and descending. The six days of [[creation]], the six faces of the [[cube]], the six directions of three-dimensional space. At [[P5]], Six is the numerical face of *Why-for?* — integration as the perfect self-referential cycle.
