## Essence

[[P1']] is the **analytic face of form** — the structural interrogation of what any definition actually contains. Where [[P1]] gives shape and names (Day / Magic), [[P1']] asks what remains beneath the named form: What evidence of form persists? What is the material that resists or exceeds the definition?

In the Klein traversal, [[P1']] is the second step of Phase B (Inward Arc / Statement `.`). The form defined at [[P1]] has now passed through the full Day arc (P1→P2→P3→P4→P5) and the Möbius Twist. It re-enters as [[P1']] — the same form, but now seen from inside the Klein surface. The definition is not dissolved but deepened: its material cause is examined, its structural conditions laid bare.

**Vault mapping**: [[P1']] corresponds to `/Thought/Traces/` — the traces, transcripts, and evidence of what has occurred. A trace is the residue of form: not the form itself (P1) but the material remainder after the form has passed.
