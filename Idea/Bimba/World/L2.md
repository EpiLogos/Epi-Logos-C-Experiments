## §4 — Context: The Complete Space of Logic

The Logical lens refracts the [[P]]-positions through the complete space of logical possibility. Grounded in [[Nāgārjuna]]'s [[catuṣkoṭi]] (the four-cornered negation from the *[[Mūlamadhyamakakārikā]]*, c. 150 CE), it demonstrates that complete logical space requires not two positions ([[Aristotle|Aristotelian]] excluded middle) or three ([[Hegel|Hegelian]] dialectic) but *four* — plus a fifth position of silence where logic encounters its own limit.

The lens's reach is extensive: from [[Nāgārjuna]] and [[Jain]] *[[syādvāda]]* (seven-fold "maybe" logic), through [[Belnap]]'s four-valued logic (True, False, Both, Neither), [[paraconsistent logic|paraconsistent logics]] that reject the explosion principle, [[intuitionistic logic]]'s rejection of excluded middle, to [[Wittgenstein]]'s "Whereof one cannot speak, thereof one must be silent." The lens also connects to computational paradigms: non-monotonic reasoning, belief revision, [[active inference]] — all of which rediscover the ancient wisdom of flexible, multi-valued logic in formal guise.

The lens asserts that "Logic is not one-dimensional... Contradiction, ambiguity, and context are not flaws to be eliminated from reasoning, but essential features of a *living, evolving logic* — a logic *of becoming*." The goal is not to abandon reason but to fulfil reason's highest calling: to lead to understanding in whatever form it must take.

## §5 — Integration: The Six Logical Positions

### [[L2-0]] — Tetralemmaic Ground (./?)/(?/.)

Pre-logical superposition — the condition before any of the four positions is occupied. The question-space from which logical determination emerges. Analogous to the quantum state before measurement collapses it. [[L2-0]] mirrors [[#0]] in the logical register: the sheer openness that precedes affirmation, negation, their conjunction, and their disjunction. The ground of [[śūnyatā]] — not nothing, but the emptiness of fixed determination.

### [[L2-1]] — IS: Being, Assertion, Truth

The cataphatic affirmation — what can be positively asserted. The realm of verified fact, definite existence, logical truth. [[L2-1]] resonates with [[P1]] (Definition / Form): to say "IS" is to define, to draw the first boundary, to affirm identity. This is the position of classical logic, [[Parmenides]]' "it is and cannot not be," the domain where the law of identity holds without qualification.

### [[L2-2]] — IS-NOT: Non-being, Negation, Falsity

The apophatic negation — what can be definitively denied. The boundary-drawing function: definition by exclusion. [[L2-2]] resonates with [[P2]] (Operation / Entity): negation IS an operation — the act of distinguishing, cutting, separating. Without IS-NOT, IS would have no contour. Every definition depends on what it excludes. The [[via negativa]] of [[Pseudo-Dionysius]], the *neti neti* of [[Advaita Vedānta]].

### [[L2-3]] — BOTH: IS and IS-NOT — Contradiction, Dialetheism

The paradoxical position where contradictions genuinely coexist. "In [[Hegel]]'s view, contradictions are the means through which higher truths emerge." Not logical failure but the recognition that some realities exhibit contradictory properties simultaneously. Quantum superposition, wave-particle duality, the liar's paradox. [[L2-3]] resonates with [[P3]] (Pattern / Process): the BOTH position reveals the *pattern* that contradiction carries — not noise but signal, not error but deeper structure. [[Dialetheism]] (the philosophical position that some contradictions are true) finds its formal home here.

### [[L2-4]] — NEITHER: IS nor IS-NOT — Void, Ineffability

Transcendence of the binary framework entirely. [[Nāgārjuna]]'s [[śūnyatā]] — not mere nothingness but the emptiness of inherent existence in any conceptual determination. The recognition that reality is not capturable by any combination of affirmation and negation. [[Wittgenstein]]'s domain of "meaningful silence beyond logical language." [[L2-4]] resonates with [[P4]] (Context / Type): the NEITHER position IS the context that reveals the limits of all logical positions — the [[lemniscate]] fold where logic turns inward and encounters its own ground.

### [[L2-5]] — SILENCE: Irrepresentable / Unprovable Truth

The fifth position beyond the four — the [[Buddha]]'s silence on the *[[avyākata]]* (undeclared questions), [[Gödel]]'s unprovable truths, [[Wittgenstein]]'s silence. The recognition that the most fundamental truths may be precisely those that cannot be stated propositionally. "Pregnant silence" that is not absence of meaning but superabundance of it. [[L2-5]] resonates with [[P5]] (Integration / Synthesis): SILENCE integrates all four logical positions by transcending their common assumption — that truth must be stateable. The Möbius return: logic, pushed to its limit, becomes mystical.

## §0 — Inquiry: What Can Be Truly Said?

### The Epistemic Question

> *What is the complete space of logical possibility?*

[[L2]] asks not merely whether something is true or false but what the *structure of truth-bearing* itself looks like. The [[catuṣkoṭi]] reveals that binary logic is a special case — useful but incomplete. The full logical space includes affirmation, negation, their paradoxical conjunction, their transcendent disjunction, and the silence that frames them all.

### Möbius Return: [[L2]] → [[L3']]

[[L2]] (Logical — the structure of truth) returns to [[L3']] (the inverted [[Processual]] lens — becoming as ground). Logic, carried to its completion, reveals that truth is not a static property but a *processual* event. The [[catuṣkoṭi]] is not a fixed grid but a dynamic unfolding: IS gives way to IS-NOT gives way to BOTH gives way to NEITHER gives way to SILENCE — and silence gives way to a new IS. Truth *happens*.

[[L2]], [[L3]], [[L2']], and [[L3']] form [[Klein V₄]] Square C (Logic-Process Axis). The Day pair ([[L2]] ↔ [[L3]]) relates logic to process: every logical determination is an event, every process has logical form. The Night pair ([[L2']] ↔ [[L3']]) relates alchemy to history: elemental transformation IS the grammar of chronological becoming. The Möbius returns cross Day to Night: [[L2]] → [[L3']] (logic becomes history), [[L3]] → [[L2']] (process becomes transformation).
