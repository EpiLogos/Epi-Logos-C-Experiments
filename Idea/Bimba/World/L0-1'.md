## §0 — Ground

[[L0-1']] is the first breach in the [[monad]]'s wholeness. [[Two]] introduces the mirror — the capacity of the One to regard itself, and in regarding, to split. [[Von Franz]]: twoness creates "reflection, opposition, and symmetry." Without Two, there is no [[consciousness]], for consciousness requires the distinction between knower and known. The [[dyad]] is the archetype of the [[Other]], of the [[Yin]]-[[Yang]] fundamental to all existence.
