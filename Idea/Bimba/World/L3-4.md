# [[L3-4]] — Community Integration

> **Sub-lens #4 of [[L3]] [[Processual]]:** How occasions form "societies" through mutual [[prehension]]. The relational, contextual dimension of process — no occasion in isolation. The [[Lemniscate]] fold.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L3-4]] is where the [[Processual]] lens encounters *context* — the discovery that no [[actual occasion]] exists alone. [[Whitehead]]'s "societies" are not mere aggregates but patterned communities of occasions held together by shared [[eternal objects]] — common forms of definiteness that thread through successive occasions like a signature. An electron, an organism, a civilisation: all are societies in this sense, sustained by the mutual [[prehension]] of their members.

This is the flowering moment where relationship reveals structure. The [[Lemniscate]] topology ([[#4]]) is active here: the system folds inward, and what appeared as a linear sequence of discrete occasions reveals itself as a web of mutual conditioning. [[Prigogine]]'s self-organising systems: order sustained far from equilibrium by the continuous flow of energy and information through relational networks.

---

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** Community Integration
**Tradition:** [[Whitehead]]'s societies and nexus; [[Prigogine]]'s self-organisation; [[Maturana]] and [[Varela]]'s autopoiesis; [[Lacan]]'s social link
**Concept:** The contextual dimension of process — how individual occasions form enduring structures through mutual [[prehension]]. [[L3]]'s encounter with [[P4]] (Context / Type): the processual face of context is community, the relational web that gives individual occasions their wider significance. No occasion exists in isolation; each is constituted by its relations.

## §2 — Operation

[[L3-4]] receives the formal repertoire from [[L3-3]] and shows how patterns become the *bonds* of community — shared [[eternal objects]] threading through successive occasions to form enduring structures. It feeds into [[L3-5]] (Satisfaction / Perishing), where the community itself achieves a kind of completion. The arc: [[L3-0]] (desire) → [[L3-1]] (occasion) → [[L3-2]] (ingression) → [[L3-3]] (eternal objects) → **[[L3-4]]** (community) → [[L3-5]] (satisfaction). [[L3-4]] is the *contextual* moment: the individual occasion discovers it was never individual at all, but always already a member of prehensive communities.
