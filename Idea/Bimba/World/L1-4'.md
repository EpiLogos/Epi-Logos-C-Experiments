# [[L1-4']] — Intuition

> **Coordinate**: `L1-4'` | **Parent**: [[L1']] Phenomenal | **#4' within L1'**: Perception of possibilities — the irrational function that grasps hidden connections and future developments.

---

## §0 — Ground: The Field of Potential

[[L1-4']] emerges from [[L1-3']]'s logical classification as the perception of *what else could be*. [[Thinking]] determines what something IS; [[Intuition]] perceives what it COULD BECOME. This is the second irrational (non-judging) function, paired with [[Sensation]] ([[L1-1']]): where [[Sensation]] perceives what is present, [[Intuition]] perceives what is *possible*. The penumbra of meaning surrounding every fact — the unseen connections, the latent trajectories, the not-yet-manifest.

[[Jung]]: "Intuition is not mere perception, or vision, but an active, creative process that puts into the object just as much as it takes out." At [[#4]], the [[Lemniscate]] position, [[Intuition]] occupies the fold-point where the phenomenal arc turns inward — sensing the deeper pattern that connects all four functions.

---

## §1 — Definition: Perception of Possibilities

**Name**: [[Intuition]] — Perception of Possibilities
**Tradition**: [[Jung]] (*[[Psychological Types]]*); [[Bergson]] (*intuition* as direct apprehension of duration); [[Schelling]] (intellectual intuition)
**Key Concept**: The irrational (non-judging) function that perceives hidden connections, future developments, and the field of potential surrounding every actuality.

[[Intuition]] does not analyse what is present but apprehends what is *possible*. The entrepreneur who "sees" the opportunity before the market does; the therapist who senses the unspoken meaning behind the patient's words; the scientist whose "hunch" precedes the proof — all operate through [[Intuition]]. [[Jung]]: "The intuitive is never to be found in the world of accepted reality-values, but he has a keen nose for anything new and in the making."

---

## §2 — Operation: The Lemniscate Fold

[[L1-4']] follows [[L1-3']] ([[Thinking]]) and precedes [[L1-5']] ([[Extroversion]]). At the [[#4]] position, it occupies the [[Lemniscate]] anchor — the point where the phenomenal arc folds inward before completing in [[Extroversion]]. [[Intuition]] and [[Sensation]] ([[L1-1']]) form the **irrational pair**: both perceive rather than judge, but [[Sensation]] perceives what IS and [[Intuition]] perceives what COULD BE. They are complementary opposites.

**Day complement**: [[L1-4]] ([[Final Cause]]). [[Telos]] is the objective directionality of things; [[Intuition]] is the subjective capacity to *sense* that directionality. Purpose ([[L1-4]]) is what things are aimed at; [[Intuition]] ([[L1-4']]) is how we perceive that aim from inside. The [[Jung|Jungian]] intuitive type is precisely the person who grasps teleological patterns that the sensation type misses.
