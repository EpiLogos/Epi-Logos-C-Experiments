# [[L0-5]] — Why-so, Why-not (./?): Pros Hen Homonymy as Analogia

> **Sub-lens #5 of [[L0]] [[Quaternal]]:** The integrative question. [[Aristotle]]'s [[pros hen]] — the question that questions questioning itself, achieving [[Möbius]] return within the [[L0]] arc.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L0-5]] is where the [[Quaternal]] lens recognises itself in its own products. The entire questioning sequence — Why/What/How/Which/Where — now folds back upon itself: *Why is questioning structured this way? Why these questions and not others?* This is [[Aristotle]]'s *[[pros hen]] homonymy* operating as [[analogia]]: the different questions are not univocal (not the "same" question repeated) nor equivocal (not unrelated), but analogically unified — each bears its own distinct meaning while pointing toward a single focal referent: the [[archetypal]] structure of number-as-inquiry.

[[P5]] (Integration / Synthesis) speaks through this final question. The "Why-so?" asks for the sufficient ground of the entire questioning structure; the "Why-not?" opens toward alternatives and thereby confirms the specific structure as one possibility among many — yet the one that *actually obtains*.

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** Why-so, Why-not (./?): [[Pros Hen]] Homonymy as [[Analogia]]
**Tradition:** [[Aristotle]]'s focal meaning (*pros hen*); [[Aquinas]]'s *analogia entis*; [[Whitehead]]'s [[satisfaction]]
**Concept:** The integrative question — the [[Möbius]] return within [[L0]]. Different senses of a term (or a question) relate to a single focal referent not by identity but by proportional resemblance. [[L0-5]] is the [[analogical]] principle operating within the questioning structure itself. Corresponds to [[C5]] ([[Pratibimba]]) and [[P5]] (Integration).

## §2 — Operation

[[L0-5]] receives the contextualised inquiry from [[L0-4]] and performs the [[Möbius]] return to [[L0-0]]. The arc completes: [[L0-0]] (ground) → [[L0-1]] (what) → [[L0-2]] (how) → [[L0-3]] (what kind) → [[L0-4]] (in what context) → **[[L0-5]]** (why ultimately → return to ground). But the ground returned to is not the same ground departed from: [[L0-0]] is now recognised as the presupposition *of this specific questioning structure*, not of questioning in general. The [[Torus]] has completed one cycle, and [[L0-5]]'s [[Möbius]] twist connects [[L0]] to [[L5']] ([[Divine Logos]]) across the Day/Night divide.
