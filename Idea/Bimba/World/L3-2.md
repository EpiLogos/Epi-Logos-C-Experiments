# [[L3-2]] — Ingression

> **Sub-lens #2 of [[L3]] [[Processual]]:** The dynamic bridge between possibility and actuality — how [[eternal objects]] enter becoming occasions. How novelty enters the world.

---

## §0 — Ground

[[L3-2]] is where the [[Processual]] lens encounters its fundamental *operation*. [[Ingression]] is [[Whitehead]]'s term for the act by which [[eternal objects]] (pure possibilities, forms of definiteness) participate in the becoming of an [[actual occasion]]. This is the sprouting moment: the timeless enters time, the abstract becomes concrete, the potential actualises. Without [[ingression]], occasions would merely repeat the past; with it, genuine novelty becomes possible.

[[Bergson]]'s creative evolution speaks here: life is not the rearrangement of pre-existing parts but the continuous creation of genuinely new forms. [[Prigogine]]'s bifurcation points: moments where the system's trajectory shifts irreversibly, where new order crystallises from instability.

---

## §1 — Definition

**Name:** Ingression
**Tradition:** [[Whitehead]]'s conceptual [[prehension]]; [[Bergson]]'s creative evolution; [[Prigogine]]'s bifurcation; [[Deleuze]]'s actualisation of the virtual
**Concept:** The operation by which formal possibility enters temporal process — the dynamic bridge between the repertoire of [[eternal objects]] and the concreteness of each [[actual occasion]]. [[L3]]'s encounter with [[P2]] (Operation / Entity): [[ingression]] IS the fundamental operation of process philosophy, the act that makes becoming genuinely creative rather than merely repetitive.

---

## §2 — Operation

[[L3-2]] receives the concrete occasion from [[L3-1]] and introduces the dimension of novelty. It feeds into [[L3-3]] ([[Eternal Objects]]), which provides the full repertoire of possibilities that [[ingression]] draws upon. The arc: [[L3-0]] (desire) → [[L3-1]] (occasion) → **[[L3-2]]** (ingression of novelty) → [[L3-3]] (repertoire of forms) → [[L3-4]] (community) → [[L3-5]] (satisfaction). [[L3-2]] is the crucial *operational* moment: without it, the process has no mechanism for introducing the genuinely new. It is guided by what [[Whitehead]] calls the *subjective aim* — the occasion's own telos, its lure toward the best possible integration.
