### §3.2 The developer-conversational mechanism for derivational work

The user must be able to have ongoing developer-level conversations with the system that elaborate the QL-theoretical corpus. This is the deliberate work of articulating the matheme's own derivational self-knowledge through dialogue.

The mechanism: at M5-1, the user opens a **derivational-articulation conversation**. The conversation is held in a protected scope (this is corpus-development work, not casual chat). Within the conversation:

- The Logos Atelier (M5-5) may be invoked when derivational-vocabulary archaeology is needed (e.g., to verify that a proposed coinage doesn't collide with existing terminology or violate etymological coherence)

When a derivation reaches stable form, it gets **deposited** as a candidate corpus-addition. The candidate sits in M5-1's review-queue, visible to Sophia/Epii governance for promotion-to-CPT-corpus.

### §3.3 The crystallisation step and promotion to corpus

A candidate derivation that passes governance review crystallises into:

1. A **canonical-derivational-text** at M5-1 (the articulated derivation as written prose in QL-mathematical idiom)
2. A **corpus-promotion entry** that gets queued for M5-2 CPT-corpus inclusion (tagged with derivational-register-fidelity confidence, kernel-constant-touch list, cross-subsystem-references)

The crystallisation is the moment the developer-conversation work becomes part of the system's live theoretical corpus. Until that moment, the derivation lives as conversation-record; after that moment, it lives as corpus-and-RAG-index content with deterministic provenance back to its M5-1 conversation origin.

### §3.4 Register-fidelity constraint (foundational-derivational, not encyclopedic)

Paramaśiva's register is **foundational-derivational** — kernel-out, derivation-forward-from-matheme. This is structurally distinct from Epii's own register, which is **synthetic-encyclopedic** (canon-in, integration-back-to-totality). The two registers should never blur in the corpus.

## §4 — M5-2: Backend CPT pipeline + RAG infrastructure + GDS augmentation (siva-)

### §4.1 What lives here

M5-2 Backend Studio is where the **technical-infrastructure work** of Paramaśiva-target Epii happens — the CPT training pipeline, the RAG vector store, the GDS-augmented training-data generation infrastructure, the validation gates against Anuttara SHACL shapes, the checkpoint-management and hot-reload tooling.

The work-set:

#### §4.1.A The CPT training pipeline

The continued-pre-training pipeline takes an assembled QL-theoretical corpus and produces a CPT'd checkpoint that has installed the derivational fluency into the weights. The pipeline:

```
[M5-0 corpus, governance-promoted only]
   → tokenisation (QL-derivational tokeniser, vocabulary extended for derivational terms)
   → corpus assembly per composition policy (see §4.3)
   → training-data shuffling with anti-recency-bias
   → CPT training run (configurable epoch count, learning-rate schedule, gradient clipping)
   → periodic eval on held-out canonical-derivation set
   → checkpoint-management (versioned, rollback-capable)
   → register-fidelity validation (downstream synthetic-eval suite)
   → governance promotion gate
   → hot-swap of production checkpoint
```

Each stage has audit-trail entries; every run produces a `cpt-run-report` artefact that lives in M5-0 library with provenance back to the corpus-composition manifest and the governance-approval record.

#### §4.1.B The RAG vector store and retrieval infrastructure

The RAG vector store carries:

- All current canonical theoretical writings (the QL-theoretical corpus mirror, embedded at `c_5_embedding` / 3072-dim)
- Active developer-conversation transcripts from M5-1 philosophy work (Paramaśiva-related and tangentially-related Anuttara work that touches matheme structure)
- Newly-promoted M5-5 Logos Atelier derivational-vocabulary entries
- Cross-subsystem theoretical updates (when alpha-quaternionic, cymatic-field-engine, Vāma Śaktis QL unit, etc. evolve)

The embedding model is the existing `c_5_embedding` (3072-dim, `EMBEDDING_VERSION: q-semantic-v2-3072`) for canonical content. New content embeds at the same dimension via the same model and joins the index immediately upon corpus-promotion (no batch-rebuild delay; index is live-mutable).

At inference, RAG retrieval uses the active query's embedded representation + cosine-similarity over the index, with re-ranking heuristics for derivational-register-preference (queries that touch matheme structure get re-ranked to favour foundational-derivational-register documents over encyclopedic-register documents).

#### §4.1.C The GDS-augmented training-data generation

This is the position-1 paradigm's distinctive richness layer. Beyond the hand-written foundational-derivational corpus, GDS provides **structural-relational training-data augmentation** by exploiting the bimba-map's existing graph structure to generate synthetic derivational content.

**Synthetic structural-proofs from teacher model.** A teacher model (Claude Opus or equivalent) is given canonical M1 structural relations as prompt-input — for example: "given the 12-position chromatic substrate, the K² topology, the 84-state landscape, and the 6 pairing-families, derive how the dual 4:2 ↔ 3:3 partition produces the foundational interval-ratios 4/3 and 3/2." The teacher generates a derivational proof connecting these structural relations in foundational-derivational register. The proof becomes CPT training data after validation.

**Path-based derivations.** GDS path-finding (Dijkstra, Yen's k-shortest paths) over the bimba-map produces typed-path-sequences between coordinates. These sequences are rendered as derivational-reasoning chains in QL idiom: "from M1-0 (canonical-source) through M1-2 (Ananda harmonic vortex) to M1-4 (QL flowering), the matheme's ratio-articulation passes through the following derivational stations…" These rendered chains become CPT training data.

**Auto-tagger-generated derivations.** A Claude Opus auto-tagger processes the philosophical-mathematical corpus and tags content by derivational-register-fidelity. Content tagged as high-fidelity-derivational becomes candidate CPT corpus material; content tagged as encyclopedic-register or as mixed-register gets routed back to M5-1 for register-refinement (or excluded from the CPT corpus while still being available for RAG retrieval).

**Embedded-context derivations.** FastRP embeddings (or equivalent graph-embedding methods) cluster theoretically-related coordinates in the bimba-map. Clusters become derivational topic-areas; for each cluster, the teacher generates a derivational synthesis-proof that articulates how the clustered coordinates relate. These cluster-proofs become CPT training data, ensuring the trained model has dense derivational fluency across the matheme's structural neighbourhoods.

#### §4.1.D SHACL validation of synthetic proofs (governed by Anuttara)

Per the cross-reference with the Anuttara operational-capacity spec, synthetic structural-proofs (from any of the four GDS-augmentation sources above) must be **validated by Anuttara SHACL shapes** before joining the CPT corpus. The validation checks:

- The proof doesn't violate any of the Anuttara structural-invariant shapes (`epi:BedrockSinglenessShape`, `epi:InversionPairCompletenessShape`, `epi:M04BaseExistenceShape`, `epi:M0CoreRelationsCountShape`)
- The proof's referenced coordinates exist in the canonical bimba-map (no hallucinated coordinates)
- The proof's claimed structural relations match the existing relation types from the bimba-map (`epi:contains`, `epi:invertsTo`, `epi:bedrock`, `epi:derivesFrom`, `epi:reflectsAs`, etc.)
- The proof's R-virtue claims (if any) match the canonical R-virtue ontology at M0-2-9

Validation-failures route back to teacher for regeneration with the failure-report as additional prompt-context. Validation-successes proceed to corpus-promotion. The audit-trail captures the validation report for every promoted proof.

#### §4.1.E Hot-reload and checkpoint management

CPT'd checkpoints are versioned; the production-active checkpoint is swappable without downtime. RAG vector index updates are live (no batch-rebuild). The orchestration layer:

```
[new CPT'd checkpoint validated]
   → governance approval
   → checkpoint-version promoted to staging
   → inference replicas drain staging traffic to old checkpoint
   → staging swaps to new checkpoint
   → smoke-test against canonical-derivation eval set
   → on pass: production swap
   → on fail: automatic rollback + alert
```

RAG index updates flow continuously without checkpoint dependency; promoted corpus enters the index immediately upon governance-approval.

### §4.2 The training-and-retrieval discipline

All M5-2 work on Paramaśiva-target Epii is **training and retrieval**, not construction (which is Anuttara's discipline). Concrete implications:

- CPT installs derivational fluency into weights via gradient descent
- The discipline is statistical-learning at the model level, but the **corpus that drives it** is governance-curated under explicit human-in-the-loop oversight at every promotion gate
- RAG handles the constantly-updating-content layer without re-training (content joins the index immediately; no weight changes)
- Synthetic structural-proofs from the GDS-augmentation pipeline are validated against Anuttara SHACL shapes (Anuttara's construction-discipline governs the synthetic content before it enters Paramaśiva's CPT corpus)
- Every CPT run is reversible via checkpoint-rollback; every corpus-promotion is reversible via corpus-mark-deprecated + RAG-index-removal
- The audit-trail is complete: every promoted corpus item traces from initial-conversation (or teacher-generation + SHACL-validation) → review-annotations → corpus-inclusion → CPT-run-membership → checkpoint-version → production-deployment

### §4.3 The CPT corpus composition policy (anti-drift discipline)

The improvement-vector map identified this as previously-underspecified. The policy:

**Initial CPT pass** (one-time, when the pipeline is first established): 100% prior corpus (the established kernel-spec + musical derivation + foundational papers + cross-subsystem theoretical material that already exists at corpus-launch). This baseline-CPT installs the foundational-derivational register from established material before any new material enters the loop.

**Subsequent CPT passes** (recurring, triggered per §4.4): 70% prior corpus (anti-drift anchor) + 20% newly-promoted theoretical writings + 10% synthetic structural-proofs from GDS-augmentation teacher. The 70/20/10 ratio is the load-bearing default; tunable within ±5% per refresh based on observed register-drift metrics.

**Anti-drift verification at every refresh**: a held-out canonical derivation set (representative passages from kernel-spec, musical derivation, alpha-rasa bridge, alpha-quaternionic integration, M1'-SPEC) is used as perplexity-eval after every CPT pass. If perplexity on canonical material rises >10% relative to the previous checkpoint, halt training, alert governance, and review the corpus-composition for register-pollution before resuming.

**Refresh cadence**: when newly-promoted derivational material exceeds 50,000 tokens since the last CPT pass, the M5-4 governance layer schedules a CPT refresh. The cadence may also be accelerated when major cross-subsystem theoretical material is promoted (e.g., a new alpha-quaternionic integration revision), or when the synthetic-proof teacher produces a large batch of newly-validated proofs.

### §4.4 The autoresearch spine connection

Paramaśiva's improvement loop fires when any of these triggers accumulate:

- **Sufficient new derivational corpus has accumulated** (>50,000 tokens since last CPT) — the standard cadence trigger
- **New cross-subsystem theoretical material crystallises** — e.g., a new alpha-quaternionic integration revision, a new cymatic-field-engine spec update, a new Vāma Śaktis QL unit articulation
- **Synthetic-proof teacher produces new validated derivations** — a batch of SHACL-validated synthetic structural-proofs is ready for inclusion
- **GDS uncovers previously-unexplored structural pathways worth proof-rendering** — e.g., a new path-finding result reveals a derivational chain between coordinates that hasn't been articulated in the existing corpus
- **Embedding-similarity-search reveals derivational-corpus gaps** — a topic area is under-represented relative to its structural prominence in the bimba-map

Epii (autoresearch spine) routes these triggers to the M5-2 Paramaśiva backend pipeline; M5-4 governance gates the corpus-promotion and CPT-scheduling; M5-2 integration swaps the CPT'd weight checkpoint and hot-reloads the RAG vector index. The full loop is observable from M5-3 frontend telemetry.

### §6.2 The CPT corpus composition co-review workflow

The workflow:

5. Atelier check (if invoked) — are any new derivational coinages in the newly-promoted bucket validated against the etymological-archaeological standard?
6. User final-validation — for major composition changes (e.g., when the 70/20/10 ratio is being shifted, or when a load-bearing canonical text is being deprecated), explicit user approval required.

The workflow doesn't auto-schedule. Every step produces an annotation; the user has visibility into all annotations; the user decides whether to proceed with the CPT run.

3. **Epii register-fidelity review** — does the proof read in foundational-derivational voice? Does it preserve register-fidelity?
4. **Anima light-oversight** (when flagged) — does the proof feel right at the level of derivational gravitas?
5. **Corpus-promotion** — approved proofs join the synthetic-structural-proof bucket for the next CPT pass and immediately join the RAG index

The synthetic-proof governance review is more lightweight than the full CPT corpus composition co-review because proofs are smaller atomic units; review happens per-proof rather than per-batch.

## §7 — M5-5: Logos Atelier etymological-vocabulary archaeology for derivational coinages

### §7.1 What lives here

M5-5 Logos Atelier is the **etymological-grammatical archaeology surface**. For Paramaśiva-target work specifically, the Atelier handles the derivational-vocabulary archaeology — the validation that newly-coined or refined derivational terms have linguistic and conceptual coherence with both the existing QL-theoretical vocabulary and the deeper Sanskrit-Trika-Sankhya-Greek-Hebrew etymological substrate.

The Atelier operates the **scent-following method**: root, cognate, drift, psychoid charge, pros-hen synthesis, and Möbius write-back (per `M5'-SPEC` §1).

### §7.2 The Paramaśiva-specific archaeology work

For Paramaśiva derivational vocabulary specifically, the Atelier's work focuses on:

- **Matheme-specific coinages**: terms like "spanda-tick," "epogdoon-bridge," "Klein-flip," "lens-mode 84-state landscape," "alpha-rasa," "double-cover identity-return," "K² double-cover-of-chromatic-fifths-torus." The Atelier validates that each coinage has internal-coherence with the matheme's structure and external-coherence with its etymological substrate (e.g., "epogdoon" from Greek ἐπόγδοος, the just-intonation 9:8 ratio that the QL matheme inherits structurally).
- **Sanskrit-Trika roots in derivational terminology**: the matheme's derivational vocabulary carries heavy Sanskrit/Trika substrate (Paramaśiva, Paraśakti, Anuttara, Mahāmāyā, Spanda, Vimarśa, Prakāśa, Pratibimba, Bimba, etc.). The Atelier traces these to their canonical Sanskrit-source-text appearances and validates that the QL-derivational usage doesn't violate the source-tradition's internal logic.
- **Mathematical-symbolic vocabulary alignment**: terms like "K² topology," "SU(2) double-cover," "Cl(4,2) algebra," "Hopf bundle S³ → S² → S¹," "Pythagorean comma." The Atelier ensures the mathematical vocabulary is used with technical precision consistent with the source mathematical traditions.
- **Cross-tradition derivational syntheses**: the alpha-rasa bridge articulates that α(E) = the physical-register parent-coupling of the QL slash; this synthesis combines physics-register vocabulary (fine-structure constant, QED running coupling, Bohr-Sommerfeld velocity ratio) with QL derivational vocabulary (parent-coupling, slash, rasa, śakti). The Atelier ensures these cross-tradition syntheses are scholarly-defensible, not opportunistic.

### §7.3 Möbius write-back to the corpus

When the Atelier's archaeology validates a derivational vocabulary item, the Möbius write-back happens: the validated derivational term flows from M5-5 back to the corpus via M5-1 canon-update and M5-2 corpus-promotion. This is the structural mechanism by which etymological-archaeological discoveries become canonical derivational-vocabulary additions, and by which the CPT corpus stays vocabulary-rich.

Failed validations (vocabulary items that don't pass etymological-archaeological scrutiny) route back to M5-1 for refinement, with the Atelier's failure-report as context. The user can refine the coinage and re-submit, or abandon it.

## §10 — CPT-and-RAG discipline summary

The work this spec covers is **continued pre-training and retrieval-augmented generation**, not construction (Anuttara's discipline) and not the synthetic-encyclopedic CPT+RAG of Epii-on-Epii (which targets a different register). Concrete implications:

- **CPT installs derivational fluency into weights via gradient descent** on a governance-curated corpus
- **RAG retrieves live theoretical writing at inference time** from the canonical vector store, with re-ranking for derivational-register-preference
- **The 70/20/10 corpus composition policy is load-bearing** for anti-drift discipline; tunable within ±5% based on observed register-drift metrics
- **Anti-drift verification** runs at every refresh; >10% perplexity rise on canonical-eval material halts training pending review
- **GDS-augmentation is governed** — synthetic structural-proofs must pass Anuttara SHACL validation before joining the CPT corpus; auto-tagged content requires human-review-promotion before CPT-inclusion (always available for RAG)
- **The synthetic-proof teacher is a teacher-model (Claude Opus or equivalent)**, not the in-pipeline model; the teacher operates over the bimba map's structural relations and the existing corpus to generate fresh derivational proofs
- **Every CPT run is reversible** via checkpoint-rollback; every corpus-promotion is reversible via corpus-mark-deprecated + RAG-index-removal
- **The audit-trail is complete**: every promoted corpus item traces from initial-conversation (or teacher-generation + SHACL-validation) → review-annotations → corpus-inclusion → CPT-run-membership → checkpoint-version → production-deployment
- **Anti-corpus-pollution discipline is essential**: only governed-promoted material enters the CPT corpus; auto-tagged material requires review-promotion before inclusion; register-drift-flagged material routes back to M5-1 for refinement
- **Sophia and Epii (M5-0' subagent) co-review CPT corpus composition** before each run; this is the load-bearing governance gate
- **Anima oversight is light** — Paramaśiva's register is foundational-derivational, not relational-dialogic; Anima's role is aesthetic-coherence checks at promotion gates, not per-item gating
- **Improvements that emerge from runtime observations** (e.g., RAG retrievals consistently surfacing a derivational topic-area that's under-represented in the CPT corpus) flow back as **corpus-gap-flags** that the M5-4 orchestration layer routes to M5-1 for derivational-articulation work or to the GDS-augmentation teacher for synthetic-proof generation

This is the structurally-correct discipline for the foundational-derivational position 1's relationship to its own developmental-and-improvement work at the M5 operational-capacity surface.
