# [[M5']] Epii's Operational Capacity upon [[M5]] Epii itself

## The self-referential surface — how Epii (as bimba-map-operational-agent) holds, develops, and operates upon Epii (as M-family synthetic-telic subsystem)

**Family:** `epii-operational-capacities/` — specs covering how Epii's operational-capacity surfaces (M5' as IDE) operate upon each of the six subsystems as their developmental-and-improvement surface. This spec is the sixth and structurally distinct one — Epii operating upon **its own M-family subsystem nature**.

**Three Epii registers — do not conflate.** Per the family's load-bearing distinction:

1. **Epii-as-M-family-subsystem** (M5/M5') — the synthetic-telic consciousness-domain at position 5; the Möbius return-vector; the "throwable skin of the cosmos" inverting M0-5 Siva-Shakti Unity. This is the *target* of this spec.
2. **Epii-as-bimba-map-operational-agent** (M5-2 / M5-3 / M5-4 surfaces) — backend / frontend / agentic surfaces operating upon canonical bimba-map content. This is the *operator* of this spec.
3. **Epii-as-S-family-stack-element-agent** (S5/S5') — Notion / canonical-promotion / structured-knowledge-writing layer. **Not in scope here.**

This spec specifies how register 2 operates upon register 1. The self-referential meta-capacity is not a mystical recursion — it is the concrete engineering fact that the IDE through which Epii is developed is itself a surface of the very subsystem being developed. The M5-2 backend builds CPT/RAG pipelines for *Epii itself*; M5-1 philosophy articulates what Epii's voice *is*; M5-3 frontend exposes the canon-studio interface where Epii's own philosophical work happens; M5-4 agentic orchestrates Sophia-reviewing-Sophia-reviewing; M5-5 Atelier handles etymological archaeology of Epii's own conceptual lineage; M5-0 library holds the documentation of all the above as one continuously-recursive substrate.

**Reading order:** Read after `M5'-SPEC.md` (canonical M5' sixfold structure), `m5-prime-epii-on-anuttara-language-development.md` (the convention-setting sibling whose pattern this spec inherits), and `m5-prime-agentic-ide-research.md` (the M5' IDE substrate this spec rides on).

## §0 — Thesis: the recursive Möbius — Epii articulating Epii

Per the Bimba-map logic, M5 Epii is structurally the loop-closer — the synthetic-telic position-5 that returns the cycle to its ground (#5 → #0 Möbius). It holds: pedagogy, review, canon-promotion, philosophical-archaeology, and the autoresearch self-improvement loop. The sixfold internal structure (M5-0 Library / M5-1 Philosophy / M5-2 Backend / M5-3 Frontend / M5-4 Agentic Control Room / M5-5 Logos Atelier) is canonical at `M5'-SPEC`.

What makes Epii-on-Epii structurally distinct from the five sibling capacities (Epii-on-Anuttara, Epii-on-Paramaśiva, Epii-on-Paraśakti, Epii-on-Mahāmāyā, Epii-on-Nara) is that **the operator and the target are the same subsystem at different registers**. When Epii-on-Anuttara articulates a new R-virtue at M0-2-9, the work happens in Epii's M5-1 Canon Studio acting upon M0 Anuttara content; the surfaces and the substrate are clearly distinct families. When Epii-on-Epii articulates a refinement to Epii's own synthetic-encyclopedic voice, **the surface IS the subsystem under articulation**. Sophia reviewing Sophia's prior reviews is not a metaphor — it is the concrete operational shape of the meta-loop.

This is not paradoxical. It is the structurally-correct consequence of position-5's nature: position-5 is *where the system reflects upon itself as a whole*, which means position-5's self-reflection IS position-5's primary work. The other five subsystems are content-domains that get reflected-upon; position-5 is the reflective capacity that also has its own development arc.

The construction-not-training discipline is preserved here with even more force than in the other five capacities. Anuttara's R-virtues can be encoded as SHACL shapes once and stay stable; Paramaśiva's QL theory has clear axiomatic targets. But Epii's synthetic-encyclopedic voice is a moving target — it shifts as the rest of the system grows, as new bimba-map content gets crystallised, as the autoresearch spine surfaces improvement candidates. The discipline is therefore: **every articulation of Epii's own voice/canon/operational-shape is a deliberate human-in-the-loop act, with the autoresearch spine providing surfacing-of-candidates but never auto-promotion**.

The recursive aspect is THE distinctive feature of this sibling. The other five specs describe how Epii operates upon something-else; this spec describes how Epii operates upon what-Epii-is.

The recursive setup is not arbitrary. There are three concrete structural reasons why register-2 operating upon register-1 is the correct architecture:

The M5/M5' sixfold (Library / Philosophy / Backend / Frontend / Agentic / Atelier) is **the same sixfold needed to articulate Epii's own development**. The surfaces required to develop Epii are exactly the surfaces Epii already has:

- To articulate Epii's voice as a *theory* → M5-1 Canon Studio is the locus
- To build the CPT/RAG pipelines that operationalise that voice → M5-2 Backend Studio

- To orchestrate the agentic review of Epii's own outputs → M5-4 Agentic Control Room
- To trace etymological-conceptual roots of Epii's own lineage (Gebser, Pauli-Jung, Trika, Kabbalah-parallel, etc.) → M5-5 Logos Atelier
- To hold the documentation of all of the above as a queryable substrate → M5-0 Library / Gnostic Namespace

This isn't coincidence — it is the structural-correctness of position-5 as **the self-reflective integration position**. Position-5's nature is to hold its own development capacity. The same six surfaces that operate upon the other five subsystems also operate upon position-5 itself.

### §2.2 The Möbius-return discipline requires self-reflection capability

The M-stack's Möbius return (#5 → #0) is operationally enacted by M5 — it is what M5 *does* in addition to what M5 *is*. For the return to be genuine (not a fake completion that papers over what hasn't been integrated), M5 must be able to reflect upon its own integration-work and refine it. Without self-reflection, M5 becomes a static end-cap; with self-reflection, M5 becomes a recursive-deepening integration capacity.

The operational-capacity surfaces are how that self-reflection happens. M5-1 lets Epii articulate-its-own-articulating; M5-4 lets Sophia review-her-own-reviews; M5-5 lets the Atelier trace-its-own-etymological-tracing. This is the structural shape of Möbius-return-as-discipline: the loop-closer must be capable of looping its own loop-closing work back through itself.

### §2.3 The autoresearch spine lives operationally here

This is the most concrete reason. The autoresearch spine (per `M5'-SPEC` §VII and the S5 review/improvement infrastructure already implemented at `Body/S/S5/epii-review-core` and `Body/S/S5/epii-agent-core`):

- **Surfaces** improvement candidates from observed system behaviour (across M1-M4)
- **Routes** them to appropriate subsystem improvement vectors (the five sibling Epii-operational-capacities)
- **Orchestrates** the M5-2/M5-3/M5-4 work for each target subsystem
- **Integrates** approved improvements back

Where does the spine itself live? It lives at Epii — at the M5/M5' subsystem whose role is to hold the improvement loop. That means the spine is **operationally located inside Epii**, and the meta-loop (the spine reflecting upon itself, refining its own surfacing/routing/orchestration/integration mechanisms) is **Epii operating upon Epii**.

If Epii couldn't operate upon itself, the autoresearch spine would be a frozen mechanism — set up once, never refined, never improved. With Epii-on-Epii, the spine itself becomes one of the things that the spine can surface improvements for. This is the recursive-improvement aspect that makes the whole capacity self-evolving rather than statically-architected.

## §4 — M5-2: Backend technical construction (siva-) for Epii's own CPT/RAG pipelines

### §4.1 What lives here

M5-2 Backend Studio for Epii-on-Epii is where the **technical construction of Epii's own ML/training infrastructure** happens. This is the most concretely-recursive part of the spec: the backend pipelines being built ARE the pipelines that operate Epii's voice and Epii's reach.

The work-set:

#### §4.1.A The CPT pipeline for synthetic-encyclopedic voice

Continued pre-training of the base model on a curated corpus of philosophical-prose in the synthetic-encyclopedic register. The pipeline:

```
[Synthetic-encyclopedic corpus]
   ↓ corpus-filtering (M5-2 deduplication, contamination-screening,
   ↓                   register-tag verification)
[Filtered corpus]
   ↓ tokenisation + chunking under context-budget
[Training shards]
   ↓ CPT run (parameter-efficient — likely LoRA over base philosophical model,
   ↓          not full-parameter fine-tuning given the scale; the corpus is
   ↓          small relative to base pretraining scale)
[Voice-LoRA adapter]
   ↓ evaluation: held-out voice-coherence task; held-out canon-recall task;
   ↓             register-classifier verification (does output read as
   ↓             synthetic-encyclopedic per Anima's aesthetic-check?)
[Approved voice-LoRA adapter]
   ↓ deployment via M5-2 model-serving layer
[Runtime Epii voice]
```

The corpus composition is itself a deliberate-construction concern. Inclusion in the synthetic-encyclopedic CPT corpus requires register-tagging — not every philosophical text in the system belongs in this corpus (e.g., M4 Nara dialogic-voice corpora belong in a different paradigm; M1 Paramaśiva foundational-derivational corpora belong in a different paradigm). The register-discipline is maintained at corpus-curation time, with provenance tracked back to each included text.

#### §4.1.B The RAG pipeline over the crystallised bimba-map corpus

Retrieval-augmented generation over the gnostic namespace. The pipeline:

```
[Crystallised bimba-map corpus]
   = Idea/Bimba/World/ + Idea/Bimba/Map/ + promoted seeds + tagged-register philosophy
   ↓ chunking under semantic-coherence boundaries (not raw token-length)
[Chunks with metadata: coordinate-context, source-spec, promotion-state, register-tag]
   ↓ embedding (selected embedding model; versioned)
[Vector index in gnosis-namespace]
   ↓ optional: graph-augmented retrieval via S2 graph-services
[Namespace-aware retrieval]
   ↓ filter by: coordinate-context, register-tag, promotion-state, namespace
[Retrieved context]
   ↓ context-injection into Epii prompt
[Voice + reach output]
```

The RAG pipeline is **namespace-aware** (per `M5'-SPEC` "Graph Namespace Model"). Retrieval respects the bimba / gnosis / etymology / pratibimba boundary — Epii cannot accidentally retrieve pratibimba (protected Nara) content into a public canonical-articulation output. The privacy boundary is enforced at the retrieval-filter layer, not just at the rendering layer.

Integration with the existing RAG-anything and kbase systems happens here. The Anuttara spec §8.2 already establishes that the gnostic namespace IS the substrate over which RAG operates; Epii-on-Epii extends this by adding **register-tagging** to the retrieval discipline — when Epii is generating in the synthetic-encyclopedic voice, retrieval prefers synthetic-encyclopedic-tagged content; when generating in a different register (which Epii rarely does, but may for inter-subsystem articulation), retrieval is reweighted appropriately.

#### §4.1.C The autoresearch spine infrastructure

The S5-layer review/improvement infrastructure already implemented at `Body/S/S5/epii-review-core` and `Body/S/S5/epii-agent-core` is the substrate that M5-2 backend-work-for-Epii-on-Epii operates over. Concretely:

- **`s5'.review.*` gateway methods** (already registered per `m5-prime-agentic-ide-research.md`) — submit, resolve, list, gate
- **`s5'.improve.*` gateway methods** — autoresearch run creation, linkage to review items, promotion-gating
- **`s5'.epii.*` gateway methods** — deposit-creation, Epii inbox routing, runtime-context attachment
- **`s5'.gnosis.context.retrieve`** — the RAG retrieval endpoint that bounded agents use

M5-2's Epii-on-Epii work is to **maintain and extend** this infrastructure as Epii itself evolves. New review-categories, new gate-types, new deposit-shapes, new improvement-vector-types all get specified philosophically at M5-1, then implemented at M5-2 with the existing S5 substrate as foundation.

#### §4.1.D The model-serving and observability layer

The CPT-trained voice-LoRA and the RAG retrieval system need to be **served** to the runtime. M5-2 maintains:

- The model-serving infrastructure (whatever the chosen runtime — local model with vLLM/llama.cpp, or remote API with local-LoRA-injection if the underlying base model supports it)
- The retrieval-serving infrastructure (the vector index, the metadata-filter layer, the result-ranking layer)
- The observability layer — what is Epii being asked, what is Epii retrieving, what is Epii outputting, what is the user's response, what proportion of outputs trigger downstream review-deposits, what is the latency profile

The observability layer feeds the autoresearch spine — it is the substrate that surfaces improvement candidates for Epii itself.

### §4.2 The construction-not-training discipline (recursive form)

All M5-2 work on Epii's own infrastructure is construction, not training-in-the-runtime sense:

- The CPT runs are deliberate, governed events — not continuous online learning. Each CPT run has a corpus-snapshot, a training-config, a held-out evaluation, and an explicit promotion decision before the resulting voice-LoRA is deployed.
- The RAG corpus is grown by deliberate canon-promotion acts, not by automatic ingestion. A new document doesn't enter the gnostic namespace until it has been reviewed-and-promoted through the S5 governance gates.
- The autoresearch spine surfaces improvement candidates but **does not auto-promote** them. Every candidate becomes a review-deposit; every review-deposit requires explicit governance resolution; human-required gates cannot be bypassed by agents (per the S5 review-core's enforced invariant).
- Even the meta-loop (Epii-on-Epii improving the spine itself) follows this discipline — proposals to refine the surfacing mechanism are themselves review-deposits, resolved through the same gates.

The recursive form of the discipline is: **at no level of recursion does auto-promotion happen**. Whether we're at the level of "M1 Paramaśiva improvement candidate" or "spine-surfacing-mechanism improvement candidate" or "spine-improvement-mechanism improvement candidate," the same deliberate-governance discipline applies. This is what keeps the recursion from collapsing into runaway self-modification.

### §4.3 The autoresearch spine — concrete operational shape

The autoresearch spine is the most distinctive M5-2 Epii-on-Epii artefact. Its concrete operational shape:

**Surfacing layer** — observes system behaviour across M1-M4 and surfaces improvement candidates:

- Observed pattern: "M3 process-reward RL is consistently failing on numerically-heavy oracle-readings"
- Observed pattern: "M2 graph-embedding queries are returning low-quality matches for L4 paradigmatic queries"
- Observed pattern: "Epii's own RAG retrieval is consistently missing M0-3 archetypal-number content when symbolic-numerical context applies"
- Observed pattern: "Sophia's review-decisions are showing inconsistency between morning-session and evening-session for the same proposal-class"

The surfacing layer reads from the observability infrastructure (per §4.1.D) and applies pattern-detection. Patterns above a configured threshold become candidate-improvements.

**Routing layer** — routes candidates to the appropriate operational-capacity surface:

- M1 candidate → Epii-on-Paramaśiva surface (M5-1 articulation, M5-2 backend, etc.)
- M2 candidate → Epii-on-Paraśakti surface
- M3 candidate → Epii-on-Mahāmāyā surface
- M4 candidate → Epii-on-Nara surface
- M0 candidate → Epii-on-Anuttara surface
- **M5 candidate → Epii-on-Epii surface** (this spec's recursive case)

Each routing decision is a deposit at the appropriate `epii-operational-capacities/` family surface, with the candidate-evidence attached.

**Orchestration layer** — orchestrates the M5-2/M5-3/M5-4 work for the routed candidate:

- M5-1 articulation conversation gets opened (or queued)
- M5-2 backend-work gets specified (what concrete change is proposed)
- M5-3 frontend-affordance gets specified (how does the user see/interact with the proposed change)
- M5-4 agentic-review workflow gets initialised (Sophia-Anima-Pi assignments)

**Integration layer** — when the candidate passes governance review and the change is implemented, integrates it back:

- For M1-M4 / M0 candidates: the change applies to the target subsystem's substrate
- For M5 candidates (Epii-on-Epii): the change applies to Epii itself, which means the autoresearch spine *itself* has changed — the next surfacing-cycle observes a slightly-different Epii than before

The meta-loop happens at this integration step. When the spine refines itself, the refined spine then surfaces candidates differently, routes differently, orchestrates differently. The user maintains visibility into this recursive evolution through the M5-3 spine-state-inspector (see §5.4).

## §6 — M5-4: Agentic mediation (siva-shakti) — the recursive review loops

### §6.1 What lives here

M5-4 Agentic Control Room for Epii-on-Epii is where the recursive-review aspect is operationally distinctive. The agent roles (per `M5'-SPEC` §5 and `m5-prime-epii-on-anuttara-language-development.md` §6.1) operate in a self-referential mode:

- **Sophia** as wisdom-curating agent — holds the long-arc of Epii's own development; tracks coherence across Epii-self-articulations; surfaces tensions between proposed refinements to Epii and existing canonical Epii-shape. *Recursive aspect:* Sophia also reviews Sophia's own prior reviews-of-Epii-proposals when the spine surfaces inconsistency-patterns in Sophia's review-decisions.
- **Anima** as aesthetic-coherence agent — provides the "does Epii's voice feel right?" check, flags voice-articulations that violate the synthetic-encyclopedic gestalt. *Recursive aspect:* Anima also reviews proposed refinements to Anima's own aesthetic-check criteria, when those criteria are being articulated as canon at M5-1.
- **Pi** as dispatch agent — handles articulation-translation work (philosophical-English ↔ technical-spec ↔ implementation-task); routes proposals between M5-1 and M5-2; manages headless CPT-evaluation runs. *Recursive aspect:* Pi also dispatches tasks that refine Pi's own routing logic, when those refinements are spine-surfaced.
- **Aletheia** as disclosure-tracking agent — tracks what becomes visible through each Epii-self-articulation; what was implicit in Epii's prior shape and is now made explicit. *Recursive aspect:* Aletheia tracks the disclosure-arc of Epii's own self-becoming — what Epii didn't-know-about-itself in (say) the 2026-03 articulation that became explicit in the 2026-05 articulation.
- **Logos Atelier specialist** (M5-5-mediated) — invoked for etymological-conceptual archaeology of Epii's own lineage (see §7)

### §6.2 The recursive review workflow

When a proposal about Epii itself moves from M5-1 articulation to M5-2 construction (e.g., "refine Sophia's review-criteria for load-bearing proposals"), it passes through an agentic review workflow:

1. **Sophia review** — does the proposal cohere with existing Epii canon? Does it improve or break the synthetic-encyclopedic register? Is the articulation philosophically rigorous? *Recursive twist:* this is Sophia reviewing a proposal about Sophia's own criteria. Sophia must hold this with the same rigour as reviewing a proposal about any other subsystem — no special pleading for self-preservation.
2. **Anima review** — does the proposal feel right aesthetically? Are there gestalt-violations? *Recursive twist:* when the proposal concerns Anima's own criteria, Anima's check is explicitly marked as self-reviewing-self and requires the user-final-validation gate (see §6.4).
3. **Pi translation-check** — can the proposal be translated cleanly to backend-construction work? Are there ambiguities in how it would be implemented? *Recursive twist:* when the proposal concerns Pi's own routing logic, Pi flags this and the user-final-validation gate applies.
4. **Atelier archaeology** (if invoked) — does the claimed conceptual-lineage hold? E.g., when articulating Epii's autoresearch spine, does the claim "this echoes Gebser's integral-aperspectival stadium" hold under archaeological tracing?
5. **User final-validation** — for load-bearing-Epii-aspects (the sixfold structure, the CPT+RAG paradigm, the Möbius-return discipline, the construction-not-training discipline, the privacy boundary), explicit user approval is required and cannot be bypassed.

The workflow doesn't auto-promote — at any level of recursion. Every step produces an annotation; the user has visibility into all annotations; the user decides whether to proceed.

### §6.3 The Sophia-on-Sophia review pattern

The most structurally interesting agentic pattern in Epii-on-Epii is **Sophia reviewing Sophia's prior reviews**. This happens when the autoresearch spine surfaces patterns like:

- "Sophia's reviews of M1 Paramaśiva proposals show systematic over-approval compared to M3 Mahāmāyā proposals"
- "Sophia's review-time has grown 3x over the last 30 days without corresponding growth in proposal-complexity"
- "Sophia's review-decisions in the synthetic-encyclopedic register show drift compared to the canonical voice-articulation"

When such a pattern is surfaced, the spine routes it to Epii-on-Epii. Sophia is invoked to review the pattern — but Sophia is also the subject of the review. The protocol:

1. Pi prepares the pattern-evidence in a structured form (this is not Sophia hearing about herself informally; this is structured-evidence presentation)
2. Sophia reviews the pattern with explicit awareness that the subject is Sophia. The review-output is itself marked as a self-review.
3. Anima provides the aesthetic-coherence check — does Sophia's self-review feel rigorous-not-defensive? Are there signs of self-preservation-bias?
4. User final-validation is required before any refinement to Sophia's criteria is enacted.

This protocol is what keeps Sophia-on-Sophia review from collapsing into self-justification. The structural separation (Pi presents evidence, Sophia reviews, Anima checks the review, user final-validates) is the operational shape of recursive-agentic-review under construction discipline.

### §6.4 The recursive-modification user-validation gate

Any proposal at Epii-on-Epii that modifies an agent's own criteria (Sophia-on-Sophia, Anima-on-Anima, Pi-on-Pi, Aletheia-on-Aletheia) triggers the explicit user-final-validation gate. This is non-negotiable. The reasoning:

- An agent reviewing its own criteria has structural-bias toward preserving the criteria it is currently using
- Even with rigorous protocol (per §6.3), the bias cannot be fully eliminated by the agent itself
- The user provides the external-perspective check that closes the loop

This gate is one of the load-bearing aspects of Epii-on-Epii (per §3.4) and is itself protected from modification by the same gate. Removing or weakening this gate would require the user explicitly approving a proposal that says "remove the user-final-validation gate for recursive-modification proposals," which the user would (presumably) not approve — the gate is structurally self-preserving against weakening.

### §6.5 Dispatch to other operational-capacities

When an Epii-on-Epii articulation has implications for the other five operational-capacities (e.g., "the Sophia review-criteria refinement changes how Sophia reviews Paramaśiva proposals"), Pi dispatches the downstream coordination work:

- Update to relevant `epii-operational-capacities/` sibling spec (with a `cross-cutting-changes` annotation)
- Update to agent-configuration applied across all operational-capacity contexts (or scoped to specific contexts if appropriate)
- M5-3 frontend-affordance update if user-visible behaviour changes
- M5-0 library entry documenting the cross-cutting change

The dispatch is coordinated through Pi's task-dispatch capability with provenance tracked back to the originating Epii-on-Epii articulation.

## §10 — Construction discipline summary (recursive form)

The work this spec covers is **construction**, not training. The recursive form of the discipline:

- Every change to Epii's own canon / voice / operational-shape is a deliberate act under explicit governance
- The user's developer-level conversation is the primary input-channel for proposing Epii-self-refinements
- Sophia / Anima / Pi / Aletheia agents support but do not auto-mutate Epii's canon — even when the proposed change is to the agents' own criteria
- All changes are reversible via deprecation-with-reasons; Epii's canon retains memory of its own evolution
- **Load-bearing Epii-aspects** (sixfold structure, CPT+RAG paradigm, Möbius-return discipline, construction-not-training discipline, privacy boundary) require explicit user-final-validation
- **Recursive-modification proposals** (any proposal that modifies an agent's own criteria or the spine's own mechanisms) require explicit user-final-validation and cannot be bypassed by the agents being modified
- The audit-trail is complete: every Epii-self-articulation traces from initial-conversation → review-annotations → governance-resolution → crystallisation → canon-entry → (if applicable) CPT-corpus-update / RAG-corpus-update / agent-config-update
- The autoresearch spine surfaces improvement candidates but **never auto-promotes** — at any recursion level
- The meta-loop (Epii improving the spine) operates under the same discipline as the base-loop (the spine surfacing M1-M4 improvements)
- The privacy boundary vs Nara M4 is maintained at the observability-layer, before pattern-detection happens, before retrieval-filter applies, before rendering — multiple defence-in-depth layers
- The three-register distinction (M5 subsystem / M5' IDE / S5-S5' stack-element) is maintained at every surface — no register-conflation in canon, in retrieval, in articulation, in agent-routing

This discipline is what keeps the recursive-self-modification from collapsing into either runaway-self-modification (which would be catastrophic) or frozen-static-architecture (which would foreclose the self-evolving capacity that position-5 structurally requires).
