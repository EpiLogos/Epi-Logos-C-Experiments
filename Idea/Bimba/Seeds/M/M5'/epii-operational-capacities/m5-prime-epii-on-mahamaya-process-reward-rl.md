## §0 — Thesis: Epii's process-reward-RL throwable-skin function over Mahāmāyā

Per the Bimba-map logic, M3 Mahāmāyā is *where calculation happens*. The kernel-tick choreography flows through Mahāmāyā as the codon-rotation-eligible computational substrate: every dinucleotide pair-evaluation, every rotational state computation, every DET projection from M2, every line-change operator application, every Tarot exact-cover match — these are calculation pathways that move through Mahāmāyā's matrix architecture. The 472-state modal-inversion landscape and the (lens, mode) ↔ (codon, rotation) bidirectional map are not static lookup tables; they are the live pathway-space over which the matheme actually computes.

This means: **Mahāmāyā is the subsystem at which training (gradient-descent) IS structurally appropriate**, because it is the subsystem whose work is *calculation* and whose pathways are *learnable*. Unlike M0 (Anuttara as language — not trained, constructed), M1 (Paramaśiva as theory — not trained, derived), and M2 (Paraśakti as graph-relational structure — trained at the embedding-level but not at the calculation-pathway level), M3 is where the matheme's own calculation can be reinforced, refined, and improved via the process-reward RL paradigm.

But here lies the load-bearing structural twist: **the training itself is gradient-descent; the *pipeline that configures, runs, reviews, and integrates* the training is construction.** The construction-not-training discipline applies to the *meta-level* — how training rounds get scheduled, how reward signals get designed, how federated rounds get governed, how genetic-program-synthesis outputs get reviewed before integration, how new symbolic programs get promoted into the runtime kernel-policy weights. Epii's operational capacity over Mahāmāyā is therefore the **pipeline-construction discipline** around a learning substrate that itself does gradient-descent.

The pipeline has three intertwined paradigm-strands (per the M5-0/M5-0' improvement-vector map subagent report's canonical position-3 paradigm):

1. **Process-reward RL** (DeepSeek-R1 / OpenAI-o1 lineage): dense step-level reward signal. Each step of multi-step calculation (each element of the eight-element kernel-tick traversal) gets evaluated and scored. The kernel-policy weights update against the per-step coherence rather than only against terminal outcome.
2. **Federated learning** (FedAvg-style local-training + central-averaging with differential-privacy mechanisms): users' opt-in calculation-pathway data trains local model updates; only the gradient-aggregate (DP-noised) leaves device; central averaging produces the next-round kernel-policy weights. No user data leaves device.
3. **Symbolic-genetic program synthesis** (neural program synthesis + genetic programming + symbolic regression): a typed program-synthesis space over the eight-element kernel-tick vocabulary, with population-based evolution producing candidate symbolic calculation-programs that the runtime can integrate as additional pathways.

These three strands are not three competing approaches; they are three complementary instruments on the same pipeline-loom, and they each carry distinctive governance demands. Epii's M5-sub-coordinates compose into the surface that orchestrates all three under coherent construction-discipline.

Per the cymatic-field paper §10 (Janus aspect at M3), every codon-rotation is a temporal-doorway — one face reads backward as inherited symbolic state, the other reads forward as possible transformation. The RL training must preserve this bidirectionality: calculation pathways are not unidirectional sequences but Janus-faced rotational-states, and reward must be defined on the bidirectional kernel-trace, never on the forward-projection alone.

### §3.2 The reward-signal articulation (the hybrid design)

The MOST important underspecified piece flagged by the M5-0/M5-0' improvement-vector map was: what concretely IS the per-element QL-coherence score for the process-reward RL? Three options were on the table:

1. A **trained reward model** (a neural critic)
2. **Anuttara-shape-validation** result (deterministic via SHACL)
3. **Heuristic over kernel-trace structure**

This spec makes the design decision explicit: **the reward signal is a hybrid, layered in three tiers, with explicit weighting and explicit fallback discipline.**

#### §3.2.A Tier 1 — Anuttara SHACL validation as deterministic base layer

Per the Anuttara sibling §4.1.C, the nine R-virtues at M0-2-9 and the structural-invariant shapes (BedrockSinglenessShape, InversionPairCompletenessShape, M04BaseExistenceShape, M0CoreRelationsCountShape) are SHACL shapes that can be evaluated deterministically. For any kernel-trace element, the relevant SHACL shapes (selected by the element's coordinate-context and sensitivity-class) are evaluated against the element's output-state. SHACL produces:

- **conformance** (passes all relevant shapes) → score component = +1.0
- **info** (info-severity finding) → score component = +0.5
- **warn** (warn-severity finding) → score component = 0.0
- **violation** (violation-severity finding) → score component = −1.0

This is the **base layer**. It is deterministic, fast (SHACL evaluation is bounded-polynomial), auditable, and grounded in the Anuttara-as-language canon (which is constructed, not learned). It provides the irreducible coherence-floor: no kernel-trace element can score above the base layer if it violates SHACL shapes that the canon has crystallised.

#### §3.2.B Tier 2 — Heuristic over kernel-trace structure as quick-gating

For kernel-trace elements where SHACL shapes do not apply or are too coarse, a deterministic heuristic operates over the kernel-trace structure. Heuristics include:

- **Pair-composition validity**: did the dinucleotide pair-evaluation invoke a valid `(p1_idx, p2_idx)` pair from `M3_PAIR_MATRIX`? (binary gate: pass/fail)
- **Quaternion-axis consistency**: did the matrix-type invoked at this element match the quaternion-axis expected by the kernel-policy at this kernel-tick element? (binary gate)
- **Rotational-state coherence**: did the rotational-state computation produce a value within `[0, rotationalStateCount−1]` for the codon-class? (binary gate)
- **4X-invariant audit**: did the codon-quaternion satisfy `pp + mm + mp + pm = 4X` after the element's transformation? (binary gate — this is the compile-time invariant from `m3.h` FR 2.3.18; runtime violation is a critical failure)
- **Evolutionary-gap honesty**: did the element correctly emit `STATUS_PROVISIONAL` if it encountered `M3_RESONANCE_GAP` (`0xFF`)? (binary gate)
- **Janus-bidirectionality preservation**: did the element's transformation preserve the backward-readable inherited-state alongside the forward-readable produced-state? (binary gate)

Each heuristic gate is 0 (fail) or 1 (pass). The heuristic tier score is the average of relevant gates. Heuristics are fast, deterministic, and operate without any trained model.

#### §3.2.C Tier 3 — Trained reward model for fine-grained coherence-scoring

For kernel-trace elements where the SHACL base and the heuristic tier are both coarse (i.e., both produce passing scores but the matheme's deeper coherence is what's actually at stake), a small trained reward model produces a fine-grained coherence score in [0, 1]. The reward model is:

- Small (target: <50M parameters; a transformer-based critic with kernel-trace tokenisation)
- Trained on **Anuttara-validated kernel-traces** as positive examples and on **deliberately-perturbed kernel-traces** as negative examples (the perturbations come from a curated bank at M5-1, not from synthetic random-mutation)
- Trained under the construction-discipline at the pipeline-meta-level: training runs are governed events at M5-4; the curated positive/negative bank is canon-managed at M5-1; promotions of new reward-model weights into the runtime are gated by Sophia review and explicit user-final-validation for load-bearing reward-signal-canon modifications
- Versioned with provenance: every kernel-trace evaluated against a reward-model version has the version-id in its score-record

#### §3.2.D The hybrid composition

For each kernel-trace element, the composite process-reward signal is:

```
reward_element = w_shacl × shacl_score
               + w_heuristic × heuristic_score
               + w_critic × critic_score
               + α_gap × gap_honesty_modifier
               + β_janus × janus_bidirectionality_modifier
```

With weights:
- `w_shacl = 0.5` (deterministic, canonical, irreducible)
- `w_heuristic = 0.3` (deterministic, structural)
- `w_critic = 0.2` (fine-grained, learned)
- `α_gap` and `β_janus` are bias-modifiers (positive when correctly honoured, sharply negative when violated) to enforce the structural-discipline that gap-honesty and Janus-bidirectionality are not optional

Weight-rebalancing is a canon-evolution act at M5-1; defaults above are the initial canonical settings.

The composite kernel-trace reward is the weighted sum of per-element rewards across the eight-element traversal, weighted by element-importance (defaults: I=0.10, II=0.20, III=0.15, IV=0.10, V=0.10, VI=0.15, VII=0.15, VIII=0.05 — articulated to reflect that elements II and VI carry the heaviest prehensive work).

#### §3.2.E Why hybrid and not single-tier

A pure-SHACL design would be deterministic and auditable but too coarse to drive meaningful RL updates (kernel-trace elements rarely violate SHACL; the RL would have flat reward most of the time). A pure-heuristic design would catch structural failures but miss subtle coherence-issues (e.g., a rotational-state that's structurally valid but contextually-incoherent with the inherited (lens, mode)). A pure-critic design would be expressive but vulnerable to reward-hacking (the critic could be gamed by kernel-trace patterns that score well but violate the canon). The hybrid resists each failure-mode: SHACL prevents canon-violation, heuristics prevent structural-degradation, critic provides the fine-grained gradient signal.

### §3.3 The federation policy articulation

The federated-learning configuration is articulated at M5-1 as canon, not configured as runtime flag. Key articulations:

- **Differential-privacy budget**: ε = 1.0 per round; total budget ε = 10 over user's lifetime, with explicit consent required for additional rounds beyond the lifetime budget. (Per the improvement-vector-map's flagged underspecification; this is the canonical setting.)
- **DP mechanism**: Gaussian noise added to local model updates before transmission. Sensitivity bound: gradient L2 norm clipping at 1.0. Noise multiplier: σ = 1.1 (calibrated to ε = 1.0 per round under FedAvg-RDP accounting).
- **Federation cadence**: monthly rounds by default; minimum 100 opted-in users per round for k-anonymity-style protection. Below threshold, the round is skipped and rolled forward.
- **Aggregation**: FedAvg with gradient clipping at norm 1.0. Weighted by participating-user count, not by individual-user data-size (to preserve per-user voice).
- **Opt-out semantics**: opt-out is always honoured. Aggregation excludes a user's data immediately upon opt-out. Previously-aggregated rounds are not retroactively un-aggregated (this is canon-tracked at M5-0 as a known privacy-discipline trade-off; future canon may add round-recall for high-sensitivity opt-outs).
- **No-data-leaves-device invariant**: only DP-noised gradient aggregates leave device. Raw kernel-traces, calculation-pathway data, and per-user reward-signal records remain local. Cryptographic verification (e.g., secure aggregation via additively-homomorphic encryption) for the gradient-transmission is a future canon-extension at M5-1.

### §3.4 The symbolic-genetic program synthesis articulation

The genetic-programming layer operates over a typed program-synthesis space built on the **eight-element kernel-tick vocabulary**. Canonical articulation:

- **Vocabulary**: typed operations over the M3 substrate — `pair_eval(p1_idx, p2_idx)`, `rotation_apply(codon, rot_idx)`, `det_project(m2_vibration_index)`, `line_change(hexagram, line_idx)`, `tarot_cover(codon)`, `gap_traverse(resonance_idx)`, plus type-respecting compositions
- **Type-system**: each operation has input-types (Codon, Pair, Hexagram, Rotation, Trace, etc.) and output-types; programs are constructed and mutated respecting type-compatibility
- **Population size**: 100–1000 programs per generation (canonical default 256; sweepable)
- **Fitness**: `kernel_trace_coherence_score(program_output) + λ_shacl × shacl_satisfaction(program_output) + λ_verifier × downstream_verifier_coherence(program_output)` — composite fitness inheriting the same Anuttara-SHACL grounding as the process-reward signal
- **Mutation**: typed-rewrite operations over kernel-tick vocabulary (e.g., swap a Watson-Crick pair-evaluation for a Same-Quality pair-evaluation, swap one rotational-state for another, reorder the eight-element traversal under type-respect)
- **Crossover**: subtree exchange respecting type-compatibility (e.g., the rotational-state-computation subtree of program A can be exchanged with the rotational-state-computation subtree of program B if both produce the same output-type)
- **Selection**: tournament with elitism preserving the top-10 programs across generations
- **Evaluation cadence**: every federated round produces a new generation; new symbolic programs that survive 5 consecutive generations and pass M5-4 governance review become candidates for runtime-integration
- **Provenance**: every emitted program carries its lineage (parent-programs, mutation-operators applied, generation, fitness-curve)

## §4 — M5-2: Backend technical construction of the training pipeline (siva-)

### §4.1 What lives here

M5-2 Backend Studio is where the **technical-construction work** of the Mahāmāyā training pipeline happens — the process-reward RL infrastructure, the federated-learning aggregator, the symbolic-genetic synthesis engine, the GDS path-mining services, and the Anuttara-SHACL-verifier integration.

The work-set:

#### §4.1.A Process-reward RL infrastructure

The process-reward RL stack is implemented as a service that:

- Consumes **kernel-traces** emitted by the runtime kernel-tick (each tick produces an ordered-eight-element trace with intermediate values, gates passed, gaps encountered, matrix-types invoked, codon-quaternion states, rotational-indices, DET-projection inputs/outputs)
- Invokes the **hybrid reward-signal evaluation** (per §3.2) over each kernel-trace element, producing the composite per-element reward and the trace-level weighted-sum reward
- Maintains the **kernel-policy weights** (a small policy-network whose parameters drive kernel-tick element-decisions when alternative pathways are available — e.g., which dinucleotide pair to evaluate first, which rotational-state to prefer at ambiguity, which DET-projection variant to apply)
- Performs **per-round policy updates** using PPO-style (proximal policy optimisation) updates with the process-reward signal as the dense per-step reward (rather than only the terminal trace-reward), preserving the dense-step-signal that DeepSeek-R1 / OpenAI-o1 paradigm requires
- Versions every policy-update with provenance (round-id, training-set summary, validation-set metrics, SHACL-conformance metrics, Janus-bidirectionality metrics)
- Surfaces **policy-diff** reports for M5-3 visualisation and M5-4 review

#### §4.1.B Federated-learning aggregator

The federated-learning stack is implemented as:

- A **local-training client** running on user device (when user opts in) that performs FedAvg-style local SGD on the user's kernel-traces over the elapsed federation-window, producing a local model-update
- A **differential-privacy noise-injection module** that adds Gaussian noise (σ = 1.1 calibrated to ε = 1.0 per round per §3.3) to the local model-update before transmission, with gradient L2 norm clipping at 1.0
- A **central aggregator service** that collects DP-noised local updates from participating-users (minimum 100 per round per §3.3), computes the FedAvg weighted-by-participating-user-count, and produces the next-round kernel-policy weights
- A **privacy-budget accountant** tracking per-user cumulative ε with explicit consent gating beyond ε = 10 lifetime budget
- A **secure transmission layer** (TLS-mutual-auth + future homomorphic-encryption extension)
- An **opt-out handler** that immediately excludes a user's data from in-progress and future rounds upon opt-out signal
- **Audit-trail emission** for every round (round-id, participating-user-count, aggregate-norm-statistics, DP-budget-consumed, governance-approval-record)

#### §4.1.C Symbolic-genetic program-synthesis engine

The symbolic-genetic synthesis stack is implemented as:

- A **typed program-synthesis grammar** encoding the kernel-tick vocabulary per §3.4
- A **population manager** maintaining the current generation (size 256 default) plus the elitism-preserved top-10
- A **mutation operator suite** implementing the typed-rewrite operations (per §3.4)
- A **crossover operator suite** implementing type-compatible subtree-exchange (per §3.4)
- A **fitness evaluator** invoking the hybrid composite fitness (kernel-trace coherence + SHACL satisfaction + downstream verifier coherence)
- A **tournament selector** with configurable tournament-size (default k=5)
- A **generation scheduler** firing each federated round
- A **survivor tracker** flagging programs that survive 5+ consecutive generations as **candidate-for-runtime-integration**
- A **provenance emitter** logging full lineage for every candidate

#### §4.1.D GDS path-finding and pattern-mining services

The bimba-map GDS infrastructure (per `m4-prime-psychoid-cymatic-field-engine.md` §18) operates upon the Mahāmāyā calculation-pathway corpus:

- **Path-finding**: Dijkstra over the bimba-graph weighted by pathway-coherence-score (typical query: "given current (lens, mode), what's the highest-coherence pathway to a target (codon, rotation)?"); Yen's k-shortest-paths for top-k alternative-pathway discovery
- **Pattern-mining**: frequent-subgraph mining over the typed-path corpus (typical discovery: "this 5-node sub-pathway occurs in 73% of high-reward kernel-traces"); motif detection (typical discovery: "this 4-node motif recurs as a kernel-tick-element II prehension across many traces")
- **Learned-embeddings**: FastRP and GraphSAGE over the pathway-corpus to cluster pathways by structural similarity (typical use: "find pathways structurally similar to this exemplar")
- **Pattern-promotion**: discovered patterns that pass M5-4 review become **canonical-recognised pathway-templates** integrated into the runtime kernel-policy as preferred-pathway-priors

#### §4.1.E Anuttara-SHACL-verifier integration

The Anuttara-SHACL stack from the sibling Epii-on-Anuttara spec §4.1.C is integrated as the Tier-1 reward-signal evaluator (per §3.2.A above). The integration:

- Calls the n10s SHACL validation API (`n10s.validation.shacl.validate`) with kernel-trace element states as evaluation inputs
- Caches shape-evaluation results keyed by (shape-id, element-state-fingerprint) for performance
- Honours shape-version (when M0-Anuttara crystallises a new shape, the verifier picks it up on next round)
- Emits SHACL-conformance metrics per kernel-trace element for M5-3 visualisation

### §4.2 The pipeline-construction-not-substrate-training discipline

The two-register distinction from §2.2 is operationalised as:

- **All M5-2 work on the training pipeline is construction**: the RL infrastructure, federated aggregator, genetic engine, GDS services, SHACL integration are deliberate-built, versioned, governed, reviewed
- **The actual training (gradient descent on policy weights, FedAvg aggregation of model updates, genetic-population evolution) IS gradient/evolutionary in nature**: the kernel-policy weights update via PPO gradients; the federated aggregate updates via FedAvg averaging; the genetic population evolves via mutation/crossover/selection
- **The substrate (M3 matrix architecture) is constructed-canonical and stays canonical**: the three M3 matrices with their explicit quaternion-axis assignments at `m3.h:166-170`, the 472-state landscape arithmetic (`40×7 + 24×8 = 472`), the 4X-invariant (`pp + mm + mp + pm = 4X`), the 360-integral (`84 + 96 + 88 + 92 = 360`), the 56+8 Tarot cover — these are not learnable; they are the canonical foundation the learning operates over

This discipline is what allows Mahāmāyā to have a genuine training pipeline without compromising the construction-discipline that the Epi-Logos architecture requires.

## §6 — M5-4: Agentic mediation of the pipeline (siva-shakti)

### §6.1 What lives here

M5-4 Agentic Control Room carries the **agent-mediated orchestration** of the Mahāmāyā training-pipeline work:

- **Sophia** as the wisdom-curating agent: holds the long-arc of pipeline-evolution; tracks coherence across training rounds; surfaces tensions between proposed policy-updates and the canonical pipeline-articulation at M5-1; maintains the federation-round governance-thread continuity; reviews load-bearing pipeline-modifications before promotion
- **Anima** as the user-specific-pathway-oversight agent: calculation-pathways carry user-specific texture (each user's matheme-traversal is informed by their personal-quaternion at M4-4-4-4 and their lived (lens, mode) preferences); Anima provides the "does this pipeline-update respect the user-pathway diversity?" check, flags policy-shifts that would homogenise user-pathways inappropriately, ensures aesthetic-coherence of the matheme's traversal-style under updates
- **Pi** as the dispatch agent: handles training-run dispatch (when a federated round is due, when a new policy-update is ready to evaluate, when a new genetic-program emerges); routes proposals between M5-1 (articulation) and M5-2 (implementation); manages governance-approval signature collection; manages rollback-handle issuance
- **Aletheia** as the disclosure-tracking agent: tracks what becomes visible through each training round and each pattern-mining discovery — what was implicit in the calculation-pathway corpus and is now made explicit by a discovered pattern; tracks autoresearch-spine emergent findings
- **Logos Atelier specialist** (M5-5-mediated): invoked for pathway-archaeology when a discovered pattern needs canonical-provenance-tracing (per §7 below)

### §6.2 The agentic governance workflow

When a training round, federated aggregate, or genetic-program-candidate moves toward runtime-integration, it passes through an agentic governance workflow:

1. **Sophia review** — does the artifact cohere with canonical pipeline-articulation at M5-1? Are there tensions with prior crystallised pipeline-canon? Does the artifact's effect-on-runtime-pathways match the intent the pipeline-canon articulates?
2. **Anima review** — does the artifact respect user-pathway diversity? Are there pathway-homogenisation concerns? Does the artifact preserve aesthetic-coherence of the matheme's traversal-style?
3. **Pi translation-and-impact-check** — can the artifact be cleanly integrated into the runtime? Are there rollback-handle issues? Are there downstream-impact concerns (e.g., does this policy-update cascade into M4-personal-rendering through the (lens, mode) ↔ (codon, rotation) map)?
4. **Aletheia disclosure-check** — what does this artifact disclose about the calculation-pathway corpus? Is there a discovery here that ought to be promoted to M5-1 as a new canonical-articulation rather than only integrated at runtime?
5. **Atelier archaeology** (if invoked) — does any claimed canonical-pattern-correspondence hold under provenance-tracing?
6. **User final-validation** — for load-bearing pipeline-modifications (per §3.6), explicit user approval required

The workflow doesn't auto-promote. Every step produces an annotation; the user has visibility into all annotations; the user decides whether to proceed with integration.

### §6.3 The autoresearch-spine routing

Mahāmāyā's improvement-loop fires when:

- **Sufficient kernel-traces have accumulated for new RL training round** — cadence: monthly, with kernel-trace-count threshold (typical: 100,000+ traces per round)
- **Federated round threshold reached** — 100+ opted-in users per `M5-1` canonical federation-cadence
- **New calculation-pattern discovered via GDS pattern-mining** — pattern frequency above canonical-threshold (typical: pattern appearing in >5% of high-reward traces)
- **New symbolic program emerges from genetic programming** — program survives 5 consecutive generations and passes M5-4 governance review per §3.4
- **New SHACL shape from Anuttara work introduces new step-level verifier** — when the Epii-on-Anuttara pipeline crystallises a new canonical SHACL shape (per the sibling spec §4.1.C), that shape becomes available to the Tier-1 reward-signal evaluator

Epii (autoresearch spine) routes these triggers to the M5-2 Mahāmāyā backend pipeline; M5-4 governance gates promotion; M5-2 integration writes updated kernel-policy weights, new symbolic programs, new reward-model weights, or new pathway-template-priors into the runtime via the runtime-integration API (§4.3).

### §6.4 The cross-subsystem dispatch

When a Mahāmāyā pipeline-update has downstream implications for other subsystems, Pi dispatches the downstream tasks to the appropriate operational-capacity surfaces:

- New kernel-policy weights affect M4 personal-rendering (the (lens, mode) ↔ (codon, rotation) map drives M4 personal-quaternion alignment) → M4-personal-rendering rebuild dispatched
- New genetic-program in runtime pathway-bank affects M3' clock-wheel rendering (additional pathways visible on the wheel) → M3'-renderer profile-contract update dispatched
- New SHACL shape adopted at Tier-1 reward signal originates from Anuttara work → M5-1 cross-reference to the Anuttara canon-update is recorded
- New pattern discovered via GDS is candidate for M5-1 canonical-articulation → review-and-promotion workflow dispatched

These are coordinated through Pi's task-dispatch capability with provenance tracked back to the originating Mahāmāyā pipeline event.

### §6.5 Governance asymmetries by tier

Different pipeline-tiers carry different governance discipline:

- **Tier-1 SHACL updates** (Anuttara-sourced) — inherit Anuttara's governance discipline; M5-4 review-only confirms integration-compatibility
- **Tier-2 heuristic updates** — Sophia review + Pi translation-check; user-final-validation only for load-bearing heuristic-changes
- **Tier-3 critic-model retraining** — full governance workflow (Sophia + Anima + Pi + Aletheia); user-final-validation for any critic-architecture change
- **Federated-round aggregate adoption** — Sophia + Anima + Pi review (Anima review is meaningful here because federated aggregates touch user-pathway diversity); user-final-validation for federation-policy-canon changes
- **Genetic-program runtime-integration** — full governance + Atelier archaeology (per §7); user-final-validation for any program affecting load-bearing kernel-tick elements

The asymmetry is intentional: the discipline-weight scales with the canon-touching-depth of the change.

## §10 — Construction discipline summary

The work this spec covers is **pipeline-construction**, not substrate-training. Concrete implications:

- Every pipeline-component (RL infrastructure, federated aggregator, genetic engine, GDS services, SHACL integration) is a deliberate-built artefact under explicit governance
- The actual training (PPO gradients, FedAvg aggregation, genetic-evolution) IS gradient/evolutionary — but it runs *within* a constructed pipeline whose configuration, scheduling, and review are governed
- The substrate (M3 matrix architecture, 472-state landscape arithmetic, 4X-invariant, 56+8 Tarot cover, three M3 matrices with i/j/k quaternion-axis assignments) stays canonical and is not learned-from-scratch
- Federated-learning privacy is never compromised — DP-ε budget honoured, no-data-leaves-device invariant honoured, opt-out immediately honoured
- Sophia governs federated rounds; Anima oversight is meaningful (calculation-pathways carry user-specific texture); Pi dispatches training runs; Aletheia tracks what new patterns disclose
- Step-level verifier sensitivity is treated structurally: the verifier shapes what gets rewarded; mis-tuned verifier propagates miscoherence; verifier-design is a governed canon-evolution act at M5-1
- Cross-reference with the Anuttara sibling spec where the SHACL-shape-based Tier-1 reward-signal integration happens — Anuttara crystallises SHACL shapes; Mahāmāyā pipeline consumes them
- ML paradigms are not invented beyond the canonical position-3 set (process-reward RL + federated + symbolic-genetic synthesis)
- Janus aspect at M3 is treated as structural training-constraint, not UI decoration — calculation pathways are bidirectional in temporal-orientation; the RL training preserves this bidirectionality via the `β_janus` modifier in the reward-signal composition
- All pipeline-state is reversible via rollback-handles; auto-rollback on runtime-metric degradation is the safety-net for unanticipated effects
- Load-bearing pipeline-modifications (reward-signal-weighting changes, federation-policy changes, critic-architecture changes, runtime-integration of programs touching load-bearing kernel-tick elements) require explicit user final-validation
- The audit-trail is complete: every kernel-trace traces from initial-emission → reward-evaluation → round-aggregation → governance-approval → runtime-integration → post-integration-metrics

This is the structurally-correct discipline for Mahāmāyā's relationship to its own pipeline-improvement at the M5 operational-capacity surface.
