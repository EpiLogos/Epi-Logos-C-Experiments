# Cycle 2 M' First Coverage Overview

This cycle is not a fresh S-stack build. The S/S' substrate is treated as **landed or partially-landed consumed infrastructure** unless a specific missing contract, broken runtime path, or governance gap is named explicitly. The primary object of development is the **M' system**: the actual Electron/Theia shell, `/body` surfaces, `0/1` parent faces, `/` operator membrane, six M extensions, two integrated plugins, Agentic Control Room, and the review/autoresearch/pedagogy surfaces they expose.

## Surface Separation Invariant

Cycle 2 must preserve the separation between the `0/1` parent surface and the `4+2` depth surface.

- Shell `0` is the lean structural/cosmic parent face over the integrated `1-2-3` signal.
- Shell `1` is the lean personal/world-return parent face over the integrated `4-5-0` signal. The flow-writing surface belongs here as the universal lived input for Nara, Epii entry, review signals, and relevant M0 ground-data.
- The `4+2` layer is the full-depth subsystem/workbench surface: M0-M5 pages, deep Nara, Epii IDE, Agentic Control Room, integrated plugin depth, recognition workbench, and canon/review/promotion workflows.
- `/` is the OmniPanel operator membrane: backend/settings, sessions, direct Pi/agent chat, gateway state, logs, readiness, tool traces, model/config, and development transparency. It must not become the primary lived UX or displace shell `1` flow.
