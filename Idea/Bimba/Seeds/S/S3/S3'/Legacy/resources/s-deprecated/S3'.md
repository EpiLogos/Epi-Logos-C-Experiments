### Philosophical Sources

**Khora** (Plato's Timaeus):
- The "receptacle" or "nurse" of becoming
- Provides the space/ground where all forms come into being
- Environment without characteristics - pure potential for manifestation

**Hen** (Plotinus's Enneads):
- The One - unity from which all emanate and to which all return
- Pre-established harmony enabling coordination without direct coupling
- The principle that binds subsystems together

**Pleroma** (Gnostic):
- The fullness or totality of divine powers
- Complete collection of all skills and capabilities
- Abundance of functional potential

**Chronos/Kairos** (Greek):
- Chronos: Sequential/quantitative time (clock time, duration)
- Kairos: Qualitative/opportune time (the right moment)
- Together: Temporal coordination and timing

**Anima** (Aristotle):
- The animating principle that brings forms to life
- Soul as the actuality of a natural body
- Agent as living entity executing with purpose

**Aletheia** (Heidegger):
- Truth as unconcealment or revelation
- Uncovering what IS through reflection
- Synthesis that reveals the system to itself
