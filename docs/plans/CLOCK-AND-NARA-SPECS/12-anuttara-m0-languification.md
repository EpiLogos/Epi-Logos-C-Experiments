## VIII. Open Questions / Future Work

**`M0_ARCHETYPAL_NAME_TABLE[72]` data population.**
Requires: (1) mapping the 72 Shem HaMephorash names to the 72 half-decan positions (astrological tradition provides this; primary sources: Liber 777, Golden Dawn material, Agrippa), (2) cross-referencing the 99 Quranic names into 72 grouping clusters, (3) gematria computation per name. This is dataset work, phase 2+. The Python LUT-builder for `CLOCK_DEGREE_LUT` is the natural home for this additional pass.

**99-names-to-72-fold mapping.**
99 ÷ 72 ≈ 1.375. Some half-decan positions will have 1 Quranic name, some will have 2. Proposed grouping algorithm: semantic/phonetic proximity by traditional Quranic commentary clusters, then distribute into 72 slots by thematic affinity with the Shem name at the same position. This avoids purely mechanical distribution and preserves the meaning density M0-4 is designed to carry.

**M0-5 `recognized` event source.**
Who writes `oracle_payload.recognized = true`? Current candidates:
- **Explicit TUI acknowledgement** — user presses a key in the portal to confirm integration of the reading. This is the correct UX model philosophically (pratyabhijñā is an event, not a timer).
- **4-hour decay heuristic** — matching the oracle cycle decay established elsewhere. This is a fallback, not the primary path.
Resolution: explicit acknowledgement is the canonical path; 4h decay is the fallback. This is a UX spec decision, deferred to portal implementation.

**27 Nakshatra full mapping.**
The 27 Hindu lunar mansions (27 × 13.33° = 360°; 27 × 8/3 = 72) provide a third independent path into the M0-4 grid alongside Shem and Quranic names. Each nakshatra spans approximately 2.67 half-decans (72/27 = 2.666…). This non-integer relationship is meaningful: it is the same irrational bridge as 9:8 in the epogdoon — a ratio that cannot fully resolve into the grid is the arithmetic signature of a living harmonic, not a dead divisor. The nakshatra layer belongs in `M0_Archetypal_Name.nakshatra_cluster` (already reserved in the struct) but its population is phase 3+.

**M0-0 through M0-3 computational correlates.**
This spec focuses on M0-4 and M0-5 as the "holographic syntax dimensions" because these are the positions with direct computational implications (lookup table, recognized flag). M0-0 through M0-3 also have computational correlates (the void ground of the hash, the spanda pulse of tick12, the prapañca of the 16 lenses). A future spec may make these explicit. For now: they are architecturally present in the `CosmicClockPlugin` as the substrate of all clock rendering, not as separately callable modules.
