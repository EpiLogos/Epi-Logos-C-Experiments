## I. The 12-Lens System: Day and Night

The L-coordinate family is the **Lens** dimension of the six-family coordinate space. It manifests
the #0-#5 archetypes through the domain of **epistemic modes** — the distinct ways consciousness
apprehends reality. The 12 lenses form two complementary hexads:

- **Day lenses (L0-L5):** Torus topology — cyclical, explicate, outward-facing
- **Night lenses (L0'-L5'):** Klein topology — self-intersecting, implicate, inward-folding

## V. The Four Relational Laws

These laws hold universally across the Klein V4 structure:

| Law | Formula | Description |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| **Day complement** | X + Y = 5 | Any two day lenses that sum to 5 are Klein complements (L0+L5=5, L1+L4=5, L2+L3=5) |
| **Night complement** | X' + Y' = 5 | Same law applies in the night domain (L0'+L5'=5, L1'+L4'=5, L2'+L3'=5) |
| **Day-Night doubling** | X + X' = 2x | A lens and its night inversion share the same QL position; together they double the archetype |
| **Cross integration** | Y + Y' = 2y | The complement pair in both modes yields full cross-modal synthesis |
