# Positions and Coherence Walk

## The Practical Runtime Flow

The practical flow is `P1 → P2 → P3 → P4 → P5 → P0`.

This does not deny `P0` as source. It means that practical execution usually enters manifestation first and returns to source after coherence and synthesis have been established. `P0` as source-condition underlies the whole walk. `P0` as renewed ground is what the walk returns to after completion.

## Functional Reading of Each Position

### P1: What Is Actually There

`P1` is the position of manifestation and presence. It establishes the issue as it concretely shows up — not as the problem you expected to find, not as the problem that would be easier to solve, but as the problem that is actually there.

The question is: what would count as misdescribing this? Get that wrong and every subsequent position inherits the error.

Good work at `P1`: the issue is present without distortion. The inquiry is standing in front of what it actually needs to examine.

Poor work at `P1`: the inquiry is already framing the issue toward a preferred conclusion, or it is too vague to drive the subsequent positions.

### P2: Tension, Dynamis, Contradiction

`P2` is the position of movement, pressure, and dynamis — the energetic core of the inquiry. Whatever is blocked, unstable, split, or in motion lives here. Whatever gives the inquiry its necessity rather than its interest.

A field without `P2` is a field without energy. The inquiry may look clean at `P1` but if there is no real tension, there is nothing driving it toward `P3` or beyond.

Good work at `P2`: the specific contradiction or resistance is named. The field is not just interesting — it is under real pressure.

Poor work at `P2`: tension is noted but bypassed. "There are tensions" is not `P2` — the tension has to be inhabited.

### P3: Pattern, Process, Form

`P3` is the position of structure — the shape that the tension of `P2` takes when it is followed carefully enough. This is where process becomes visible as process, where recurrence becomes recognizable as recurrence, where the form that organizes the tension can be named.

`P3` is not the solution. It is the structural recognition that makes the solution possible. Getting `P3` right means the shape of the movement becomes available, not just its direction.

Good work at `P3`: a real pattern is identified — something that would allow prediction, recognition, or intervention.

Poor work at `P3`: the pattern is hand-wavy, or `P3` is being used to rush toward synthesis before the form is genuinely visible.

### P4: Contextual Fold

`P4` is the position where the inquiry is re-situated inside its larger field. Context here is not just background — it changes what the prior positions mean. A `P4` that merely adds context without recontextualizing is not doing `P4` work.

When `P4` is doing real work — when the fold actually changes what `P1–P3` showed — the topology may need to escalate from `torus` to `lemniscatic`.

Good work at `P4`: the meaning of the inquiry shifts. Something that looked like one thing at `P1–P3` now shows itself differently.

Poor work at `P4`: context is added but nothing changes. The run just becomes bigger rather than deeper.

### P5: Synthesis

`P5` opens only when `P1–P4` genuinely cohere. This is not an aspirational claim — it is a structural one. Synthesis that skips the coherence walk is not synthesis. It is a conclusion with synthesis's vocabulary.

When `P5` genuinely opens, what becomes available is a higher-order relation that was not accessible before the walk. Not just a summary, but something that could not have been stated at `P1`.

The temptation to rush `P5` is real, especially when the field starts to look coherent. The discipline is to check: has `P2` been inhabited? Has `P3` named a real pattern? Has `P4` actually changed something?

### P0: Renewed Ground

`P0` after the walk is not the same as `P0` before it. What returns is not the original ground unchanged — it is ground that has received the result of the walk and become something new because of it.

The question at `P0` is: what is now foundational? What new question or orientation is possible because of this return? What changes in the next cycle?

## Night Positions: The Analytic Face

The Night movement runs `P0' → P1' → P2' → P3' → P4' → P5'`.

Night is not merely generic critique or error-checking. It is the analytic face of the same surface — the same genus-1 topology traversed in its complementary orientation. Day and Night are not two different systems. They are the two directions through which the same structure is read. The prime positions (P') exist because the topology generates them: a closed orientable surface traversed in both orientations produces exactly 4g + 2 positions per pass, and two passes give the full twelve.

This matters structurally: the primes are not optional. They are what the surface requires.

- `P0'`: radical question — what is not being asked?
- `P1'`: hidden residue or concealed form — what is present but not named?
- `P2'`: obstruction or challenge — what is resisting?
- `P3'`: latent pattern — what structure is operating but not yet visible?
- `P4'`: missed context or discovery — what context changes everything?
- `P5'`: crystallized insight — what does this yield when pushed through?

If a Day run looks coherent too quickly, run Night against it. The Night positions are specifically designed to find what premature closure hides. A traversal that uses only Day is a traversal of half the surface.

## The Walk as Discipline

The walk is not a template to fill. It is a discipline for earning coherence rather than performing it. Every position has a genuine question it is asking, and that question can be answered genuinely or avoided. The discipline is to tell the difference.
