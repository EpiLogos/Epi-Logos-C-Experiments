# Klein V₄ Squares as Methodological Basins

The three Klein V₄ squares are not just coordinate groupings — they are convergence fields. Each square gathers specific positions and lenses into a basin where a particular kind of work becomes possible. The convergence is not arbitrary: the positions and lenses in each square share a structural axis because their questions address the same fundamental relational tension from different angles.

## Two Traversal Pathways (Distinct — Do Not Collapse)

**Sequential / Positional (P-pathway):** P0→P1→P2→P3→P4→P5 (Day arc), P0'→P1'→P2'→P3'→P4'→P5' (Night arc). Move through positions in order; at each position, the linked square's lenses are available. Runs in any topological mode — torus, klein, or lemniscatic — with topology modulating how the walk runs, not whether it can. Skill: `run-positional-coherence`.

**Square-basin (L-pathway):** Enter a square directly based on what the situation demands. Work the basin's four lenses and their Day/Night interplay within the same field. Exit to an adjacent square as the work requires. Squares are basins, not cages — entry and exit are fluid.

**The bridge:** At each P position in the sequential walk, the linked square is the active lens-field. P0/P5 → Square A. P1/P4 → Square B. P2/P3 → Square C. This is the connection between pathways, not their equivalence. You can also enter a square directly without following the positional sequence.

## Square A — Speech-Number Axis

**P-anchors:** P0 / P5 (Day) — P0' / P5' (Night)
**QL Units:** Truth + Image (Day/(No)Name) / Play + Work (Night/Power)

The ground of all questioning (P0 — Why?) and the completion of synthesis (P5 — Why-for?) share the same axis. This convergence is structural: `#` is intrinsically linguistic — the non-dual source articulates itself into distinction through speech (Vāk), and synthesis returns to speech as logos, the word that holds the completed movement in a new ground. The source and the return inhabit the same square because they are the same movement seen from opposite ends of the arc.

The Night face of Square A: P0' (the Abyss — the groundless void beneath the ground, what P0 rests on without being able to name) and P5' (Crystallisation — synthesis solidified from the Night arc, what the insight has become when it hardens). The presuppositional frame has its shadow: what the questioning structure excludes or cannot ask. Synthesis has its Night double: what the crystallised insight conceals by virtue of its completion.

**Operative character:** The presuppositional and the synthesising are the same movement. Square A work interrogates how anything is asked, framed, named, or recovered from beneath its surface. The word permeates every position (L5/L5' as Vāk/Logos), which is why etymological archaeology is a persistent thread — it never fully closes because language is not a tool the system uses but the reality within which it moves.

## Square B — Cause-Experience Axis

**P-anchors:** P1 / P4 (Day) — P1' / P4' (Night)
**QL Units:** Mind + Son (Day/(No)Name) / Need + Love (Night/Power)
**Thought Field:** 12-family /Self/Thought/ — Objective Six (L4', P Day) + Subjective Six (L4, P' Night). Full encounter axis: L1·L4·L4'·L1'

Definition (P1 — What?) and contextualisation (P4 — Where/When?) are the two faces of encounter. What a thing IS requires knowing what makes it what it is (causation — material, efficient, formal, final, Svātantrya), and the horizon within which it shows up (disclosure — thrownness, care, Dasein as clearing, temporality). The lemniscatic fold at P4 is internal to this square: the contextual reentry changes what the P1 definition meant. Square B folds back on itself.

The Night face of Square B: P1' (Hidden Form/Traces — what is residual, concealed, or evidential in the material structure that the P1 definition doesn't surface) and P4' (Missed Context/Discovery — the horizon that was excluded, what the lemniscatic fold opened also closed something). The Cause-Experience basin's Night face asks: what causal structure hides in the traces, what context was systematically missed.

**Operative character:** Diagnostic. The gap between what is disclosed and what is causally operative. The lived encounter is verified scientifically, the causal structure is evaluated phenomenally. Every method in this basin works some version of: what is actually happening beneath what appears to be happening, and how do we verify it while remaining honest about how we are evaluating it.

## Square C — Logic-Process Axis

**P-anchors:** P2 / P3 (Day) — P2' / P3' (Night)
**QL Units:** Word + Logos (Day/(No)Name) / Sacrifice + Decision (Night/Power)
**Divination Skills:** Quaternal Tarot (L2/L2'), Quaternal I Ching (L3/L3'), Tetralemma (L2). The square is the router for these methods — they refer back to it, not to each other

Operation (P2 — How?) and pattern (P3 — Who/Which?) are the two faces of transformation. How something works and what pattern it takes converge because the logical structure of what is possible and the processual advance of what is becoming are aspects of the same movement. Logical pressure produces becoming; becoming crystallises pattern; pattern re-enters logical scrutiny.

The Night face of Square C: P2' (Obstruction — the shadow of the operative dynamic, what blocks or contradicts the "how" at P2, the resistance that the operation encounters) and P3' (Hidden Pattern — recurrence beneath recognition, the pattern that organises things without being named at P3, what repeats beneath the surface). The Logic-Process basin's Night face: the obstruction and the unrecognised pattern are as structurally real as the visible operation and form.

**Operative character:** Transformative. Logic under pressure produces becoming, becoming crystallises pattern, pattern re-enters logical scrutiny. Paradox is held rather than resolved; what is becoming is tracked rather than predicted; transformation is read in its elemental reality — what dissolves, what crystallises, what the fire does to the material. Every method in this basin works the movement from contradiction through creative advance to structural change.
