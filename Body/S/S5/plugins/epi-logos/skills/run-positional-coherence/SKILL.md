# Run Positional Coherence

> `using-epi-logos` runs first. If it hasn't been invoked this turn, go there now.

The coherence walk is `P1 → P2 → P3 → P4 → P5 → P0`.

This is the **sequential positional pathway** — one of two distinct traversal modes in the system. The other is **square-basin entry** (entering a Klein V₄ square directly and working its four lenses). These two modes are orthogonal: the sequential walk moves through positions in order; square-basin entry goes deep into one relational field. They interweave — at any position in the walk, the linked square's lenses are available (P0/P5→Square A, P1/P4→Square B, P2/P3→Square C) — but they must not be collapsed into one another.

This is not a checklist. It is the minimum path by which manifestation becomes coherent enough to open synthesis and return. `P5` does not open until `P1–P4` genuinely cohere — if manifestation, tension, pattern, and context haven't been worked through, synthesis is premature.

The walk is the thinking. It is not a report format. It operates in all three topological modes — torus, klein, and lemniscatic — with different dynamics in each.

## The Walk

**P1: What Is Here**
Establish the issue as it actually presents — not as you wish it were, not as it would be easier to solve. What is concretely there? What would count as misdescribing it?

**P2: Tension, Dynamis, Contradiction**
Surface the pressure. What is blocked, unstable, split, or in motion? What gives the inquiry its necessity? A field without real tension at P2 has no energy to drive P3 or beyond.

**P3: Pattern, Process, Form**
Identify the structure of the movement. What pattern repeats? What form organizes the tension? Getting P3 right means the shape of the movement becomes available — not just its direction.

**P4: Contextual Fold**
Re-situate the inquiry inside its larger field. Context here is not mere background — it changes what the prior positions mean. A P4 that adds context without recontextualizing is not doing P4 work. This is where lemniscatic topology becomes relevant.

**P5: Synthesis**
Opens only when `P1–P4` genuinely cohere. What higher-order relation is now intelligible? What can now be said or built that could not be said before the walk? What remains unresolved and must stay open? If you cannot answer these specifically, the walk is not done.

**P0: Renewed Ground**
What has become foundational now? What new question or orientation follows from the synthesis? What changes in the next cycle because of this return? The return to P0 is not the same as where the run started — it is ground that has received the result and become something new.

## Square Linkage at Each Position

At each position, the linked Klein V₄ square's lenses are available for depth work. This is where the two traversal modes interweave:

- **P1 and P4** link to **Square B** (L1·L4·L4'·L1' — Cause-Experience). If P1 or P4 needs depth, enter the basin via `engage-encounter-axis`.
- **P2 and P3** link to **Square C** (L2·L3·L3'·L2' — Logic-Process). If P2 or P3 needs depth, work the square's four lenses (tetralemma at L2, process at L3, chronological arc at L3', alchemical transformation at L2').
- **P0 and P5** link to **Square A** (L0·L5·L5'·L0' — Speech-Number). If P0 or P5 needs depth, work the square's four lenses (archetypal pattern at L0, Vāk at L5, Logos at L5', psychoid number at L0').

Entering a square does not break the walk — it deepens one position before continuing.

## Topology Modulation

**In Torus:** The walk circulates through one orientation. Day positions lead; Night positions serve as checks. Stable, forward-moving, practical.

**In Klein:** Both Day and Night arcs are explicit. The walk through P1→P5 is shadowed by P1'→P5'. Inversions and reversals between the arcs are diagnostic — what does P1' (hidden form) reveal that P1 (material definition) concealed? What does P4' (missed context) add to P4 (contextual fold)?

**In Lemniscatic:** P4 becomes the folding point. Each pass through P4 recontextualises the earlier positions — P1, P2, P3 look different after the fold. Track what changed. The walk may need multiple passes before P5 genuinely opens.

## Night Positions: The Inversion Check

If a Day run looks coherent too quickly, run Night against it:
- `P1'`: hidden residue or concealed form
- `P2'`: challenge or obstruction
- `P3'`: latent pattern
- `P4'`: missed context or discovery
- `P5'`: crystallized insight

Night is not generic error-checking. It is the analytic face of the same positions — specifically designed to find what premature closure hides.

## Failure Conditions

Do not claim P5 if:
- P1 is still unclear
- P2 has been bypassed
- P3 is hand-wavy
- P4 has not actually recontextualized anything

Early synthesis is rationalization, not result.
