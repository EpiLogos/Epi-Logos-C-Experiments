# Choose Topological Mode

> `using-epi-logos` runs first. If it hasn't been invoked this turn, go there now.

Topological mode is not decoration. It determines what kind of movement is available, what contradictions can be held, and what synthesis is actually possible. Determine it internally and act from it. Do not narrate the selection to the user.

This orientation is available throughout a run — at the start, at the beginning of any child skill, or mid-run when the inquiry reveals its real shape. It is not a one-time pre-run lock. Topology can escalate (torus → klein → lemniscatic) or simplify as the field demands.

## The Rule

Pick the least complex topology that still tells the truth.

- Do not default to `torus` because it feels simpler.
- Escalate to `klein` when one-sided circulation is hiding the issue.
- Escalate to `lemniscatic` when recursive context is the actual work.

`Möbius` underlies the whole field as inversion and return — it is not typically selected as a standalone run mode, but its logic is always present.

## Modes

### Torus

When the inquiry can circulate coherently through one foregrounded aspect without collapse.

Signals: tractable without heavy inversion; one side of the encounter axis can lead while the other serves as check; practical, exploratory, or explanatory without deep frame conflict.

Runtime effect: stable circulation, one aspect of `L4/L4'` foregrounded.

### Klein

When the inquiry must hold inside/outside, subjective/objective, or explicit/implicit together.

Signals: contradiction persists; the problem changes depending on who names it; each side exposes the other's blind spot; diagnosis is central.

Runtime effect: doubled encounter, inversion without collapse — better for critique, conflict, and paradox mapping.

### Lemniscatic

When the inquiry must generate its own higher-order opening through recursive contextual folding.

Signals: `P4` is doing real recontextualizing work; nested reframings change the nature of earlier positions; neither torus nor klein yields synthesis alone.

Runtime effect: recursive folding through context, transcendence generated internally rather than imposed.

## Failure Modes

- `torus` used while contradiction is still active — flattens what needs to stay paradoxical
- `klein` used to sound profound when stable circulation would do
- `lemniscatic` used to avoid making concrete progress

## Mid-Run Revision

Topology can be revised when the inquiry shows its real shape. If torus starts and contradiction emerges that it cannot hold, escalate to klein. If P4 begins doing recursive work that neither can accommodate, escalate to lemniscatic. What is not acceptable is leaving topology implicit — it is always operating.
