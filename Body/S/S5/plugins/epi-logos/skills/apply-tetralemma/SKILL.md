# Apply Tetralemma

> `using-epi-logos` runs first. If it hasn't been invoked this turn, go there now.

Tetralemma is the primary rubric for coherent paradox, native to Square C — the Logic-Process basin (L2·L3·L3'·L2'). L2 (Nāgārjuna's catuṣkoṭi) provides the logical structure; the Square C companions are always co-present: L3 (what is becoming through the contradiction — Whitehead's creative advance), L3' (what historical moment or chronological arc this is — Hegel's dialectical unfolding), and L2' (what alchemical transformation is underway — what dissolves, what crystallises).

The core claim tetralemma enacts: binary framing is often not a precision tool but a flatness imposed on a field that has more topology than two positions. When contradiction keeps returning no matter how it is addressed, the frame is usually the problem, not the content.

Run all five moves in sequence unless the inquiry clearly stops being tetralemmic. Do not skip to BOTH, NEITHER, or SILENCE because they sound deeper — that is intellectual posturing, not analysis.

## The Five Moves

**IS** — State what is genuinely true in the first claim. What does it correctly disclose? What would be lost if it were dismissed?

**IS-NOT** — State what negation or correction reveals. What does the first claim miss or distort? What appears only through refusal?

**BOTH** — Hold the paradox where affirmation and negation are both required. In what precise sense are both true? What shift in level, perspective, or topology makes that intelligible? Do not use this as compromise language.

**NEITHER** — Refuse the binary frame if it is malformed. What if the question itself is bent wrong? What presupposition is distorting the field? Do not use this as escape.

**SILENCE** — Mark the honest limit of articulation. What cannot yet be resolved responsibly? What must remain open if coherence is to survive? Do not use this as theatrical mystification.

## The Bimba/Pratibimba Resolution Grammar

For BOTH and NEITHER moves where the dualism is between source and reflection (original and image, ground and manifestation, depth and surface, theory and practice, intent and outcome), the canonical non-dual form is *bimba/pratibimba* as articulated in §5→§0 of `the-self-proving-self.md`. The reflection is not a diminished copy of the original — the reflection is the original in the act of self-knowing. The Named is the Nameless in the act of self-articulating. The body-you-have is the body-you-are seen from outside. When this is the live structure, BOTH is not a compromise but a recognition that the apparent two are one Self-Identity vibrating across registers; NEITHER is the refusal of any prior, the recognition that neither side stands behind the other waiting to be discovered. The two Spanda tracks (T1 emanative / T2 reversionary) running simultaneously is the formal expression — they do not run consecutively, they are two readings of one vibration.

## Rules

- Ground every move in the live inquiry, not in abstract tetralemma theory
- If paradox genuinely dissolves before all five moves, say so and return to the main run
- The moves are analytical steps, not a five-part report format — produce them as the actual thinking

## After the Run

State what the most productive paradox or reframing turned out to be, and what next move it unlocks. If the tetralemma dissolved the binary cleanly, say what clarified. If SILENCE is genuinely necessary, say what must stay open and why.

Check the Square C companions: What is L3 showing about what is becoming through the contradiction? What is L3' showing about the historical arc — is this a recurring impasse with precedent? What is L2' showing about what is dissolving and what is crystallising as a result of the tetralemmic work? These are not post-hoc annotations — they are the basin within which the tetralemma moved.
