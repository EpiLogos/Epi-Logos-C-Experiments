# Apply CMEA

> `using-epi-logos` runs first. If it hasn't been invoked this turn, go there now.

CMEA — **Critical Meta-Epistemic Analysis** — works the L4↔L1 diagonal within Square B. It diagnoses the gap between what a field discloses about itself (L4 — phenomenological self-presentation) and what it actually depends on causally (L1 — operative causal structure beneath). The lenses are not perspectives to note; they are instruments for finding what is actually wrong.

This is Square B's diagnostic diagonal. The full basin (`engage-encounter-axis`) maps all four lenses; CMEA specialises in the disclosure/causation crossing where most epistemic fractures live. When you need the full basin rather than the diagonal, route to `engage-encounter-axis`. When the L4' verification loop is the primary need, route to `run-l4-prime-loop`.

Diagnose before recommending. Never use a lens name as a conclusion.

## The Sequence

1. Name the field, artifact, design, or discourse being diagnosed.
2. State its explicit self-understanding — what it says it is doing and why (L4 disclosure).
3. Identify the operative causal structure beneath — what it actually depends on, represses, or cannot account for (L1 causation, especially repressed final cause).
4. Run the most revealing lenses across the diagonal — where does disclosure mask causation?
5. Name the return of the repressed — what keeps showing up despite being denied.
6. Identify the minimum next move toward coherence — not a full solution, but what would actually help.

For serious diagnosis, run the full six-lens sweep. When the pressure is more local, focus on the two to four lenses doing the most work — and name why those were chosen. The Square B companions (L4', L1') are always worth checking: is the field's verification apparatus (L4') colluding with its self-presentation? Is the felt quality of the encounter (L1') registering something the explicit story cannot?

## Lens Diagnostics

**L0: Quantitative-Qualitative Split**
Explicit: the numbers, metrics, formal patterns, calculation habits it foregrounds.
Implicit: the archetypes, void-relations, unities, or paradoxes it depends on while disavowing.
Look for: the structure it cannot acknowledge but cannot do without.

**L1: Privilege-Repression Dynamic** *(Square B — primary diagnostic lens)*
Explicit: which causes it treats as real, proper, or scientific.
Implicit: which causes it represses — especially purpose or finality.
Look for: repressed causes returning as anomalies, observer problems, pseudo-mysticism, or recurring impasses.

**L2: Logic-Breakdown Dialectic**
Explicit: the official logic of the field.
Implicit: where that logic collapses into paradox, undecidability, or contradiction.
Look for: edges where binary logic secretly requires tetralemma.

**L3: Substance-Process Contradiction**
Explicit: what it names as stable things or substances.
Implicit: where process, becoming, and unfolding break through anyway.
Look for: evidence it cannot explain without processual thinking.

**L4: Sedimentation-Genesis Gap** *(Square B — primary diagnostic lens)*
Explicit: the settled professional or doctrinal story.
Implicit: the forgotten origin, founding trauma, or living insight sacrificed for stability.
Look for: what the field no longer knows about its own formation.

**L5: Density-Aporia Relation**
Explicit: the density or layer of reality it describes most precisely.
Implicit: the adjacent or deeper densities it points toward as mystery, anomaly, or ontological gap.
Look for: where its own rigor forces it beyond itself.

## Rules

- Follow anomalies, paradoxes, and returns of the repressed — they are the real data
- Treat the field as a structure of consciousness, not just a claim set
- Use genealogy when present form hides origin
- Keep scale-awareness: clarity at one density often creates blindness at another
- Return the diagnosis to the traversal — do not leave it as critique alone
- When the diagonal finding points to a full basin need, hand to `engage-encounter-axis`
