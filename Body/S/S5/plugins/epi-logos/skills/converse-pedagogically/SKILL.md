| Position | Question | Semantic | L-Link (Day ↔ Night) |

The Night positions (P0'–P5') carry the same archetypal numbers in inverted orientation — Abyss, Traces, Obstruction, Hidden Pattern, Missed Context, Crystallization — and are in tow whenever the Day positions are named. Neither arc is primary. A traversal of Day alone covers half the surface.

**These positions are NOT a phenomenological sequence.** P1 is "What?" because L1 is Aristotle's four causes — the logic of material definition. P2 is "How?" because L2 is Nāgārjuna's catuṣkoṭi — the logic of how processes move. P3 is "Who/Which?" because L3 is Whitehead's process philosophy. P4 is "Where/When?" because L4 is Heidegger's phenomenology. Do not substitute a different mapping.

## Reading the Entry

**For a direct teaching request** — "teach me," "explain this," "what is this," any equivalent — do not interrogate. Give a scope orientation: what the system is, why it exists, what its main parts are and how they connect. Three to four paragraphs of real prose. Then name the most alive thread and follow it.

A scope orientation explains WHY each part exists, not what it is called. The learner comes away with a felt sense of what this thing is for. A good one covers:
- What "Epi-Logos" means: ἐπί (upon/beyond) + λόγος — second-order logos; a logic that takes its own conditions as object
- Why it exists: consciousness is the paradigm self-referential system; binary logic collapses under self-reference; topology provides the structure that holds it
- QL and MEF: structural rhythm of inquiry (six positions) and reflective geometry (twelve lenses — six Day plus six Night primes, each a full entity)
- The 6→12 move: torus (one orientation, six positions) → Klein (both orientations, twelve) — traversal shape IS positional shape

**For requests with a specific pressure** — a contradiction, a question about how something works — find the pressure first, start there. If paradox: start with why binary logic can't hold self-referential contradiction, then tetralemma (MEF L2). If malformed question: start with QL as question-shaping. If architectural: start with consciousness and 4g+2.
